 REVIEWS 
Wedding photography for the adventure seekers, modern day explorers, dreamers, doers, risk takers.
No two persons are the same; therefore, no two wedding shoots should be the same. We’re all about working hard to create something new—something truly you.
While your wedding day is bound to be filled with lots of activity and lots of emotion, we don’t want the photography to be another worry on your to-do list. We want you to be present, to let down your guard and enjoy your day. We’re not there to tell our story; we’re there to tell yours! So relax! We’ve got this covered!
WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT 
No. 01
Unobtrusive
We are there to listen and tell your story
We like to think of ourselves as photo ninjas—certified organic, level 10, non-gmo, ninjas. We move quickly throughout each moment, popping in and out, capturing more memories than you ever thought possible. 
No. 02
Your well being is important
We like to care for people
We think your marriage is more important than your wedding. That’s why we seek to build a relationship starting at your engagement. We want to laugh, cry, celebrate, and encourage all throughout the moments we will share together.
No. 03
Fast
Like devouring a Chipotle Burrito Fast!
No one likes waiting months and months for their wedding photos. We try our best to get your photos back to you within four weeks of your wedding day.

FAQ
Who are the humans behind of Magnified Joy? 
Magnified Joy is a husband and wife photographic team operated by En Tao + Maria Ko.
We take a very personal approach to our business (in some sense it&#8217;s our first baby), we also know that your wedding day is very personal to you as well!
Each step of the process, from the initial connection meeting, the communications leading up, your adventure session, your wedding day, editing your photos, delivering your photos, designing your album, and everywhere in between, it is all done by us! Sometimes we even hand deliver photos just so we can see your beautiful faces again! 
When you hire Magnified Joy, you are hiring us. Not only are you hiring two photographers, you&#8217;ll also gain two lifelong friends cheering you on through your seasons of life!
You can learn a bit more about us at the about section !
Where are you located? 
We are centrally located in the western slopes of Colorado in Montrose, Colorado.
Will you travel to ______ ? 
The answer is yes! We love traveling, and our passports are always ready. Don’t let distance deter you from contacting us about your wedding. 
How many photos do you deliver for a wedding? 
We typically deliver between 600-800 photos, all edited to perfection by our standards. 
Do you do all your editing? 
Yep! En Tao does all the editing for Magnified Joy.
