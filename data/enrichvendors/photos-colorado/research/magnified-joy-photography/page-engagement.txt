You say engagement session, we say adventure session
ENGAGEMENT IS JUST ONE OF LIFE’S MANY ADVENTURES. THAT’S WHY YOU DON’T NEED TO BE ENGAGED TO JOIN US ON AN EPIC PHOTO ADVENTURE.
Adventuring is our favorite way to get to know each other and get to know new friends. So let’s go! Hiking. Biking. Turning over rocks. Let’s discover a secret path or explore a hidden ice cave. 
Pick an adventure that is true to you, and we’ll tag along to capture your story for the world. We’ll snap photos of you + your mate, you + your friends, or just you being you!   Our goal is to have a fun time while creating the most incredible images we can. 
WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT 
No. 01
To have a good time
This is mandatory!
One of our grooms confessed to not wanting to have his picture taken, but his fiancé made him do it. However, after the experience, he admitted to having a ton of fun and actually enjoying the experience! He even said he’d do it again!
No. 02
Absolutely zero awkwardness
Trust us. We'll make you look good.
Our couples often warn us that they are awkward and need lots of help. We joke, saying we only work with professional models. But the truth is, the couples we work with are just normal, everyday people like you! We take an organic approach in our direction so as to help you feel comfortable and allow us to capture true depth and authentic joy.
No. 03
Epic photos
Remember that time we...
One of the best parts about adventuring together is the memories we create. When you look back at your photos, you won’t remember just another staged photo shoot. You’ll remember the fun you had, discoveries you made, and the stories you’ll want to tell!
FAQ
Where should we go for our adventure session? 
The possibilities are endless. No distance is too far, the goal is to capture something that is truly you. So if that is skinny dipping at a hidden lake in Glacier National Park or riding an elephant through the jungles of Thailand and reenacting scenes from Indiana Jones, let&#8217;s hear it!
What do we wear? 
Whatever you want! Just try not to be too matchy-matchy. Remember, we don’t do awkward! It’s important to wear something that makes you feel comfortable and confident. If you need further guidance, feel free to ask us before your session 
How many outfits can we have? 
We typically only have enough time for one. 
PRO TIP: Wear layers for an easy way to create a different look! 
Can I get a picture like this? 
There is no need to show us your Pinterest board. You don’t want photos just like everyone else’s do you? 
Can we hang out after the shoot? 
For sure! En Tao loves a mean burrito bowl from Chipotle. And if you’re lucky, you may even get to come to our home for a home cooked meal! 
How do you pronounce En Tao? 
While Maria is a much more common name, En Tao tends to trip people up. So let us help you out: 
En &#8211; sounds like the letter “N” 
Tao &#8211; rhymes with cow 
How exactly do you put your couples at ease during a photoshoot? 
Our goal is to help you forget the camera is there. We do this in a couple ways. First of all, En Tao is known for being corny and loves a good “dad joke.” If you’ve heard any new ones, please pass them along! 
And second, we seek to enjoy the adventure right alongside. We want you to enjoy yourselves just as you would with any other friend. You being free to be yourself is of utmost importance to us because that is, after all, where the magic happens.
