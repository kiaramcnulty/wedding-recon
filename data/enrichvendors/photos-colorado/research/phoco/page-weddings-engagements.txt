Weddings & Engagements
Weddings are special to us! The way we see it, when your wedding day is over, you’ll walk away with two things: your marriage and your wedding photos. We hope that you choose a team who helps you relive your priceless, timeless memories again and again. At PHOCO, we don't take this responsibility lightly and we would be honored to capture your wedding day just as it unfolds. In the process, we hope to become not only your photographer or videographer, but friends. Or as we call it, PHRIENDS!
