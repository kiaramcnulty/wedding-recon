Engagement Sessions
Engagement sessions are a chance to step away from the planning and spend time together in a way that feels easy and unhurried. We love photographing these sessions outdoors, where movement, light, and natural surroundings help everything feel more relaxed and genuine. The photos from your session can be shared with loved ones through invitations, save-the-dates, or even shown during your wedding as a way to bring guests into your story.
​ 
More than anything, engagement sessions help create comfort and trust before the wedding day arrives. Getting familiar with how we work and how it feels to be photographed allows the wedding day to unfold with less pressure and more presence. That ease carries through the entire celebration, making space for real moments to happen naturally.

Save-The-Date & Pre-Wedding
Films 
A first chapter of your wedding story.
Save-the-date and Pre-Wedding films are a creative way to share the excitement of your wedding before the day arrives. They allow you to tell a small piece of your story through motion, sound, and atmosphere, creating something personal that can be shared with friends and family in a more meaningful way than a traditional announcement.
​ 
These short films are perfect for sending with digital invitations, sharing on your wedding website, or playing during your celebration. They also offer a relaxed opportunity to get comfortable in front of the camera and set the tone for a wedding day that feels familiar, effortless, and true to you.
The Process 
1. Getting to know you 
We start by learning about you, your story, and what you want this film to feel like. This helps shape a concept that feels personal and aligned with your wedding. We can develop an intricate story about the engagement, create visually striking scenes, or just create a vibey set piece to play during cocktail hour. It's up to you! 
2. Bringing the story to life 
Filming is relaxed and simple, usually taking one to two hours. We work together to turn our curated shot-list into an actual short film. We can get as creative as we want! 
3. A film you can share and keep 
Your save-the-date film is crafted into a short, cinematic piece you can send as a wedding announcement, share on your website, or play during your celebration. It becomes a meaningful keepsake you'll return to long after the wedding day.
