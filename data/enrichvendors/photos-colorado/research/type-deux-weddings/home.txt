What It's Like to Work Together
A brief timeline of what it's like to choose Type Deux.
Now
1. Reach out about your date 
 Share a few details about your wedding, the location, and whether you're looking for photography or a wedding film. Availability and fit are always the first priority. 
3-5 Days from now
2. A short, relaxed conversation 
 We connect over a call to talk through your plans, what matters most to you, and how you want your day to feel. This keeps expectations clear and ensures the coverage fits your wedding naturally. 
The next 3-12 Months
3. Thoughtful planning to curate a gallery you will love 
 Before the wedding day, time is spent understanding what matters most to you, the flow of the day, and the details you've put care into. This allows the coverage to feel effortless while still capturing the moments, relationships, and atmosphere that make your wedding uniquely yours. The goal is a gallery or film that feels cohesive, emotional, and true to the experience, not just a collection of images. 
On Your Wedding Day
4. A wedding story that lasts 
 Your photographs or film are crafted with care on your wedding day as a cohesive story that feels honest, cinematic, and timeless long after the day has passed. 
6-8 Weeks after your wedding
5. The Reveal 
 Your images or film are seen by you for the first time during your reveal celebration. instead of just delivering your wedding story through a dropbox link, we plan a special first look that feels like a movie premiere. this can be with your closest friends and family, or just you and your partner. Either way, we want it to be special.
