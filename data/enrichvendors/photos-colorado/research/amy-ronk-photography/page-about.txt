ABOUT AMY 
Contact information and a little about Amy Ronk. 
Hello Friends!
Hi! I'm Amy, a passionate photographer with a deep love for freezing those precious moments in time. 
 My photography journey began as a way to tell stories through images, and that's what I continue to do with every click of my camera. I thrive on the authenticity and emotion that photographs can convey, and I'm committed to capturing those heartfelt moments that make life truly remarkable.
 Beyond weddings, I embrace a wide range of photography genres, from branding and headshots to landscapes and product photography. My goal is to create timeless and compelling imagery that resonates with you, no matter the occasion.
 Contact me today to discuss your photography needs and let's bring your vision to life!
When I'm not taking photos I can usually be found out and about hiking or road-tripping with my two adorable rescue mutts, and my husband, Ben. I must admit my dogs are by far my favorite subjects to photograph! I also have about one million hobbies - my biggest struggle is trying to find the time to do everything I love at once.
 Please contact me for pricing/inquiries, or anything really-- lets go on all of the adventures together!
