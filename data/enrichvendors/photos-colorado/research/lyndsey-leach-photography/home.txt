WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN LIFESTYLE, DOCUMENTARY, AND PHOTOJOURNALISM PHOTOGRAPHY? 
PHOTOJOURNALISM: Documenting a story without bias 
DOCUMENTARY PHOTOGRAPHY: Documenting the story you want to photograph 
LIFESTYLE PHOTOGRAPHY : Documenting a story while inserting yourself in the scene with ideas and prompts

Sustainability 
I am veryyy passionate about keeping a low carbon footprint and I strive to run my businesses the same way&#8230; let&#8217;s be real, we should all be frantically changing our habits to help save this planet .  If this is something you choose to not think about or are an &#8220;ignorance is bliss&#8221; kinda human, then I definitely recommend you checking in with your privilege going down the Google rabbit hole because what you are going to find isn&#8217;t good. 
If you are overwhelmed, that&#8217;s ok because it is a very overwhelming topic. Acknowledging that climate change is real is the first step and then learning how to take action, appropriately, is the next step. $$ talks and investing your money into items, businesses, and people that do their part is FAN-FREAKIN-TASTIC.  
By no means am I an expert on this stuff, but I am knowledgeable and want to share all of my practices, areas where I can improve, and just general info and sustainability and how I apply things to my business, my own life, and to weddings/events/portraiture. I know this is not the most exciting topic, unless you nerd out to this stuff like I do, but I think it&#8217;s really important to educate yourself about what a wasteful industry we are in and what we can do to help. 
Yes, I am mean very wasteful!
