FAQ
Featured 
Do you offer payment plans?
YES. I know money doesn’t grow on trees, and I know covid hit a lot of families really hard. we can create a totally custom payment plan that fits your needs best. I truly don’t believe people should sacrifice quality for price, so that’s where the payment plans come in handy.
How long can I expect to wait for my full wedding gallery?
A sneak peek will be sent out within 48 hours of your wedding highlighting the day, the rest of the photos will be finished in 6-8 weeks from the next “business day” after your wedding.
Can you help me decide what to wear?
ABSOLUTELY! Heck yes. I love this part. I’ll send you a styling guide of colors, outfit ideas, prints etc. then you can shop around or check your closet and send me photos.
What’s all included in a wedding package?
I don’t believe weddings are a one size fits all, so I’m happy to create something unique to you. You will receive a complimentary engagement session, a styling guide, and all the edited photos from the session. You also will have two photographers covering your wedding day, all the edited photos on an online gallery, and a print release to print photos wherever you’d like.
