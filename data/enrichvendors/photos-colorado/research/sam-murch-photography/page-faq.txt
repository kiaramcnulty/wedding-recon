frequently asked questions
01. Do you have a second shooter
I do! I have an awesome second shooter, and I'd love to talk with you about the benefits of having a second photographer for your celebration!
02. Do you provide Vendor Contacts?
I've had the privilege of working with amazing vendors that are local to this area. There are so many great reasons to use businesses that are local to the area. Including, more peace of mind on your day, and less logistical struggles. All of my clients receive a list of hand picked vendors, that I know do wonderful work, and are committed to this community.
03. What happens if the weather is bad?
For the most part, we can work around the rain or snow, but sometimes it's coming and there is just no way around it. So let's make magic in the weather! I swear, some of the most romantic shots of all time were created with crazy wind blown hair in a blizzard! 
04. How long is your turn-around time?
Wedding and Elopement galleries can be expected in 6-8 weeks from the event date.
Family, senior, and other small sessions can expect their gallery within 4 weeks.
05. How will we Receive our images?
You will be given a private link and password to an online gallery of your images. You will be able to download and save high resolution files of your images. You will be connected to the highest quality professional print lab, as well as presented with a print license to use at any lab of your choosing. 
06. Do you photograph Families too?
I absolutely love capturing families and the different milestones in your life! Please contact me about senior sessions and maternity as well!
