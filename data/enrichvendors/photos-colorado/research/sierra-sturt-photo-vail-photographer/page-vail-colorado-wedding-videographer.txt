Amber & Neven Hart - February 2026 
Filming Amber and Neven Hart’s wedding carried a depth that you could feel in every moment of the day. Their story is one shaped by resilience, devotion, and choosing each other through the unexpected—after Neven’s life-changing accident in 2022 left him paralyzed, their relationship only deepened, leading to an engagement on the anniversary of that moment and a wedding that felt like a full-circle celebration of everything they’ve overcome. Neven Hart walking down the aisle with leg braces and a walker wasn’t just a moment—it was years of determination, hope, and love made visible. Just days before, they had also shared they were expecting their first baby, despite being told it was nearly impossible, adding another layer of meaning to an already emotional day. Capturing their wedding wasn’t just about documenting what happened—it was about preserving the weight of their journey, the quiet strength between them, and the undeniable sense that this day meant more because of everything it took to get there.

Vail, Colorado Wedding Videography That Feels Real 
Vail weddings are unlike anywhere else in the world - dramatic alpine views, intimate mountain venues, and an energy that’s equal parts elevated and effortless. As a Vail wedding videographer, I approach each wedding with intention and calm, blending seamlessly into the day so moments happen naturally. 
I focus on: 
 Real emotion over staged moments 
 The in-between interactions you didn’t even notice 
 Movement, sound, and storytelling, not just highlights 
Your wedding film won’t feel trendy or overly produced. It will feel true.

Vail is stunning, and also logistically unique. Having a wedding videographer who knows the area matters. I’ve filmed weddings throughout Vail, Minturn, Beaver Creek, Avon, and Eagle County, and I understand the realities of mountain weddings. 
This includes: 
 Mountain lighting and fast-changing weather
 Snowy winter timelines and summer afternoon storms
 Venue flow at locations like Vail Chapel, Sonnenalp Vail, Lionshead, and private mountain estates
 How to work seamlessly with planners and photographers in the Vail wedding industry
