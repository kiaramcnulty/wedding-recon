 Vail Proposals & Engagements 
Hi, I’m Sierra. 
As a Vail proposal and engagement photographer, I specialize in capturing intimate, authentic moments in one of the most breathtaking landscapes on earth. From the peaks of the Gore Range in the winter to the lush meadows of mid-summer with the gorgeous rivers, I help couples document their moment with a blend of natural emotion and artful, editorial and documentary elegance.

Planning a Surprise Proposal in Vail? 
A surprise proposal is a high-stakes moment. Whether you’re planning a quiet knee-drop at the Betty Ford Alpine Gardens or a grand gesture at the top of Gondola One, you need a "silent partner" who knows the terrain. 
As your Vail proposal photographer, I help you navigate the logistics - from scouting the perfect hidden spot to ensuring we catch the best light possible. I’ll be tucked away or "blending in" as a tourist until the big moment, so the surprise remains yours alone or we come up with a plan so they won't even know that the bystander you're asking for a picture by has two cameras in her bag ready to fire up when you get down on one knee.

Artful Engagement Sessions in Vail Village 
Vail offers a stunning variety of backdrops year-round. My approach to Vail engagement photography is rooted in honesty and quiet elegance. We might start with a coffee and a stroll through the cobblestone streets of Vail Village and the iconic Covered Bridge, then head to a secluded aspen grove for that classic Colorado glow. 
I create a relaxed, supportive environment where you can feel completely yourselves, allowing me to document the genuine connection that makes your story unique. 
Why Work With a Local Vail Photographer? 
The mountains are unpredictable, but your gallery shouldn't be. Being a local Vail photographer means I know the "hidden gems" that aren't on a tourist map. I understand the "golden hour" or the best light at your desired time and can recommend the best spots for a celebratory dinner at The 10th or Sweet Basil. 
While I call Vail home, I am honored to document love stories across Colorado’s most iconic destinations: 
 Beaver Creek | Breckenridge | Aspen | Snowmass | Telluride | Steamboat Springs | Crested Butte & beyond

Vail Engagement & Proposal Photography Approach 
Your engagement or proposal should feel intentional and personal. As a Vail engagement and proposal photographer, I focus on how the mountain light and the natural landscape frame your story. My goal is to capture the moments that unfold naturally, ensuring your photos feel like an authentic reflection of your relationship. 
I provide a calm, steady presence throughout the moment and planning process - offering guidance when needed and stepping back to document unguarded, candid emotions. My style blends editorial refinement with documentary storytelling, capturing everything from sweeping Vail mountain views to the quiet interactions that often go unnoticed. 
The experience of being photographed is just as important as the final images. From scouting the perfect Vail overlook to delivering your final gallery, I ensure the process is seamless and professional. Whether you are planning a Vail proposal or engagement session, I handle the logistics and the artistry so you can simply be present in the moment.
