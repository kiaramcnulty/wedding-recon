PRICING + INFORMATION GUIDE
What does it look like to work with me?
✺
You reach out. I respond.
 It’s that easy, really! Email me or fill out my contact form. From there I reach out and we review any initial questions you have an budget/package options.
✺
We schedule a phone call, facetime or zoom call. Or heck - if email is more your vibe tha’s a-ok with me. 
 From there we review your wedding day details, vision and I learn all about the two of you. 
 This is also when I send you a proposal.
✺
Yes, a million times yes! 
 I propose. Sort of. 
 I’ll send over a digital contract and invoice which includes all the details we went over regarding your wedding date, hours of coverage and what type of coverage you’d like. Once you sign and issue your initial payment - you’ll be booked!
✺
I support all my couples through the planning process. Anything from location suggestions to vendor referrals, I’m your girl!
 I check in throughout the planning process and will work side by side with you or any other vendors you have. 
✺
Leading up to your big day I’ll send you a questionnaire to either fill out on your own time or we can schedule a call to review it together. This questionnaire will be used to curate a PDF plan with all your must have shots, timeline and a full plan for your day!
✺
It’s the BIG DAY!
 I’ll arrive ready to go, plan in hand, equipment charged and prepped. Pumped to celebrate the two of you and your love story. 
 Afterwards you’ll get a sneak peek of your day (if you book photo) and within 10 weeks - you’ll get your deliverables!
Let's Do The Dang Thing
Meet your Photographer + Videographer
I'm Molly, of Molly Margaret Photography (duh) and I am pretty simple. I like sour candy, reality TV and singing in the car with my kiddos. My brother, Luke bought me photoshop and my first camera for my 15th birthday, "it's this or drivers ed" he said. Well, I didn't drive till I was 18 but I've been following people around with a camera ever since. Really though. Ask my family, following around . But heck - how else was I going to learn? I am a self taught photographer and videographer - both digital and film.
 The biggest compliment I can receive is getting genuine full of happiness moments and laughs from my couples and clients . I aim to put you at ease because let’s be honest - getting your photo taken can be awkward as heck . From goofy jokes to giving you specific directions - i’m there to help! My goal is to take away that awkwardness and replace it with an awesome hang out session where you get cute, vibrant, full of love and life moments capturing you and your love story.
 Also, because it’s important to be said - this is a safe space where I celebrate all love stories.
