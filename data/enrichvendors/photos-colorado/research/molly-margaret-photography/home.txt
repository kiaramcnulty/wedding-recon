Meet Your Photographer + Videographer Molly (that’s me!)
Oh, Hey! I'm Molly, your friendly neighborhood Colorado wedding photographer and videographer that shoots both digital and film with a love for storytelling. Originally from Wisconsin, I now call Colorado home. When I'm not busy editing photos or capturing precious moments for clients, you can find me hanging out with my awesome husband and our two adorable children. 
 Okay, I admit it, during my downtime I have a guilty pleasure for reality TV. Can we please have a Real Housewives of Denver edition? How fun would that be?
 I believe in creating a relaxed and comfortable atmosphere, because let's be honest - getting your photo taken can feel super awkward sometimes. But don't worry, I've got your back! I'll go the extra mile to put you at ease. Whether it's cracking goofy jokes or guiding you with specific directions, I'm here to help you look and feel amazing. My goal is to transform that initial awkwardness into a fun and memorable experience – like hanging out with a friend – where we capture cute, vibrant, and full of life photos that truly reflect who you are. Let me tell you, the biggest compliment I receive as a photographer is when my couples and clients have genuine moments of happiness and laughter - and I get to capture that! 
 Also, because it’s important to be said - this is a safe space where I celebrate all love stories.
