Frequently Asked Questions
How would you define your style?
 I specialize in capturing genuine, unscripted moments for couples who embrace the joyful chaos of their elopement or wedding day. The ultimate compliment for me is to see my clients filled with happiness and laughter in their candid shots. I edit in a true-to-color style to create classic images that will remain timeless and beautiful, whether displayed in your home or shared on Instagram.
 We really want to book you! What do we do next?
 First off - YAY! I’d be so flipping honored to capture your big day, you do not even realize. 
 Second off - reach out to me via the book me button above or email me at hello@mollymargaretphotography.com - or heck slide into my DMS on insta or facebook but I will just direct you back to those two listed above ;-)
 Do you offer any other photography? Like families or newborn?
 I do and I love capturing my clients family photos! It’s a limited weekday only schedule but feel free to check out my pricing here, and my portfolio here. These sessions are also available as digital or film and digital combination.
 Do you offer Super 8?
 As of mid 2025 I do offer Super 8! It is limited to Video only bookings at this time. You can add it on to photo/video bookings or photo bookings just note it when you inquire.
Why should we choose you?
 When it comes to selecting your wedding photographer or videographer, it's more than just skill – it's about finding someone you click with. Yes, it might sound simple, but hey, you'll be spending your entire big day with me! If you love to laugh, have a blast, and still want things to run smoothly, then I'm your go-to gal.
 Hailing from the Midwest, I bring that friendly vibe where smiles and "how are you's" go a long way. I've played multiple roles on weddings – from being a makeshift therapist for my brides to stepping in as an extra bridesmaid when the situation calls for it (Boutonniere pinning? Dress bustling? I've got you covered!). And when you're just overflowing with happiness, I'm there to be a supportive shoulder to lean on.
 I'm not afraid to take charge to capture all those must-have shots, yet I'm always kind and accommodating to your excited guests who are ready to celebrate with you. Let's make your day stress-free and full of fun – because that's what it's all about!
 What equipment do you use?
 I work with Full Frame Nikon and Sony cameras, along with professional lighting equipment as required. In certain situations, I also employ off-camera flash or continuous lighting for dimly lit areas or receptions. I always have backups for my cameras, lenses, and batteries. Additionally, I shoot with a dual card system that provides in-camera backup for your images. I do not offer any drone coverage for photo or video. 
 For my film photography and videography I shoot on canon super 8 and nikon film cameras.
 Can I print my photos when you send them to me?
 Yes! I recommend either printing directly through your album that I send you (pixieset), mpix, or Artifact Uprising use MOLLYMARGARET15 for 15% off.
Do you work with all types of couples, love stories and religions?
 Absolutely! I want to celebrate all love stories.

 Will you be the person who captures our wedding or will you hire someone else?
 I do not assign my weddings to associate photographers. However, I may bring on second photographers, second videographers, or non-shooting assistants to support me during the event. These additional team members are there to collaborate with me. In the rare event that I am unable to attend your wedding due to an emergency or an act of god, I may need to send an associate photographer/videographer in my place.
 Do you offer video services?
 Yes! I am a photographer AND videographer, check out in my pricing guide for both photo/video pricing options or just video options if you’re interested :-) I also offer some video add-ons like ceremony films etc if you’re just looking for special moments captured in addition to photography of your full day.
