Hey, I’m Molly
I'm Molly, of Molly Margaret Photography (duh) and I am pretty simple. I like sour candy, reality TV and singing in the car with my kiddos. My brother, Luke bought me photoshop and my first camera for my 15th birthday, "it's this or drivers ed" he said. Well, I didn't drive till I was 18 but I've been following people around with a camera ever since. Really though. Ask my family, following around . But heck - how else was I going to learn? I am a self taught photographer and videographer - both digital and film.
 The biggest compliment I can receive is getting genuine full of happiness moments and laughs from my couples and clients . I aim to put you at ease because let’s be honest - getting your photo taken can be awkward as heck . From goofy jokes to giving you specific directions - i’m there to help! My goal is to take away that awkwardness and replace it with an awesome hang out session where you get cute, vibrant, full of love and life photos capturing you and your love story.
 Also, because it’s important to be said - this is a safe space where I celebrate all love stories. 🏳️‍🌈
