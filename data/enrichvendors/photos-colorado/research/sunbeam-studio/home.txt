Who We Are
 “At Sunbeam Studio we believe that you deserve the very best, from in-depth hair consultations with expert stylists to clean & sustainable hair products . With every detail, we have you in mind. We are here to make your hair dream a reality!”
High-End Hair That Is Low Maintenance
 Sunbeam Studio is Louisville’s top salon for personalized dimensional color and high-end hair that is low maintenance . Sunbeam Studio was founded by April Wetzel in 2020 as a single chair studio and is now a 6 chair luxury salon. Located in beautiful Downtown Louisville, Sunbeam Studio is in a building that was once an auto body shop that has been transformed into the home of the ultimate laid back luxury hair experience.
Sunbeam Studio is a proud Gold Level member of the Colorado Green Business Network!
As a Green Circle Salon partner, Sunbeam Studio diverts 93% of our waste by taking the extra steps to make sure we are composting and recycling.
The clean beauty products used and sold at Sunbeam Studio are organic, naturally derived, sustainably sourced, refillable, recyclable and made of recycled material. 
Sunbeam Studio donates 1% of all profits to Conscious Alliance to help deliver means to children and families in need.

At Sunbeam Studio, we are deeply committed to sustainability, which is why we have a 4% green business tax . As a gold-level sustainable salon, we go the extra mile to ensure that we use all organic and sustainable products. Additionally, we partner with Green Circle Salons to divert 93% of our waste by composting and recycling.
 This tax is crucial for several reasons:
Funding Sustainable Products 
Organic and sustainable products often come at a higher cost compared to conventional alternatives. The tax helps us cover these additional expenses, ensuring that our clients receive eco-friendly services without compromising on quality.
Waste Management Costs 
Diverting 93% of our waste involves meticulous sorting, composting, and recycling processes, which can be labor-intensive and costly. The tax helps offset these expenses, allowing us to maintain our high standards in waste reduction.
Green Circle Salons Partnership 
Our participation in the Green Circle Salons program requires an investment in specialized waste management systems and practices. The tax supports this partnership, enabling us to adhere to stringent sustainability protocols.
By implementing this tax, we ensure that we can continue to provide environmentally responsible services, support our green initiatives, and contribute to a healthier planet.
