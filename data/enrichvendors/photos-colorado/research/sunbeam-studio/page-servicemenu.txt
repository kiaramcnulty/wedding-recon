custom color services
 At Sunbeam Studio, we believe you deserve the very best for your hair. This is why every custom hair color service includes all glosses and toners, bond builders, deep conditioning treatments, a trim and gray blending if needed, and style of your choice. Not sure which session to book? Text the salon!
New Client Full Custom Color Session: At Sunbeam Studio we want to start your hair journey off on the right foot! If you are a new client, booking a Full Custom Color Session will ensure that your stylist has enough time to create a beautiful look. This includes a hair detox and clarifying treatment. 
 Time needed: 3+ hours 
Full Custom Color Session: This is perfect for anyone wanting to transform their hair color. From lived-in blonde, dimensional brunette or natural reds- this session includes a full head customized color placement, combining techniques like balayage, highlights, lowlights and color melting to create your desired look. We recommend a Full Custom Color Session 1-2 times a year.
 Time needed: 3+ hours 
Partial Custom Color Session: Enhance your color with a partial head customized placement, combining balayage and highlights to create your desired look. We recommend a Partial Custom Color Session every 3-4 months.
 Time needed: 2.5+ hours 
Refresh Custom Color Session: Does your color need a little pick me up? This session includes a custom color refresh of face frame/hairline and the top of the head where you part your hair, plus a refresh of your gloss/toner to keep your color looking fresh between services. This session is also a great option for grey coverage and gloss. We recommend a Refresh Session every 8 weeks, and can be an easy in-between session to get your hair color to last between Full or Partial sessions. Returning clients can book this session for express grey blending as well. Please do not book this service if it has been 8+ weeks since your last visit or if you have never been to Sunbeam Studio before.
 Time needed: 1.5+ hours 
Modern Grey Blending: A low-maintenance color service designed to blend grey hair beautifully with fewer salon visits. Using modern placements that work with your natural color, this technique creates a soft, seamless grow-out, adding natural dimension while preserving balanced tones and healthy hair. If you are a new client, 6-8 weeks+ of regrowth is preferable before your first modern grey blending appointment so your stylist can see your natural color.
 Time needed: 2-3+ hours 
Custom Color Melt Gloss and Trim: This session is the perfect way to add shine and revitalize your current hair color while enhancing the shape and health of your haircut. A customized gloss will refresh tone, add shine and condition your hair. The perfect pick-me-up between longer color services. Suggested every 6 weeks.
 Time needed: 1.5+ hours 
Transformational Color: A transformative and corrective service to give your hair a complete makeover. This session is necessary if your hair needs an overhaul. Your hair’s health is the most important thing for us, and we will get you set on the right path in the most gentle way possible. Let us help you hit the reset button and get you on the way to having dream hair. 
 Time needed: 4+ hours 
 Please fill out online consultation form to request booking for this service.
