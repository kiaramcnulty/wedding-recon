cutting services
 Our haircutting sessions are gender neutral. Choose the session that best suits your current hair length and what you are trying to achieve! 
1 Hour Haircut Session: Treat your hair to a detox and clarifying treatment, deep conditioning treatment, remove split ends and create a fresh shape for your hair. This session includes a wash, blow dry and style of your choice.
Long Haircut: Keeping your hair long? No problem. Our stylists can refresh your ends and add movement and shape to your long locks. Book this haircut session if you are wanting to keep your hair at shoulder length or longer. This session includes a wash, blow dry and style of your choice.
Modern Shag Haircut: The perfect haircut for clients with hair shoulder length or longer. This cut creates movement, adds curtain fringe and brings out your natural texture with a Modern Shag look that is timeless. This session includes a wash, blow dry and style of your choice.
30 Minute Dry Haircut Session: This is perfect for returning clients who would love to maintain length and/or are overall happy with the shape of their hair, but could use a little reshape and removal of split ends. This is a great way to keep your hair feeling fresh and healthy. Shorter clipper style cuts and fades are also booked under this session. Clients must come to this appointment with clean, dry hair.
