How do we book with you?  
Booking a wedding or engagement session with us is an incredibly easy process. We ask for a 40% retainer up front, and the remainder of the balance can be paid at any point up to 4 weeks before your wedding date.
Who will our photographer/videographer be?  
You&#8217;re able to choose your photographer based on who you feel like would be the best fit for your wedding! Or, we would be happy to recommend one. Your videographer will be assigned three months from your wedding.
All of the members of our team are experienced and will provide you with an amazing experience. To learn more about who your media team will be, please visit our Booking Process page. 
How long until we get our photos and video back?  
We will deliver all photos and a draft of your video within 8 weeks.
Which package is best for us?  
We like to have an initial consultation with all our couples before booking with us. It&#8217;s during this process we&#8217;ll get to know you, gather information on your wedding day and make recommendations based on your needs. You can view our pricing and services here.  
Where is your area of coverage?  
We provide photo and video coverage throughout all of Colorado and parts of Wyoming and Nebraska. Do you have a destination wedding? Contact us and we&#8217;ll put together a custom package for you.
How far out should we book you? 
For prime wedding dates, most couples book us an average of 8 months out from their event.
