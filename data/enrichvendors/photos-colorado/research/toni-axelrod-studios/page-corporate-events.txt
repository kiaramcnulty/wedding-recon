Your company's event says something about who you are as a business.
 Look to Toni Axelrod Studios to provide complete unsurpassed media services including photography and videography to the discerning corporate client. We are available to capture and document company meetings, award ceremonies, head shots, celebratory parties, retreats and group adventure activities like skiing, golfing, and rafting. We can turn media around fast with on-site printing or high resolution image downloads. Additional services we offer include: on-site head shot studio , product photography , lecture and meeting video documentation , green screen capabilities , standard and green screen photo booth , custom DVD creation, animations, diagrams, highlight videos, and aerial coverage .
 Contact us for more information regarding your company event!
