Wedding Photography
 Let us capture your wedding day story with unique and breathtaking images that you and your family will cherish for a lifetime. From the Vail and Beaver Creek Chapels to high mountain venues and destinations around the world, we have your wedding photography covered! We will help preserve your memories so that the details of your big day will never be forgotten. With Colorado and the Rocky Mountains as your backdrop, your guests will know your event will be one to remember! 
 We are all about capturing connection and telling the story of your entire wedding weekend. Here are some of the services we offer:
 Pre-wedding photography, a standard in Asia and throughout Europe, is when the wedding couple is photographed prior to the wedding date. Pre-wedding photography is one of our favorite ways of documenting the beauty and passion of your relationship without the time constraints of the wedding day. We pair dramatic lighting and landscapes to create unique photographs that will be shared with generations to come. 
 Rehearsal Dinner Photography allows us to capture your families interacting during a more informal gathering. This is a great time get additional photographs of guests and extended families enjoying the evening. 
 We love photographing your guests playing golf, hiking the trails or goofing off on a ropes course. This is a great time to record your guests letting go and taking in the beauty of Vail and Beaver Creek. 
 Bridal Sessions allow you to show off the dress in another location before the big day. Some of our clients prefer to have their bridal sessions in a city and their weddings in the mountains. We can cater to your preferences and stage the shoot with dramatic lighting. 
 We can work seamlessly with your wedding planner to help them develop a timeline that keeps your event running smoothly and to make sure that the scheduling of your formal photo sessions during the wedding will leave you with plenty of time to share the joy with family and friends. We have assisted with the planning and documenting of wedding proposals - so exciting! 
 Additional services include wedding films, green screen or standard photo booths, and so much more. 
 Contact us to request a phone call or video conference so we can start planning for the excitement to come!
 Our passports are current and we will travel to any destination you desire!
 Contact us for more information!
