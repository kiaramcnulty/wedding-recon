Mountain Proposals Photography
We get so excited every time we hear about a mountain proposal! What better place to pop the question than in Mother Nature’s most beautiful canvas. We capture mountain proposals tucked away in secret until the moment comes to share the news. As local photographers, we know all the best places to get down on one knee, hide in the trees, and help make this the most memorable day in your lives to date. And we want to help.
With our mountain proposal offering, you not only get this incredible moment forever frozen in time but for those seeking a little help getting to the big question, we’re happy to be there for you too.
Once we’ve provided you with local suggestions for the picture-perfect place to pop the question, then we decide if I’ll be completely hidden, hiding in plain sight (like a tourist), or part of an elaborate story your future fiance is told. Plan a family session so your partner is dressed and ready for their close up, and we’ll play the part! Whatever you imagine, we get so excited to help you create it.
Once the big question has been asked, we take some time for a mini engagement session to capture fun candid portraits in the mountains together. Then we’ll provide a sneak peek of your proposal photos typically the same day so that you have beautiful imagery to share the big news with.
These images make great save-the-dates, wedding website photos, and even wedding reception details. You’ll be happy you have these at so many different moments throughout your lifetime.

What’s Included in a Colorado Mountain Marriage Proposal?
We can help you plan the proposal
Our Colorado mountain marriage proposal includes beautiful imagery for you to share at your engagement party or wedding, that you’ll frame in your future homes and show your children. But there’s a lot of planning that goes into these once-in-a-lifetime moments to make them perfect. We’ll take care of that too. 
If you want the full-service all-inclusive experience we do that too! You’ll receive a local location guide (with places to propose in, and around Breckenridge, Beaver Creek, Keystone, and Vail, Colorado), prep and planning, setup and teardown, and a complimentary bottle of champagne with toasting flutes. Then, a mini engagement session following your proposal, once the surprise has been celebrated. We welcome the opportunity to make this day unforgettable and the photos equally as memorable.
Same Day Sneak Peek Photos
Included in the photography is a same-day sneak peek so that you can share the great news with your family and friends afterward.
Don’t leave such an important moment to chance. Allow us to be your local guide to getting down on one knee and ensuring every moment is picture-perfect so you can marvel in your memories for a lifetime.
