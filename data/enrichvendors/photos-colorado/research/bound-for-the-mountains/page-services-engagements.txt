Colorado Engagement Photography
Our Colorado engagement photography is some of our favorite work to produce. In this setting, we have the time to be fun and creative together while you bask in the glow of the excitement of your upcoming wedding. You direct the style, we create the setting. Formal or casual, you decide what you’d like the experience to be and what the images portray. We’ll make it memorable in each and every way. 
Our Colorado mountain engagement photography sessions are perfect for couples who want to capture this special time but perhaps don’t plan on getting married in the mountains. This session gives you that magical setting in everyday clothes that you might frame more often than your wedding portraits. If you’re recently engaged and looking for beautiful outdoor imagery to share on a save-the-date or use at your upcoming wedding, then consider engagement photos in the mountains.
Newlyweds on your honeymoon in Colorado?
Already married and looking for a fun session dressed up in your wedding day clothes? Then our newlywed session is perfect for you! What are the best places for Colorado Engagement Photos?
There’s no better place for portraits, especially Colorado engagement photos, than in the scenic town of Breckenridge. We’ve explored nearly every trail and scenic spot in the surrounding area, in pursuit of the most gorgeous backdrops and views. We look for areas that are easy to access, but somewhat hidden in order to avoid much foot traffic. We seek out a variety of texture and color for the backdrop, which helps to create striking photos. We pay attention to lighting at different times of day to know exactly when and where to set up for the best Colorado engagement photos. We set us all up for success ahead of time so the day is one of enjoyment and magic. We take into account your preferences for attire, activity, and hiking length.
Some of our favorite spots around Summit Country for Colorado engagement photos include:
Sapphire Point, Breckenridge
Blue Lakes, Blue River
Windy Point
Boreas Pass Road
Lake Dillon
Loveland Pass
