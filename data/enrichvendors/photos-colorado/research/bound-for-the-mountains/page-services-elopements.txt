What is included in Colorado Elopement Photography Collections?
We offer four types of elopement collections:
Micro Weddings
Mountain Elopements
Intimate Weddings
Short and Sweet
Micro Weddings
Micro Weddings cover every detail of your small, intimate wedding. Up to 6 hours of wedding coverage means that all the thoughtful details will be beautifully captured including getting ready, first look, ceremony, newlywed portraits, family and wedding party, and your intimate reception dinner.
Mountain Elopements
Mountain Elopements are the perfect mini collection option for those who want their vow exchanged captured and gorgeous pictures of just you two in the mountains. Coverage up to 2 hours of photography. Exclusively for just you two and up to 10 guests.
Intimate Weddings
Our Intimate Weddings option is for couples planning an intimate wedding with their loved ones in the mountains. We assist with planning locations and recommending local vendors who are perfect for giving you personally curated details for your day. Coverage up to 4 hours.
Short & Sweet
For something Short and Sweet, we document your self-solemnization ceremony and mountain newlywed portraits with just the two of you.
