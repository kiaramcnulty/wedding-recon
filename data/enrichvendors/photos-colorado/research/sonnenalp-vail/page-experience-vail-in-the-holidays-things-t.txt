 Christmas and Winter Holiday Fun in Vail, Colorado 
Holiday Magic at Sonnenalp & Beyond 
There’s no place quite like Vail’s holiday and Christmas magic. Each December, Vail Village and Lionshead transform into a winter wonderland of cobblestone streets draped in sparkling lights, towering Christmas trees in every square, and the scent of fir and pine floating in the cold alpine air. From the charming European-style Vail Kris Kringle Market and festive tree lighting ceremonies to ice-skating under the stars and cozy sleigh rides through snow-covered trail, there's something for everybody. And when the mountain fun winds down, relax in luxury at Sonnenalp Hotel with special Christmas Eve dinners, warming fires, family ornament-decorating and hot cocoa by the fireplace.

Signature Holiday Events in Vail
VAIL RESTAURANT WEEK 
Kick off the holiday season early with incredible and diverse culinary offerings available throughout the Vail and Beaver Creek Villages. To further enhance you dining experience, each bite is perfectly paired with spectacular winter views spread across the stunning mountain backdrops provided by Vail and Beaver Creek.
REVELY VAIL 
Revely Vail welcomes the brilliance of winter, offering family-oriented activities throughout Vail Village including Ice Skating Exhibitions, Silent Discos, Tree Lighting, the Kris Kringle Market and more. November - December  https://www.revelyvail.com/ 
ART WORKSHOPS 
Join us after your ski day to create your Vail memorable moments in this free art workshop with Alpine Art Center.
VAIL APRÉS | VILLAGE CONCERTS 
Enjoy this festival-like atmosphere with multiple live outdoor concerts on the International Bridge in Vail Village.
REVELY TREE LIGHTING 
Join us to light up the village with the Lionshead Holiday Tree Lighting! Enjoy a free 15 minute exhibition of Champion Figure Skaters on the Arrabelle Ice Rink, live music and special appearance of a special red clad, jovial, bearded guest.
OUTDOOR ICE SKATING SHOWS 
Enjoy a free, 15 minute outdoor exhibition of Elite Skating. The show is high energy ice entertainment with great popular and holiday themed music. Featuring World, International, NATHAN CHEN Team USA Skaters, and Seasoned Professionals.
 https://vailskatefest.com/ 
YETI HOCKEY GAMES 
Experience the thrill of ice hockey in Vail with the Yeti Hockey team, where fast-paced action meets family fun, making it a must-visit event for an unforgettable night out in the heart of the Rockies!

New Year's Eve in Vail
New Year’s Eve Silent Disco 
Join in New Year's Eve festivities in Vail! 4:00 – 7:00 pm boogie at the Silent Disco in Lionshead. Get in the fireworks spirit with an outdoor Silent Disco at Sunbird Park near the ice rink.
New Year’s Eve Fireworks 
Kick off the festive evening with a New Year's Even Fireworks display! The short show starts at 6:25pm and will be visible from all across the valley floor including Vail Village, Golden Peak, Lionshead Village, and across I-70 in West Vail.
