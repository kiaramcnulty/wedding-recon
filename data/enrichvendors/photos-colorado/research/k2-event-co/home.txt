 Our passion is watching joy unfold in the process of curating events unique to each client. Whether surrounded by the natural beauty of landscapes or building a bold look from a clean slate, K2 Event Co. is ready to produce an unforgettable event for you.
LEARN ABOUT OUR PLANNING
Kristen began her weddings and events work with the Telluride Ski Resort in 2015 and founded the K2 planning team in 2020. From winter wedding logistics to mountain rain storms, she has seen it all, and handles any obstacle with knowledge and grace. Trisha comes from a hospitality and IT background, creating the perfect get-it-done duo! These ladies love what they do, and you can rest assured knowing that items are always moving forward for your day, since they divide and conquer as a team.
