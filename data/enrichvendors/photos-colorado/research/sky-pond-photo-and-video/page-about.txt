A little bit about Sky Pond Photography and Video Productions
My last class at University of Michigan in Ann Arbor as a Natural Resource and Environment major happened to be a nature photography course. I was instantly hooked and never looked back. I made Estes Park, Colorado my home in 1999 and pursued landscape photography in Colorado with a passion. It was soon thereafter that I realized I loved being creative in all fields of photography. Whether it be photographing an Estes Park family reunion, the interior of a stunning mountain cabin or a couples dream mountain wedding, capturing a moment in time with beautiful imagery is unbeatable. I also found that I loved the challenge of working in all different mediums and the added dimension of telling stories through video was a thrill. 
Sky Pond Photo and Video is a member of 1% for the Planet . We donate 1% of our gross revenue to environmental organizations. We recycle 100% of viable material including composting our food waste. Living at the doorstep of Rocky Mountain National Park reminds us daily of the importance of environmental stewardship. 
Alexander Ho 
 Hiking with My Daughter in Yellowstone National Park, Fall of 2018
