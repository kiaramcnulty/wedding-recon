EXPERIENCE ABOVE ALL
PROCESS 
Getting to know you from our first hello is a privilege . 
Because we only photograph a select number of weddings a year, we focus on you and your celebration in a way that only a truly natural connection can foster. 
We will craft a personalized package for you, and check in with you and your wedding team to make sure we know what means the most to you. We can deliver the day you have envisioned. 
“We will cherish our photos for the rest of time.”
— B & K 
APPROACH 
Technically sound so we can tell your story correctly, we use purely professional cameras and equipment. We use flash to light receptions, and true natural light throughout your day. 
We collaborate perfectly with your other vendors, and with your family dynamics. We dress well and know the names of your favorite people. 
EXPERIENCE 
You will receive full resolution images with printing rights, a private hosted online gallery, and personal pre-wedding consultations. 
In addition, you will have the opportunity to add on custom albums, as well as pre-wedding and engagement sessions.
