Howdy, We’re 
Colt + Hannah!
We’re husband and wife Durango based wedding, elopement, and lifestyle photographers traveling through the beautiful San Juan Mountains and the desert Southwest to capture some of life's most precious moments. Specializing in weddings small and large, lifestyle family sessions, family, maternity, and all the in-between. We are lovers of adventure, exploring our little pocket of Southwest Colorado as often as we can. We love spending time together outside, exploring new wild places, and creating memories with our own sweet little family. 
We chased our dreams of an adventure based lifestyle and made Durango our home a little over six years ago. We've been capturing and telling your stories since landing in Southwest Colorado. Every story is unique and deserves to be authentically captured and told. It would be our joy to explore the mountains, valleys, rivers, and sandstone arches with you. Let's embark on a journey together to celebrate your story, to create moments to remember, and to create feelings to relive for a lifetime.

Our love story
Our love story began by chance one afternoon when we both happened to be exploring those Alabama dirt roads. My friend’s car broke down so we killed the time by dancing to our favorite tune on the side of the road. A car pulled over to give us a hand and guess who it was - a super cute fella named Colt. A short hello, but a long lasting impression. We hung out everyday that week and quickly fell in love. Our love grew deeper as we started adventuring, creating, traveling near and far, and enjoying life together. We picked up our life in Alabama and moved across country to land in Durango, Colorado for our next great adventure. 
 We eloped in the mountains outside of Silverton, Colorado in June 2020. Just parents, siblings, and of course our little fur baby, Juniper. We couldn’t have imagined a better way to celebrate our love. We welcomed our first silly girl into the world in November of 2023, Cohen "Coco" Fetters. She's as joyous and as sweet as sunshine.
Let’s adventure together!
We’ve built our lives around two of our most cherished relationships: our relationship with the outdoors and with people. When these two things are combined, it fills up our cups and stokes the fire inside us! We live for alpine hikes, epic ski tours, and backcountry bike rides, especially those that are shared amongst friends, new and old. 
We care about these places, we hope you do too!
We give one percent of our annual revenue to One Percent for the Planet , an international organization that distributes funds to environmental causes to protect the environment.
