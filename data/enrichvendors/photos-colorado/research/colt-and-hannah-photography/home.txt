Why choose Durango for your wedding and elopement
What makes Durango a special place to get married? 
There’s something about Durango that makes people slow down and soak in the moment. With the San Juan Mountains, historic downtown, and endless outdoor adventures, your wedding becomes more than a single day. It becomes an experience your favorite people will remember. As locals, we love that Durango blends adventure with small-town charm, creating the perfect place to celebrate your story.
What are the best places to get married in Durango? 
One of our favorite things about Durango is the incredible variety of wedding venues and landscapes. From elegant ranches and mountain lodges to resorts and private properties, couples can create a celebration that feels completely their own. Some of our favorite Durango wedding venues include River Bend Ranch, Purgatory Resort, and Black Diamond Lodge. Each offering a beautiful backdrop for an unforgettable day.
Is it easy to get married in Colorado? 
Colorado makes getting married refreshingly simple. Couples can easily obtain a marriage license, and Colorado allows self-solemnization. Meaning you don’t need an officiant or witnesses if you want a more private ceremony. Whether you’re planning a traditional wedding or an intimate elopement, the process allows you to focus on what matters most: celebrating your marriage.
What time of year is best for a Durango wedding? 
Every season in Durango brings something unique. Summer offers wildflowers and warm mountain evenings, fall brings golden aspens and crisp air, winter creates a cozy snow-covered landscape, and spring offers fresh greenery and a quieter experience. The best season is simply the one that feels most like you.
Is Durango more affordable than other Colorado mountain towns? 
Durango offers the beauty of a Colorado mountain wedding without the same price tag found in many popular resort towns. Couples can create an unforgettable destination wedding experience with incredible venues and local vendors. We feel like it’s one of Colorado’s best-kept secrets.
What does wedding photography look like in Durango? 
As Durango photographers, we’re constantly inspired by the landscapes surrounding us. Within a short drive, you’ll find mountain peaks, alpine lakes, wildflower meadows, rivers, forests, historic streets, and desert views. Or as we like to see it, endless possibilities for dreamy backdrops and dramatic Colorado views.
