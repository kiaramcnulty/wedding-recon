Elopement Services
Belleview Events offers out-of-the-box, creative, and adventurous elopements in the surrounding mountains and valleys of Crested Butte, CO. Have you ever dreamed of hiking into a private ceremony under a blue bird sky with only the mountains as your witness? Want to spend your wedding night in a custom built camper under the Milky Way? Eat an incredible picnic dinner made for you by Crested Butte&#8217;s premier personal chef?
YOU CAN! 
Elopement services are offered in a similar manner as Belleview&#8217;s main event service but with a lower minimum. Jamie will handle all vendor bookings, set up, breakdown and delivery to your one-of-a-kind elopement.
