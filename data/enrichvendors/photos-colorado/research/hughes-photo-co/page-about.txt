CHRISTOPHER HUGHES
My 4 year old son took this photo of me. It’s blurry, It’s obviously not perfectly composed, but it’s important you understand this:
 This is me without the burden of perfection you see in most photos, and this is the way I’ll approach your wedding day (obviously with your photos in focus, though). 
 I have a beautiful family of 4. My wife (Hannah), son (Jude), and daughter (Della) are 50% of the reason I do what I do. You’re the other 50%. 
 The reason people are the driving factor behind what I do is this: My parents were missionaries when I was growing up and seeing them serve people unabashedly and wholeheartedly in China, Taiwan, Thailand, and many other countries inspired me to do the same. 
 Travel is a huge part of our family’s lives, and for that reason it’s been heavily incorporated into my business. I travel all around the United States for most of my shoots, but have also had the privilege of shooting in Ireland, Scotland, New Zealand, and over a dozen states in North America.
 If you’re ready to make the leap hit the “Contact” button, I’m excited to get to know you and your story better — I can’t wait!
