ABOUT Catherine Norwood Photo 
 Catherine leads Catherine Norwood Photo, a Colorado photography company based in Denver. Catherine excels at telling genuine and joyful stories through photography.
 With over a decade of experience photographing weddings across the U.S. and internationally, Catherine Norwood Photo specializes in documenting weddings with a balance of editorial art direction and an honest documentary approach. Catherine continues to refine her craft and finds inspiration from film photographers, nature, novels, friends, family, and meditation. Catherine Norwood Photo is LGBTQIA+ owned and inclusive of all stories.

When do clients book? 
The earlier clients reach out, the better chance Catherine Norwood Photo will have their preferred date available for a proposal, wedding, or portrait photography. Most wedding clients book around 8-18 months out, and proposal clients book around 1-2 months out.
What's Catherine Norwood’s Photo’s Style? 
As a Colorado photographer, Catherine specializes in timeless, true-to-color photography that feels true to each client. She uses a documentary approach to capture authentic moments with gentle posing guidance so clients feel natural and at ease. Catherine Norwood creates galleries of mixed candids, relaxed portraits, and a few editorial poses. She blends editorial art direction with her documentary photo approach, tailoring her approach to each client’s needs.
Catherine Norwood Photo Origins 
Catherine has over a decade of professional photography experience and a BFA in photography and design. Catherine has photographed hundreds of weddings, portrait sessions, and events. Catherine Norwood Photo started portrait photography in 2011, started wedding photography in 2017, and began full-time documentary wedding and lifestyle photography in 2021.
Balancing Candid and Posed Photos 
The balance between candid and posed photos depends on the session duration. While shorter shoots have more posed shots, longer sessions blend candid moments with curated poses.
Turnaround Times 
Catherine Norwood delivers final photos within four to six weeks and previews within two weeks. Catherine Norwood Photo delivers weddings within eight to twelve weeks and previews within two weeks.
Travel Beyond Denver 
Catherine is happy to travel up to two hours outside the Denver metro at no extra cost. Clients can reach out for a personalized travel quote for further US and international destinations.
How Will Clients Feel At A Session? 
Catherine provides an encouraging, and prepared presence for all. She uses her tools as a trained photographer and mindfulness coach, along with her background in hospitality, to create fun, empowering experiences for each client.
Catherine Norwood Photo Celebrates And Welcomes All. 
Catherine Norwood Photo works to create welcoming, celebratory spaces for all. As a queer-owned Denver photography business, she is proud to document all love stories rooted in joy and inclusion. She has photographed many BIPOC, LGBTQIA+, and multicultural weddings and sessions and brings deep care, curiosity, and respect to everyone she works with. Everyone deserves to feel seen, safe, and celebrated. Catherine also serves as the Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, and Belonging Director for the Wedding International Professional Association’s Colorado Chapter. Through training and education within and beyond the organization, Catherine brings together others in the wedding community and uses tools to create a more affirming, accessible, inclusive, and equitable wedding world.
Are you licensed and insured? 
Yes, Catherine Norwood Photo is a licensed and insured Colorado photographer. If a proposal location requires a Certificate of Insurance, Catherine is happy to provide one.
