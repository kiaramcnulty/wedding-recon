Hi, I'm Jess. 
I'm a Western Colorado photographer, Navy veteran, and mom based in Montrose. I photograph weddings, families, and seniors across Telluride, Ouray, and the San Juan Mountains.
Most of my clients tell me they feel awkward in front of the camera &mdash; and by the end of the session they're surprised by how natural the experience felt. 
My work centers on connection, movement, and honest moments that feel like real life. I'm drawn to the quiet interactions people share when they feel comfortable &mdash; the kind of moments that tell the real story of a relationship, a family, or a season of life. 
My Approach
Most people come to a session thinking they need to know how to pose or what to do. The truth is, you don't. 
I guide sessions naturally so nothing feels stiff or forced. From choosing the right location to giving simple prompts during the shoot, my goal is always the same: 
Some of the best images happen in between the planned moments &mdash; laughter, movement, the way people look at each other when they forget the camera is there. 
Why This Work Matters to Me
My years in the Navy taught me to observe quietly and pay attention to the details most people miss. Photography grew out of that same instinct &mdash; noticing the moments that matter and preserving them. 
Now as a mom, I understand even more how quickly life moves. The photographs we keep become part of how we remember the seasons we lived through. 
​ 
That's what I hope to create for every client: images that feel meaningful not just today, but years from now.
