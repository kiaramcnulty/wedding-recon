Aether is Aurelie Slegers and Ben Eng 
We are a pair of photographers, filmmakers, storytellers, artists, and mountain-dwellers committed to crafting a raw, genuine vision of the most important day of your life. 
Together we bring almost 2 decades of combined experience (and living in Telluride) to the wedding world tempered with Aurelie&#x27;s background in filmmaking and portraiture and Ben&#x27;s background in editorial, winter sports, and environmental portraiture. Wether its a 200 person blowout at Gorrono Ranch or just the two of you on top of a 14&#x27;er at sunrise, we are there for you.
