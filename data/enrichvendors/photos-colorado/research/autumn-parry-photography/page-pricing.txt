What's the difference (Doc-style VS Associates?) 
There are a few key differences between the two collections: the photographers, the perks, and the pricing.
 First, the photographers. Autumn and Griffin are former photojournalists with backgrounds in the newspaper industry, which makes them especially attuned to real moments as they naturally unfold. The associate packages, by contrast, take a slightly more hands-on approach on the wedding day, with a more classic touch in the overall style.
 Second, the perks. The documentary-style packages include more add-ons, such as a photo booth, ceremony video, and Just Married session.
 Finally, the pricing. The associate collections are priced lower to better fit some couples’ budgets.
What is a Just-Married session? 
This is a new perk we just started offering and it’s designed to let you fully embrace your wedding day documentary experience, knowing you’ll have dedicated time for newlywed portraits afterward. Scheduled within 30 days of your wedding, this 2–3 hour session takes place in a beautiful outdoor mountain location. 
Can you hold a date for me? 
In order to hold your date we require a signed contract and a deposit to make it official! We accept bookings on a first come, first served basis!
How far in advance should we book? 
Couples typically book anywhere between 6-18 months in advance. Booking early not only guarantees availability but also provides peace of mind, allowing couples to focus on creating a seamless and stress-free experience. While this timeframe is common, we encourage you to reach out as soon as you have a date in mind, as popular dates and seasons tend to fill up quickly.
What is your payment schedule? 
Our payment structure is divided into three installments for your convenience:
Initial Deposit (30%) – A non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking to secure your date.
 Second Payment (35%) – The second installment, totaling 35% of the balance, will be due midway through the planning process.
 Final Payment (35%) – The remaining 35% balance will be due 30 days prior to your wedding date.
How long before I get my pictures? 
For our wedding photography packages, your final gallery will be delivered within 6 to 8 weeks following your wedding day. Elopement galleries have a slightly quicker turnaround, typically arriving within 5 to 7 weeks. For engagement sessions and portraits, you can expect your gallery within 3-4 weeks of your session.
All galleries are delivered digitally via an online platform, providing you with easy access to view, download, and share your professionally edited images.
