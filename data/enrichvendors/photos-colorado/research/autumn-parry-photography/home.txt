What no one tells you about your wedding day & WHY documentary style is the secret :  
 The truth is, most of your wedding day is completely out of your photographer’s control. Real moments don’t wait, and they can’t be staged and maintain real emotion. 
 You need a photographer who can read the room, spot meaningful moments as they happen, and capture them without missing a beat. 
 Not all photographers work this way.  Some photographers miss crucial moments and then step in to recreate them. And in doing so, the authenticity is lost. 
 We’ve seen too many couples spend hours away from their guests for portraits or be asked to recreate moments that should have been captured as they happened.  
 But that doesn’t have to be your experience because our documentary-style approach is different. 
 It’s about embracing the unpredictability, the spontaneous fun, the unexpected, and the in-between moments you didn’t even realize were happening, so you can stay in the moment. 
 We believe you work way too hard planning this incredible day, to feel like your wedding day has turned into a photoshoot. 
 With a storytelling-focused, documentary approach, you get the freedom to embrace the day you’ve been dreaming about, with wedding photos that embody the energy + emotion of your day. It&#8217;s the best of both worlds.

Why choose documentary style wedding photographers?
1.  Ditch mediocre photography
The wedding photos you&#8217;ve seen are all starting to blend together, but you&#8217;re looking for something beyond the ordinary. You&#8217;re looking for images that evoke emotion and fit your vision for the day you&#8217;ve been dreaming about. You want images that are just as one-of-a-kind as your wedding will be — capturing the raw, unscripted moments that documentary style wedding photography prioritizes so you can remember your day as it really was. 
2.  Choose moments over details
Your wedding day is about more than just perfect details — it’s about celebrating your love and reveling in the moments that give you butterflies. You want to let go of anything that doesn’t align with your vision and focus on being at the heart of your celebration. Prioritize authenticity over perfection, meaning over expectations, and make space for the joy and connection that will define your day. 
3.  Embrace experience, not perfection
You value your experience above all else. What truly matters are the people you care about and the memories you’re about to create together which is why you deserve the gift of being fully present with the people you love most. Embrace the fun, emotional and unexpected moments that are filled with meaning and depth. And because it&#8217;s so rare to have everyone gathered in one place, you need your day captured in a way that honors your experience so you can meet the moments that matter most.

 And you can trust that all of your most memorable moments will be documented. What is documentary style wedding photography?
Documentary style wedding photography honors your experience by taking a candid, photojournalistic approach to capturing your moments as they naturally unfold, free from the hassle of being constantly posed. By remaining unobtrusive and observant, we capture the intimacy and energy of your wedding day with minimal interference so you can stay IN the moment.
