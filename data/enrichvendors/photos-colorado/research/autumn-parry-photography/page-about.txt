Hey! We're Autumn & Griffin
We’re Autumn and Griffin, documentary-style wedding photographers (and former community photojournalists) on a mission to help couples like you have the best of both worlds: incredible wedding photos and an amazing experience. 
What we didn’t realize while planning our own wedding was that the most important parts of the day weren’t the details we had been meticulously planning. They were, in fact, the connection, the energy, and the unexpected moments, the things we could never plan for. 
And embracing those moments elevated our day in a way we could have never imagined. We realized not every wedding photographer shared the same perspective or approach, which made us realize that this was something couples desperately needed. 
We want this experience for you too, which is why we are passionate about our documentary style approach that honors your experience and keeps you in the moment, so you can fully embrace your day. 
With over 20 years of combined experience in the photography industry, 100+ weddings photographed, you can count on us to bring expertise, passion and creativity to every wedding we document. 
Let’s celebrate your love, with no regrets. 
xo Autumn + Griff

AUTUMN
Autumn grew up in a small Vermont town and studied photojournalism at RIT in Rochester, NY, where she met Griffin!
 From college classmates to photojournalist colleagues at the same newspaper in Virginia, they used to go head-to-head in photo competitions at school. Let’s just say that some “friendly” rivalry may… definitely still linger.
 Autumn brings the big-picture perspective and is the calm, organized force that keeps your wedding day running smoothly. She’s a master problem solver and communicator—especially when it comes to working with coordinators and vendors to keep everything on track.
 Incredibly empathetic and intuitive, she is very good at adapting to the energy around her. It’s this quiet superpower that makes navigating the natural ebb and flow of a wedding day a sixth sense.
 She&#8217;ll be your personal hype girl who genuinely cares about your experience and will make sure your day reflects what matters most to you.
