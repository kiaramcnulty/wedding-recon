Elopements & Micro-weddings 
Elopements and intimate weddings are all about the freedom to celebrate your love exactly as you imagine it. Why Elope in Colorado?
Epic Scenery: From alpine lakes and aspen groves to red‑rock canyons and sand dunes, the variety of landscapes here means every elopement feels like a once‑in‑a‑lifetime adventure.
Intimacy & Presence: With fewer guests and less traditional structure, you can focus solely on each other and the natural beauty around you.
Self‑Solemnization: Colorado is one of the only states in the U.S. that allows self-solemnization &mdash; meaning you can legally marry yourselves with no officiant, no witnesses, and no one else present if you don't want them. Just the two of you, your vows, and whatever landscape you choose. It's one of the reasons Colorado is such a special place to elope.
