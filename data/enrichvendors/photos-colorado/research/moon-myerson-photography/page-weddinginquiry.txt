Wedding Inquiry 
If you're considering me for your big day, the first step is to reach out and see if I am available on your date. I recommend doing this ASAP as I do tend to fill up. Here's how it works:
Three easy steps
ONE 
Let's get things started! Get in touch using the form below to see if I'm available. If I am, we'll connect and see if I'm a good match. Then we'll reserve your date with a contract and retainer.
TWO 
We'll do a pre-wedding consult so that I can learn all about you and your vision. I can offer timeline suggestions on the schedule or connect with your wedding planner, if you have one.
THREE 
On the day of your wedding, you can relax into the moment, I'll make sure to get the photos you want so you can savor everything later.
