HOW IT WORKS 
Simple, transparent, and designed to keep your wedding planning as stress-free as possible.
1 
Reach Out 
I respond within 24 hours &mdash; usually the same day. We'll set up a quick call to make sure we're a good fit before anything else. 
2 
Book your date 
Paperwork and a booking fee lock in your date. The balance is due one month before the wedding &mdash; simple and straightforward. 
3 
Pre-wedding consult 
A few weeks out, we'll walk through your day in detail. I'm also available anytime before then for questions or planning advice. 
4 
Your wedding day 
I blend in, stay alert, and capture everything &mdash; the moments you planned for and the ones you didn't see coming. 
5 
Gallery delivery 
Highlights within 2&ndash;4 weeks. Full gallery within 4&ndash;6 weeks. Every image backed up to the cloud and multiple drives from day one.
