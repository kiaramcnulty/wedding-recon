Our awesome, intimate engagement sessions are included in most wedding packages or can be purchased a la carte at a discount for wedding or elopement clients. Let us take you somewhere awesome and show you what its like to work with us before the wedding day. It's a great way for us get to know each other while we explore some amazing places. 
 We offer engagement sessions in our luxury photography studio as well as in all of the Four Corners Region, including Durango, Silverton, Telluride, Cortez, Moab, Aztec, Santa Fe, and beyond. We want to capture your unique relationship through your engagement photos. What do you love to do together? What are you favorite places to visit? Let’s put those ideas into a creative engagement photo session. Do you love to get dressed up and head to the Historic District of Durango? Ride the gondola in Telluride? Do you love to hike and camp in the Rocky Mountains? Perhaps you love to fish together at Vallecito Lake or have a favorite sports team you love to watch together. Maybe you want us to take you on a mountain adventure to find some waterfalls in the San Juan National Forest. Maybe you want us to plan it for you or you have your own ideas. How about exploring ancient ruins in Cortez? Finding hidden arches in the desert? We can do it all and can’t wait to plan your dream engagement session with you.
