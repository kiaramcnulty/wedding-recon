Hi, I’m Alexi Hubbell
 Based in Durango, Colorado, USA 
I am a photographer based in Durango, Colorado, USA. I specialize in capturing weddings, commercial projects, heashots and portraits. I aim to create beautiful, lasting images of life's special moments, drawing inspiration from the stunning landscapes around me and my passion for fine art. I work both on location and in my boutique portrait studio in Durango.
