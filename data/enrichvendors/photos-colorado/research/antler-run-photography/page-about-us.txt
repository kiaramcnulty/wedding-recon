About 
Hi, I'm Christine! Your Ride-or-Die Wedding Photographer...
Wedding days are beautiful, emotional, and fast. You plan for months, then suddenly you're in it, juggling timelines, family dynamics, and whether you've eaten anything besides adrenaline.
You shouldn't have to handle that alone.
I'm Christine, the photographer behind Antler Run Photography. Based just outside Denver, shooting all over Colorado, and fully invested in your day from the moment we start working together.
I show up prepared, present, and tuned in. I'll lead when you need me to and disappear into the background when you don't. I'll cry at your vows and I will not leave that dance floor.
What I'm actually chasing is the stuff that passes too fast to notice. The in-betweens, the reactions, the moments you don't fully see until you're looking at photos weeks later. Not perfection. Just the truth of how your day actually felt.
Documentary at heart, with a little direction when it helps. Real moments, true color, nothing forced.
Off the clock I'm pretty simple. Fourteen years married to my best friend Mike, completely obsessed with our dogs Angus and August, and usually found with a book or something on the stove. I have a wicked sarcastic streak, a playlist that goes from Danzig to John Denver with zero explanation, and I will absolutely be in the middle of your wedding day moshpit if Blink-182 comes on. Also tacos. Always tacos.
The way I show up for my people is exactly the way I show up for you. Fully, every time. If you love hard, value the humans in the room with you, and want photos that still wreck you twenty years from now, we're going to get along just fine.
