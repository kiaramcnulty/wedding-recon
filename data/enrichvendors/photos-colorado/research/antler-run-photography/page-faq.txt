Frequently Asked Questions
What to Expect When You Work With Me
Your wedding day will unfold like a beautiful blur of moments. The quiet anticipation, the spontaneous embraces, the joyful tears, the organized chaos, the pure celebration—my purpose is to capture it all with artistry and intention. These are the questions couples ask most often, and I'm here to make everything feel clear and effortless.
How did Antler Run Photography get its name? 
Photography has been my passion for almost as long as I can remember. But it was wildlife photography that started it all. 
My husband Mike and I spent countless weekends exploring Colorado's wilderness, cameras in hand, chasing the kind of shots that require patience, instinct, and a whole lot of waiting. Friends and family started noticing the work. Portrait requests followed. 
Then a dear friend asked me to photograph her wedding. I hesitated. "I'm a wildlife photographer," I told her. She laughed and said in her Russian accent, "Your moose are beautiful... please shoot my wedding!" 
So I did. And I never looked back. 
Turns out the same instincts that make a great wildlife photographer make a great wedding photographer. You learn to anticipate the moment before it happens, to stay ready, to never miss what's unfolding right in front of you. 
Antler Run Photography is a nod to where this all began... the Colorado wilderness, the wildlife, and the wild beauty that still inspires every frame I capture. It's why every wedding collection is named after Colorado's native wildlife. A little reminder of how we got here. 
How does booking work? 
Reach out through the contact form and I'll get back to you within 24 hours. From there we'll set up a call or grab coffee — whatever works for you. We'll talk through your day, find the right collection, and make sure we're a good fit. Once you're ready to move forward, a signed contract and 20% retainer locks in your date and you're officially on my calendar. 
How's that?
What's included in each package? 
Each collection is carefully built to give you everything you need for your day — two photographers, a hand-edited gallery, a heirloom album, and more. Full details on every collection are on our Wedding Collections page.
How many images will I receive? 
Every collection comes with a guaranteed minimum — because you should know exactly what you're getting. But honestly, I've never stopped at the minimum. I deliver every image that tells your story, minus duplicates and the ones where someone blinked. You'll never feel shortchanged... and you'll never get a folder full of filler either. Quality over quantity, every time.
What's your delivery timeline? 
Your sneak peek collection arrives within 72 hours of your celebration. Complete galleries are delivered within 8–10 weeks, as each image is personally hand-edited to ensure consistency, beauty, and authentic storytelling. No shortcuts—only care and craftsmanship.
Do you offer videography? 
Videography isn't something I offer directly — and that's intentional. Photography is my craft and I want to give it my full attention on your wedding day. That said, I work alongside a trusted team of Colorado videographers and I'm happy to make an introduction. Just mention it when you reach out.
Do you travel for weddings? 
Absolutely. Based near Denver, I photograph celebrations throughout Colorado and beyond. Share where your love story is unfolding, and I'll discuss any travel arrangements needed for your destination.
I feel awkward in photographs. Can you help? 
You're in wonderful company—most couples express this concern, then discover how enjoyable the experience becomes. I provide gentle direction when helpful while creating space for authentic moments to unfold naturally. And if your beloved pet is joining the celebration? I'm already enchanted. Extra points for formal accessories.
Are you licensed and insured? 
Completely. I maintain full licensing and insurance, plus comprehensive backup equipment—multiple camera bodies, lenses, memory cards, batteries, and complete backup systems. I also have trusted photographer colleagues available for true emergencies, though I've never missed a celebration. Your peace of mind is paramount. I am happy to provide a certificate [COI] to your venue
How do we make this official? 
Once we've had our consultation and you're ready to move forward, I'll send over a contract and your retainer invoice. Sign the contract, pay the retainer, and your date is locked in.
Let's Talk Money 
My wedding collections start at $4,500 and are built around giving you two photographers, a heirloom album, and images you'll love forever — no hidden fees, no surprise upsells. Full details on every collection are on the Wedding Collections page. 
A 20% retainer reserves your date, with the remaining balance due 30 days before your wedding. I accept Venmo, Zelle, and all major credit cards. Payment plans are available — just ask. 
Do You Photograph Other Events or Portraits? 
Absolutely. Most of my portrait and family clients are couples I first photographed on their wedding day... and I wouldn't have it any other way. I also photograph Quinceañeras, Bar and Bat Mitzvahs, communions, anniversaries, and other milestone celebrations. If you've got something worth documenting, let's talk.
