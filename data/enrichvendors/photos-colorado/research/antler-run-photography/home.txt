Welcome To ...
Antler Run Photography
Wedding & Portrait Photography in Colorado & Beyond
Because someday the memories will fade. The photos won't.
For couples who value art, legacy, and a damn good time. 
You're not looking for perfect. You're looking for real. 
Some couples spend their wedding day performing it. The best ones just live it.
You're here for your favorite people. For the moment your person sees you for the first time. For laughing too loud and crying happy tears and sneaking away for five minutes just to breathe each other in. You planned every detail and then let it all go the moment the music starts.
You're exactly who I'm here for.
I'm Christine, the photographer behind Antler Run Photography. Based just outside Denver, shooting love stories all over Colorado. I'm everywhere at once and somehow invisible... until you need me, and then I'm right there. I make nervous people laugh. I wrangle families without losing my mind. I stay until the last song.
Your guests will wonder what I'm doing. Your photos will answer that question.
This is your day. I'm just lucky enough to document it.
And it doesn't stop at the wedding. Some of my favorite humans started as wedding clients and just... never left. Watching them grow into families and showing up for all the chapters after... that's the reason I love what I do.
