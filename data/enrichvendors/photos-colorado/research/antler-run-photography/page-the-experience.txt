Let’s Start Your Story 
Before Your Wedding Day
Every great story starts with actually knowing each other. Before the camera comes out I take time to get to know you. Your energy, your priorities, what matters most when the day starts moving fast. Coffee, a video call, whatever works. We'll build a timeline that actually makes sense and keeps the day feeling easy, not rushed.
Engagement sessions are one of my favorite ways to kick things off. Low pressure, lots of laughs, and by the time your wedding day arrives you're already walking in confident. We've got this before we even start.
Ride or die isn't something I turn on for your wedding day. It's how I work from the beginning. Timeline planning, vendor recommendations, logistics, and all the random "is this normal?" questions that pop up at 11pm while you're deep in a planning spiral. You're never on your own and you're never bothering me.
By the time your wedding day arrives we're already locked in. I know what matters to you, how you move together, and how to keep things flowing without killing the vibe. I'll step in when you need direction, handle the behind the scenes chaos, and disappear when a moment just needs to happen.
No stiff posing. No forced smiles. No performative Pinterest nonsense. Just your day, exactly as it felt, captured by someone who was fully in your corner from the start.
After Your Wedding Day
When the last song fades and you're off living your next chapter, I get to work. Every image is hand edited with true color, real emotion, and longevity in mind. Photos that still feel like you twenty years from now.
Your gallery lands in a clean online space where you can download, share, and print with ease. And if you want to take it further, additional album sizes, fine art prints, and film options are available. Nothing cookie cutter, nothing you don't actually want.

Why Choose Us
Here's Why Our Clients Choose Us
Peace of mind comes standard. 
Two photographers. Professional cameras with redundant memory card slots. Your images are backed up to three separate locations before a single edit is made. Full professional insurance, with certificates available for your venue upon request.
In ten years I haven't missed a single wedding. I don't plan to start with yours. But if the unthinkable ever happened, a member of my trained team would step in seamlessly or you would receive a full refund. No exceptions.
