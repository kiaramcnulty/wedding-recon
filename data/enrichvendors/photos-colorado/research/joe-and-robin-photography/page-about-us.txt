We are husband + wife photographers that provide proposal to wedding photography for adventurous couples using a vibrant style of the natural you . 
Our Journey Here 
We both grew up loving photography and began developing our skills in high school.  Robin began shooting weddings in 2008 after studying art for 6 years. Understanding how to position the body and editing are just some of the ways she enjoys using her art background.  Joe began photography shooting on film and developing prints in a dark room.  He joined the photography business after marriage. His technical background in theater lighting brings a creative approach to lighting. The end result is you get images with the Colorado background and the ability to get photos in any lighting condition.  We began shooting all types of photography but quickly limited ourselves to focus on our passion for weddings, engagements, and proposals! 
Our Take on Weddings 
• A connection between you and your photographer will ensure more authentic and relaxed photos. • A couple should be able be natural and have a fun in front of the camera. • Starting the day off with a coffee in hand is the perfect way to begin a wedding day. • Having all your friends and family together on your wedding is a memory cherished forever. • Your wedding should reflect your personality • Images are the main investment that will help you relive your wedding experience. • Your dog should be invited to your engagement shoot and wedding.
