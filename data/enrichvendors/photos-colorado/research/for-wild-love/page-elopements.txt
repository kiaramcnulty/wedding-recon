Wild Lovers Elopement Package 
Dreaming of an adventurous and unforgettable elopement day? This package is designed for couples who want a stress-free, magical experience, whether it’s just the two of you or an intimate celebration with up to 25 guests . Available worldwide, this package includes full planning, design, and day-of coordination to make your special day as seamless as it is extraordinary. 
 Cost: Starting at $4,000 
WHAT'S INCLUDED 
Full design and styling for your ceremony & reception, including a personalized mood board
Location scouting & permit research
Vendor recommendations & management
Vendor contract review & booking assistance
Budget estimates & tracking with vendor payment reminders
Creation of wedding day timeline distributed to all vendors at least 2 weeks prior to elopement day
Day-of coordination including setup & breakdown of personal decor for the ceremony & reception
Monthly video calls to curate your day with unlimited communication by phone & email
Use of planners wedding emergency kit
Bouquet(s) & Boutonnieres(s) *limited to 2
