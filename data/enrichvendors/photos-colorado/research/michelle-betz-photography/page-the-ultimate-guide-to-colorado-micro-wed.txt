Ultimate Guide to Colorado Micro Weddings: 
Here are 30+ stunning micro wedding and elopement venues in Colorado that will make your special day unforgettable.
Airbnb & Bed and Breakfast Micro Wedding Venues: 
Dunton Hot Springs (Dolores, CO) &#8211; A luxury ghost town with natural hot springs and chic cabins.
The Little Nell (Aspen, CO) &#8211; A five-star resort with intimate settings and stunning alpine views.
Blackstone Rivers Ranch (Idaho Springs, CO) &#8211; A creekside retreat with a cozy mountain lodge.
Cliff House Lodge (Morrison, CO) &#8211; A hidden gem with private cottages and Red Rocks Amphitheatre nearby.
Beaumont Hotel & Spa (Ouray, CO) &#8211; Victorian elegance in the heart of the San Juan Mountains.
Strawberry Creek Ranch (Granby, CO) &#8211; A barn with mountain and meadow views.
The St. Vrain (Longmont, CO) &#8211; An industrial-chic venue with a cozy, intimate vibe.
Joyful Journey Hot Springs (Moffat, CO) &#8211; A peaceful retreat with hot springs and desert views.
Mountain Goat Lodge (Salida, CO) &#8211; A charming B&B with alpacas and stunning Sangre de Cristo Mountain views.
Holden House 1902 Bed & Breakfast (Colorado Springs, CO) &#8211; Victorian charm with romantic suites.
Restaurant Wedding Venues with Mountain Views: 
Flagstaff House (Boulder, CO) &#8211; Fine dining with panoramic views of the Flatirons.
Wines of Colorado (Cascade, CO) &#8211; A restaurant that rests by Pikes Peak, and has a forest and river that runs through the balcony.
Pepper Tree (Colorado Springs, CO) &#8211; A romantic setting with city and mountain views.
Beano’s Cabin (Avon, CO) &#8211; A mountain lodge accessible by sleigh ride in the winter.
Game Creek Restaurant (Vail, CO) &#8211; A European-style chalet with dramatic views.
Allred’s (Telluride, CO) &#8211; A mountain-top restaurant with sunset views.
The Broadmoor’s Golden Bee (Colorado Springs, CO) &#8211; A lively, intimate pub with historic charm.
Juniper Mountain House (Evergreen, CO) &#8211; A boutique inn with a terrace and dreamy views.
Phantom Canyon Brewing Co (Colorado Springs, CO) &#8211;  This restaurant has elegant and big windowed event spaces on the third floor for your reception.
Epic Outdoor Ceremony Sites & Lodge Wedding Venues 
Sunrise Amphitheater (Boulder, CO) &#8211; An open-air stone amphitheater overlooking the city.
Edgewood Inn (Divide, CO) &#8211; A Gorgeous lodge and intimate ceremony/reception site with mountain views. &#8211; (Recommended the most for your Colorado micro wedding)
Garden of the Gods Resort (Colorado Springs, CO) &#8211; Unparalleled views of red rock formations.
The Lodge at Breckenridge (Breckenridge, CO) &#8211; A rustic-chic lodge with sweeping mountain views.
Grand Lodge on Peak 7 (Breckenridge, CO) &#8211; This gorgeous ski in/ski out resort offers amazing hot tub views. The resort also has a restaurant with a large balcony that overlooks the Rockys.
Piney River Ranch (Vail, CO) &#8211; A lakeside gem surrounded by mountains.
Sapphire Point (Dillon, CO) &#8211; A scenic overlook perfect for tiny weddings and elopements.
Lost Gulch Overlook (Boulder, CO) &#8211; An adventurous mountaintop spot with unbeatable views.
Rocky Mountain National Park (Estes Park, CO) &#8211; Endless breathtaking backdrops for a true wilderness wedding.
Mount Princeton Hot Springs Resort (Nathrop, CO) &#8211; A mountain resort with natural pools.
Tennessee Pass Cookhouse (Leadville, CO) &#8211; A backcountry dining experience with alpine scenery.
Blue Lake Ranch (Hesperus, CO) &#8211; A lush retreat with flower gardens and mountain views.
San Sophia Overlook (Telluride, CO) &#8211; One of the most dramatic ceremony locations in Colorado.
Evergreen Lake House (Evergreen, CO) &#8211; A log cabin venue right on the water.
Garden of the Gods (Colorado Springs, CO) &#8211; Towering Red Rocks, with the Rocky mountains in the background.
