BEHIND THE CAMERA 
Hi, I'M HANNAH 
I am here to capture the moments that disappear before you blink. Those moments that take your breath away. I strive to give you photos with true and real colors that not only tell a story but also make you feel something. I want you to look a photo and hear the laughter of your child and to feel the love and passion between two people that just promised to write the next chapter of their lives together. Some people might just think I'm taking a photo but I'm really capturing your life as it is right now. Sometimes it might be chaotic and crazy and a straight up mess but that's okay. It's a season. 
Document it. Cherish it. Enjoy it. I have learned life isn't going to last forever but the memories will and I'm here to make sure they do.
