HEY Y'ALL, I'M HANNAH.
I LOVE WHAT I DO. CAPTURING MOMENTS THAT ONLY HAPPEN ONCE IN A LIFETIME ARE SO IMPORTANT TO ME. I AM OBSESSED WITH THE LOVE TWO PEOPLE SHARE TOGETHER AND I WANT TO MAKE SURE THAT IT IS REMEMBERED FOREVER. I LOVE WATCHING CHILDREN AND THEIR FAMILIES GROW UP RIGHT IN FRONT OF MY LENS. I AM SO LUCKY TO MEET AND WORK WITH SUCH AMAZING PEOPLE AND AM SO THANKFUL THAT THEY LET ME BE JUST A SMALL PART OF THEIR LIVES. TELLING YOUR STORY IS MY PASSION.
I GREW UP IN KANSAS ON A FARM AND SPENT A LOT OF TIME HANGING OUT WITH MY CHICKENS AND COWS. I LOVE GOING BACK HOME AND ENJOY SPENDING TIME IN THE TRACTOR. I LIVED IN NEBRASKA FOR SIX YEARS AND NOW RESIDE IN COLORADO SPRINGS. I CURRENTLY HAVE 2 DUCKS AND 5 CHICKENS AND 2 DOGS.
I HAVE TWO BOYS, HUNTER (14) AND WYATT (2), WHO ARE SO FUNNY. I ALSO HAVE A LITTLE BROWN COW NAMED MOCHA. SHE ISN'T REALLY A COW OR THAT LITTLE ACTUALLY, BUT A CHOCOLATE LAB MIX. WE ALL LOVE TO HIKE AND BE OUTSIDE AS MUCH AS POSSIBLE! MY SON AND I LOVE TO DO THE INCLINE AND MOCHA LOVES TO HIKE 14ERS AND SHE IS FORCED TO RUN MILES WITH ME ON THE TRAILS. I ALSO HAVE ANOTHER DOG LEXI, WHO IS A BLUE HEELER AND COULD PLAY FETCH ALLLLL DAY LONG. MY HUSBAND BLAKE, WHO YOU SOMETIMES WILL SEE SECOND SHOOTING WEDDINGS WITH ME, LOVES TO CHAT AND WILL BASICALLY BE ANOTHER GUEST AT YOUR WEDDING. TOGETHER WE OWN HIGH ALPINE TRANSPORTATION. IF YOU ARE NEEDING ANY TRANSPORTATION FOR YOUR WEDDING, BIRTHDAY PARTIES, CORPORATE EVENTS, ETC. DEFINITELY REACH OUT! 
I TALK ALL THE TIME AND IF WE'VE NEVER MET BEFORE OUR SESSION I WILL PROBABLY TALK THE ENTIRE TIME. I WILL MOST LIKELY BE AWKWARD BECAUSE I JUST RAMBLE ON AND ON AND ON, BUT I PROMISE YOU BY THE END OF THE SESSION, WE WILL BE BEST FRIENDS. PSA: I LAUGH AT EVERYTHING.
MY UNCLE, GRANDPA AND DAD ALL PASSED AWAY WITHIN THREE YEARS OF EACH OTHER. THE PHOTOS I HAVE WITH THEM MEAN MORE TO ME THAN I COULD HAVE EVER IMAGINED. I HOLD THOSE PHOTOS AND MEMORIES SO CLOSE TO MY HEART AND THAT IS WHAT I WANT TO GIVE YOU.
LIFE IS SO SHORT. TOMORROW ISN'T PROMISED AND WE SHOULD ALL FOCUS ON TODAY. I WANT TO CAPTURE YOUR LIFE AS IT IS. I WANT YOU TO REMEMBER IT EXACTLY HOW IT IS TODAY, BECAUSE IT WILL BE COMPLETELY DIFFERENT TOMORROW.
I GOT ENGAGED TO MY BEST FRIEND ON TOP OF A 14ER AND AND WE SAID WE'D ADVENTURE TOGETHER FOR THE REST OF OUR LIVES AT TWIN LAKES BY LEADVILLE, COLORADO. IT WAS A SMALL WEDDING WITH OUR FAVORITE PEOPLE AND IT WAS THE BEST TIME. NOW THAT MY WEDDING PLANNING IS OVER, I LOVE TALKING ALL THE DETAILS WITH MY COUPLES AND WOULD LOVE LOVE LOVE TO HELP PLAN YOUR DAY!
"I LIVE FOR THOSE MOMENTS THAT DISAPPEAR BEFORE YOU BLINK. THOSE MOMENTS YOUR HEART CAN'T LET GO OF. THOSE MOMENTS THAT MAKE YOU FEEL ALIVE. THOSE MOMENTS THAT JUST TAKE YOUR BREATH AWAY."
