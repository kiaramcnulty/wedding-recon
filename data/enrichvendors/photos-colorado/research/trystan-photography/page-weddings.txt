Things to Consider About Us
What is our vibe/energy at Trystan Photography? We are fun, goofy, high-energy photographers. We take very serious photography in very not-serious atmospheres. We love to laugh and goof around with our couples. 
We are not &#8220;one-size-fits-all&#8221; photographers. There is a type of bride and/or groom we absolutely do not work well with, and we should not photograph their weddings. What is that type? The type of person that if there is a bow out of place on a flower girl&#8217;s dress, &#8220;&#8230;someone&#8217;s head is going to roll!&#8221;. Yes, the &#8220;Type-A&#8221; persona. The &#8220;bridezilla&#8221;.
Why? They never relax. Their guards never come down, and they don&#8217;t laugh and have fun with their photography. A fun, relaxed openness is critical to how we work, and the kinds of images we capture. Type-A &#8220;bridezillas&#8221; need a very serious, High-Fashion style photographer. We are not right for them. At all.
However, if you and your fiance are fun, easy-going people (who may not even be comfortable in front of a camera), but love to laugh and play, you might be the perfect couple for us to photograph.
Weddings in Colorado: One moment in time, one shot at this, no do-overs&#8230;
Colorado weddings are a thrill to photograph. The weather can be temperamental and surprising at times, and the winds can make any hairstyle a challenge. The best part? Every wedding in is unique. The details, the love story, the venue, the natural beauty, the celebration&#8230; all different and deserving of the utmost care and artistic sensibility &#8230; and don&#8217;t forget joy, laughter, and skill.
We love our couples, and we are so honored that we were picked to be such an important part of so many weddings. 18 years into this awesome ride, and it still is just as thrilling and fun to be Colorado wedding photographers as the day we started.
Thank you for considering us for your wedding!
