Frequently Asked Questions About Our Portraits
What location will my session be at?
I have many fun and beautiful locations to shoot at all over Colorado Springs! I love to collaborate with all of my clients and pick the perfect spot for your family, couple or individual photo shoot. We also have access to our studio if indoor photography is a preference. 
Are the digital image files watermarked?
No, the digital image files that come with your portrait package are non-watermarked and full resolution. They are the same final digital negatives we use.
What is your booking policy?
We require a 1/2 deposit with your signed contract to book your date. The remaining 1/2 is due on the day of the portrait session. All installments are non-refundable in event of your cancellation.
