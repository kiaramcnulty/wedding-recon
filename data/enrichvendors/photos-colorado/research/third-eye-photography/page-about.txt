When I was a kid, as soon as I saw that massive dragon on the edge of town I always felt right at home. After graduating from Syracuse University with a degree in photojournalism , I moved to Crested Butte led by the intuition to call this place my own. The plan was to be a ski bum for a bit and then grow up. Turns out I fell in love with more than just this small, quaint, funky town that has an inescapably special place in my heart. I am so lucky to be married and raising my family here , with a career doing what I love. With Third Eye I combine two of my favorite things - my passion is being creative, and I love working with all kinds of amazing people. 
I have had 4 black lab dogs since I have moved to Crested Butte, 3 at one time, and we just adopted a new puppy!
