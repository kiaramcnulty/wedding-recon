Meet Francis
Austin Francis Sylvest is a wedding photographer and videographer based in Colorado with over 10 years of professional experience.
His style evokes those warm, lived moments that celebrating the beauty in the ordinary, emphasizing real moments over staged ones, garnering a true to life feel.
With a strong film focus, he shoots both 35mm and medium format photography mixed with digital mediums. In addition, he shoots 16mm and super 8 cinematography complimented by digital mediums.
He specializes in weddings in Colorado in Aspen , Telluride , Vail , Denver , Boulder and Steamboat Springs . In addition, he has a strong rapport with destinations such as New Orleans , New York City & Iceland .
