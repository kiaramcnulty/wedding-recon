Francis Sylvest
Austin Francis Sylvest is a film wedding photographer and filmmaker based in Colorado by way of New Orleans. His images evoke those warm, lived moments. Yet, they also capture his subjects in a way that best demonstrates his journalistic background and love of film photography and indie cinema.
With nine years of professional experience, Austin photographs wedding celebrations that include large multi-day events to elopements across the globe, with an emphasis in Colorado, Iceland, and New Orleans. His work garners a true to life, yet poetic take on the world that surrounds us.
