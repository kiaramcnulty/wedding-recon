Laid-Back, Elegant & True To Life Wedding Photography and Videography
I&#8217;m Austin Francis Sylvest, an Aspen wedding photographer and videographer based in Colorado with over 10 years of professional experience. My style evokes those warm, lived moments that celebrating the beauty in the ordinary, emphasizing real moments over staged ones, garnering a true to life feel.
Notably, I specialize in shooting both 35mm and medium format photography mixed with digital mediums. In addition, I shoot 16mm and super 8 videography complimented by digital formats. I specialize in weddings in Colorado in Aspen , Telluride , Vail , Denver , Boulder and Steamboat Springs . In addition, I have a strong rapport with destinations such as New Orleans , New York City & Iceland .
