An Elegant, Laid-Back & True To Life Experience
I&#8217;m Austin Francis Sylvest, a Colorado-based wedding videographer and photographer with a true to life, documentary style, with over 10 years of professional experience and a love of analog mediums. My style evokes those warm, lived moments that celebrating the beauty in the ordinary, emphasizing real moments over staged ones, garnering a true to life feel.
Notably, I specialize in shooting videography on 16mm and super 8 film in addition to digital mediums. In addition, I also specialize in film wedding photography shot on 35mm and medium format film mixed with digital mediums.
I also specialize in weddings in Colorado in Aspen , Telluride , Vail , Denver , Boulder and Steamboat Springs . In addition, I have a strong rapport with destinations such as New Orleans , New York City , Brooklyn , Manhattan & Iceland .
