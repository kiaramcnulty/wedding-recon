Greystone Castle Wedding - Boulder Colorado Wedding
Wedding 
Dec 13 
Written By Mallory Munson 
One of my favorite wedding venues is in Boulder, Colorado. The lovely Greystone Castle opened its doors to weddings a few years back. I was lucky enough to capture the owner’s daughter’s wedding there and the rest is history. I fell in love with the family and now have the honor of capturing more weddings here.
 Susie & Chris your wedding day was full of love, a bit of rain and many rainbows! If you know me, my obsession with rainbows is intense so to say I was excited is an understatement.
 Always love working with some of my favorite in the business, wedding planners Table 6 Productions and florist Kimball Floral and dress by Emma and Grace Bridal and the amazing band Wash Park Band and Colorado Party Rentals .
 P.S. I love when couples ask to take a photo with me, scroll to see my joy :))))
Mallory Munson
