The Broadmoor Wedding - Fall - Winter - Colorado Springs Wedding
Wedding 
Dec 28 
Written By Mallory Munson 
Capturing a wedding at The Broadmoor in Colorado Springs is special. This place is a museum of beautiful corridors, a library, artwork, stunning architecture and every backdrop you are in awe. The staff is incredible and it feels like home, but on a grand scale &lt;3
 On Alex & Jacob’s wedding day it started with sunshine and by the time the ceremony rolled around it was a full on blizzard. It was gorgeous and memorable and worth the frost bite ;)
 Always a joy working with Table 6 Productions . The reception had one of the best vibes of the year brough to you by The Walton Stout Band all the way from Dallas, TX. Hair and makeup by my favorite Divine Beauty Artists . Bridesmaids dress by Azazie and flowers by The Olive and Poppy !
Mallory Munson
