 500+ WEDDINGS 
 approximately 789 slices of cake…but who is counting? 
I am easily excited by the little things. Good food, someones perfume, colorful sunsets. If I like something I am not shy to share my feelings. 
 I love humans, like truly think we are so cute. 
 Other peoples joy feels as if it is my own. I love to watch lovers hug at airport arrivals and listen to people laugh uncontrollably. 
 I sing when I am happy! 
 Every year I start a new creative project, a few years back I learned the ukulele and last year I wrote a children’s book for highly sensitive children. 
After my first love turned into a divorce, I promised myself I would see the world - solo. I started with Australia , spent months in Asia and finally made it to Europe .
 I spent the last 5 years living part time in Germany and now live part time in Bali in my dream villa.
 I get goosebumps alllll the time. I feel so deeply.
 I would love to be reborn into a rockstar, singing my heart out on stage.
 I was born in Minnesota, grew up in Arizona and moved to Colorado in 2014 with a dream to start this very wedding photography business!
