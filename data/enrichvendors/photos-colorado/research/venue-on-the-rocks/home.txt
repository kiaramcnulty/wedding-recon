About Us 
Born from the local favorite, Coffee on the Rocks , Venue on the Rocks was created to share the same warmth, beauty, and sense of connection that our guests have always loved. What began as a cozy gathering place has grown into a venue made for life’s most meaningful moments, 
 from your morning coffee to saying “I do.” 
 We invite you to experience the beauty of Estes Park in a space that feels like your own mountain hideaway, with a team that’s here for you every step of the way .
