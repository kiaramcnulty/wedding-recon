The Heart Behind 
Venue on the Rocks
Family-owned and operated, 
we’ve created a place where every moment 
feels unforgettable .
From quality coffee and handcrafted cocktails to our duck pond and the peaceful flow of the Big Thompson River, everything we do is rooted in one simple belief: people deserve a space that helps them slow down, connect, and celebrate the moments that matter. 
 Our story began in 2007 with Coffee on the Rocks . We wanted to build a cozy spot where locals and visitors could enjoy a warm drink, watch the wildlife wander by, and take in the beauty of Estes Park. Coffee on the Rocks became a gathering place, a pause in the day, and a reminder of how special it is to enjoy the mountains with loved ones. 
 This space quickly grew just as dear to our visitors’ hearts as it did our own. People kept asking if they could host events here to celebrate life’s biggest moments. In 2022, we made it happen. Venue on the Rocks was born to celebrate with warmth, ease, and natural beauty that turns celebrations into unforgettable memories .
 No matter what you’re celebrating with us, we look forward to helping you feel calm, cared for, and completely ready to enjoy your day.
