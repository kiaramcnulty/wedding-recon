Welcome to
Venue on the Rocks
Nestled next to the Big Thompson River and framed by the Rocky Mountains, our peaceful hideaway invites you to slow down, breathe in the beauty around you, and celebrate the first day of your forever. 
 Here, you’ll find flexible packages that fit your vision and budget, a charming duck pond for an extra hint of whimsy (and a few feathered guests) , and a family-run team that pours our heart into every detail. From the moment you arrive, we’re here for you . We’re excited to listen to your vision and eager to create a wedding that feels unmistakably you.
 We can’t wait to share our space with you and welcome you into the Venue on the Rocks family.
How We Bring 
Your Vision to Life
We believe your wedding should feel personal, warm, and memorable . And while we’ll happily keep your guests cozy with fire pits and space heaters, the warmth we care about most is the kind that comes from your love story and the people you’re celebrating with .
 With no required vendor list, you can choose the team that fits your style and your budget, and we’ll work seamlessly alongside them. From intimate micro weddings to reception celebrations of 200 guests, we’re here to help you shape your day without stressing you out.
 Our team is with you every step of the way: 
 Offering guidance when you’re unsure where to start
Helping you personalize the flow so the day feels simple and enjoyable
Coordinating with your vendors so everything comes together smoothly
Supporting your vision from start to finish

Where Your Day Unfolds
Your Riverside “I Do”
 There’s no better way to say “I do” than in front of the river with the mountains rising behind you. Our ceremony space sits right along the Big Thompson River, offering a peaceful, breathtaking setting that feels truly made for moments like these.
 With room for up to 200 guests , your loved ones can gather comfortably as you walk down the aisle surrounded by nature’s charm. And once the ceremony ends, guests don’t have to travel far or wait around, as the reception area is just steps away. This seamless flow keeps your day feeling natural, easy, and exactly how it should be: focused on celebration, connection, and enjoying every moment together. 
 Celebrate Into the Evening
 Just steps from the ceremony site, our covered pavilion welcomes your guests into an atmosphere that feels both charming and effortlessly relaxed. Twinkling string lights cast a warm glow over the space, while cozy firepits keep your guests cozy as they gather, laugh, and celebrate your special day. It’s a setting that feels intimate, even with a full celebration, offering the perfect blend of comfort and mountain charm.
 With space for up to 200 guests , the pavilion is designed for everything you want your reception to be, from dining, dancing, and heartfelt toasts. This space is ready to bring your celebration to life , whether you imagine an elegant dinner, a lively dance floor, or simply a joy-filled evening surrounded by the people who matter most.
 Packages Made for Every Love Story
 Every couple arrives with a different vision, a different budget, and a different way they want their day to unfold, and we believe each one should be unforgettable . That’s why our wedding packages are designed with flexibility in mind, giving you the freedom to choose a celebration that feels just right for you, your guests, and the story you’re beginning together.
 Are you dreaming of a quiet morning, “I do?” Or a full day, lively celebration? Whatever you’re looking for, from micro weddings to big parties, you’ll find an option that fits your day, your rhythm, and your forever.
