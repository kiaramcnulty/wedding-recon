Wedding Planning
You said yes. It’s a moment full of joy, and the only thing that could possibly be more exciting is your wedding day. But first, you need to plan the event, and that can feel overwhelming, daunting, or just downright frustrating. 
 We’ve seen couples who were more than ready to be married, but less excited about the wedding. And we believe the first day of your forever should be memorable for all the right reasons.
 That’s where our wedding planning services come in. You found Venue on the Rocks because you wanted the serenity of the space. Let us help you feel that same peace from start to finish. 
 Our wedding planning services are flexible; we can step in as much or as little as you want to build your dream day, coordinate with vendors, and solve problems on your special day before you ever even hear about them.
 We listen to what you want and your budget, and guide you through all the small details so nothing slips through the cracks.
 Our planning services are designed to: 
 Save you time and unnecessary stress
Keep your vision clear and your decisions confident
Create a seamless experience for you and your guests
Allow you to be fully present on your wedding day
