HI, I&#8217;M GABBY!
I’m a trained yoga instructor, a certified emotive posing coach, and a lifelong student of embodiment. I founded Embodied Art Boudoir in 2020 with a vision of creating something deeper than a traditional boudoir photoshoot.
But more than the credentials: I’m the woman holding the container for your experience .
I guide you through intention setting. I coach you into authentic, natural expressions. I create a space where your nervous system can soften and you can feel free to just be .
Yes, I know how to pose every body (including yours) beautifully. I care deeply about the details and want you to feel profoundly supported. 
And we will probably laugh a lot! Because self-love should be fun . 
My mission is simple: to help women turn down the volume of their inner critic, not by forcing confidence, but by creating a space where they can see their magic clearly. 
Through a process grounded in intention, embodiment, guided support, and artistic expression, I help women come home to their bodies and anchor their truth in something they can see, feel, and hold.

Your Questions, Answered
Why should I choose Embodied Art Boudoir? 
Because this is more than a photoshoot.
It’s an intentional, guided experience designed to help you feel safe, seen, and fully expressed.
Every session includes:
• A grounding meditation to ease nerves and help you reconnect to your body.
• Emotionally guided posing that captures your personality, not just your angles.
• Professional hair & makeup and a full day dedicated entirely to you.
• A body-positive, trauma-informed approach where all bodies are celebrated.
• Intentional support & resources so this experience serves your growth long after the session ends.
And if you’re still unsure, our 5-star reviews speak for themselves.
Where is the boudoir photography studio? 
The studio is located in Golden, Colorado , just off Route 6 near Green Mountain, the Jeffco Fairgrounds, and the Colorado Mills Mall. 
You’ll enjoy free, covered parking and easy access without the hassle of downtown Denver traffic. 
We regularly serve clients from Denver, Lakewood, Arvada, Boulder, Colorado Springs, Summit County, Fort Collins, Wyoming, and beyond.
When should I book my session? 
Most clients book 3–8 months in advance, especially if they’d like to take advantage of flexible pre-session payment plans.
Artwork is typically ready within four weeks of your session. If you’re booking for a wedding, birthday, or milestone event, let us know when you inquire so we can ensure perfect timing.
What if I don&#8217;t know how to pose ? 
You don’t need to.
You’ll be guided every step of the way, not only in how to move your body, but how to express your authentic personality & emotion.
Your session is fully customized to your intention. You won’t be placed into generic poses. Instead, you’ll be coached into natural expression that feels like you, and be guided to explore new sides of yourself.
How much does it cost? 
The Embodied Art Experience begins at $2,900. This all-inclusive investment includes:
• Professional hair & makeup
• Intention setting and guided meditation
• Fully coached photoshoot experience
• Same-day image reveal and ordering session
• A handcrafted 8&#215;8 album featuring 20 images
• Support & guidance throughout this journey
A $400 deposit is paid at booking to secure your date.
After years of offering à la carte options, I realized most women didn’t want to piece together their experience, they wanted guidance. So I simplified everything into one intentional, all-inclusive offering designed to give you exactly what you need without overwhelm.
Optional upgrades & expanded artwork collections are available if you fall in love with more (which most women do).
Flexible, interest-free pre-session installment plans are available to make your investment manageable and aligned with your timeline.
Do you provide hair & makeup? 
Yes. A professional hair and makeup artist will be onsite when you arrive to ensure you look and feel your best.
Whether you prefer natural glam or something more dramatic, our stylists are experienced in working with all skin tones (we have products for all colors & conditions), hair textures (including curly and Black hair), ages (experienced with mature skin), and personal styles.
How long is the session? 
Plan for a full day dedicated to you.
You’ll arrive at 10am for professional hair & makeup, followed by intention setting and a grounding meditation.
Your photoshoot lasts approximately 90 minutes. After a lunch break, you’ll return for your same-day image viewing and ordering session.
Most clients leave by 4–5pm feeling grounded, celebrated, and seen.
Do my photos go online? 
Only with your explicit consent. Your privacy is respected at every step. You’ll decide whether or not your images are shared after viewing them during your same-day ordering session.
