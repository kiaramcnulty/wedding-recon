MY NAME IS JENNIFER AND I'M A WEDDING PHOTOGRAPHER SERVING TEXAS, COLORADO, AND BEYOND 
After graduating from Texas A&M, I moved straight from College Station to Colorado, where I spent 2021&ndash;2025 photographing weddings in some of the most stunning settings &mdash; from luxury hotels and mountain elopements to traditional and non-traditional celebrations. Now in my fourth year of shooting weddings, I've had the privilege of capturing a wide variety of love stories, each unique and full of genuine moments. I also got married in Colorado, which gave me an even deeper appreciation for the magic of a wedding day &mdash; from the quiet, intimate moments to the big, joyful celebrations. Those years in the Rockies shaped my style into something both authentic and adventurous, and I fell in love with the mountains, the light, and the incredible couples I got to work with. I hope to return to Colorado soon to shoot more weddings there, because it will always hold a special place in my heart. 
Now back in Dallas to be close to family, I love being part of wedding days, helping wherever I can, and crafting photographs that couples will treasure forever. When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me traveling, exploring the outdoors, or spending time with my husband and our dogs.

The Grand Tetons are my favorite National Park 
Big Bend is my favorite destination in Texas
My dream destination to shoot a wedding is Italy 
Documenting weddings and graduations is truly an honor for me. I grew up taking photos as a hobby, never imagining that one day I'd be capturing some of the most important moments in people's lives. 
My ultimate goal as a photographer is to go above and beyond for my clients&mdash;I want to build a genuine connection with you and capture your authentic personality. I know being in front of a camera can feel intimidating, but if you trust me and are willing to step out of your comfort zone, we can create something truly amazing together. 
Meeting a stranger for a photoshoot can feel a little nerve-wracking, but I promise I don't bite! I'll guide you every step of the way, making the experience fun, relaxed, and totally stress-free. So, if you're ready to take a risk, try something new, and come away with some seriously kick-ass photos, let's make it happen! 
- Jennifer Glenn
