FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS!
What’s included in your elopement packages? 
Answering your question with a question: what are you looking for for your elopement day?! We tailor each package for the couple we are working with to be what they want and nothing extra and nothing forgotten. Our most popular packages are photo & video with lots of planning help and are all day experiences! 
Do you help with planning or just show up and shoot? 
We help with it all! From scouting the perfect location to building your timeline, recommending vendors, and securing permits - we’re your planning partners and your creative team. 
 We believe the more you can just enjoy the experience and worry less about the logistics, the better!
How do you deliver the photos and videos in ONE WEEK? That's so fast! 
Our one-week delivery is often unbelievable compared to the multiple months you might have to wait from other elopement videographers/photographers but let me tell you this…
 We don’t rush to get it done quickly, we’re just experienced. 
 We’ve been making films and photos professionally for 10+ years. 
 In that time, we’ve made over 100 elopement films. 50+ commercials. 30+ documentaries. 200+ Youtube videos, 500+ shorts, and 200,000 photos.
 We’ve spent a lot of time developing our skills so we can deliver the best videos and photos we can, as fast as we can. 
 We love what we do and we don’t want you have to wait to share and relive your elopement experience. 
 You won’t find this guarantee anywhere else.
What if we’re awkward in front of the camera? 
This is totally normal so no need to worry. We keep things relaxed and natural, guiding you just enough when needed and documenting the rest as it unfolds. No forced and uncomfortable poses. No pressure. Just the two of you, being yourselves and enjoying your day.
How many elopements do you take per year? 
We only take on a limited number each season so every couple gets our full attention, creativity, and care!
 It usually averages out to a handful of elopements per month! 
 If you're thinking of working with us, reach out early to secure your date!
Can we bring our dog? Or a few close friends? 
Yes and yes! We love when elopements include the people (or pets) that matter most. 
 We specialize in intimate experiences - usually just the couple or up to five guests but are open to plan, film and photograph elopements for up to 25 people.
What’s the investment? 
That’s a great question! We love to go over pricing based on what you’re looking for on our first call! If you’re interested in learning more about our pricing or working with us, we highly recommend reaching out so we can jump on a call to chat more and figure out what is best for you!
How do we book you? 
Reach out through the contact form and we’ll set up a casual call to hear more about your day. 
 If it’s a good fit, we’ll send over a simple online contract and retainer invoice to lock in your date.
