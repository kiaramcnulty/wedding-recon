FAQ
Will there be a travel fee? Travel fees are only assessed to venues more than 30 miles from Denver.  To get that mileage, we use pin location “Denver, CO” on google maps and calculate mileage from there.  This is how we calculate our  travel fee:  Total round-trip mileage from Denver: _________ &#8211; 60 miles = _______x $1.25 =______x number of photographers = ________.  Travel fee will not go below $100.
