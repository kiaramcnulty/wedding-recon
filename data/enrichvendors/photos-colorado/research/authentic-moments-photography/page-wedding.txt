See Your Day Anew
You’ll be deep in the moment on your wedding day—or tucked away somewhere—and that
 means you’ll miss a lot of it. The laughter from across the room. A stolen glance. The
 fall of a happy tear. You want someone who's out in the thick of it, capturing it
 all for you to relive and treasure later on.
That's why I strive to be both ubiquitous and invisible. I shoot quietly, from a
 distance, and with a sharp eye for timing. That way, I can preserve the real story
 unfolding around you—the one you weren't even aware of in the moment.

What to Expect
We’ll start with a short consult to get aligned on the details, and I’ll send over the
 booking paperwork. The week before the wedding, I’ll check in again to make sure
 nothing’s changed. On the big day, I’ll arrive early and do my best to be both
 ubiquitous and invisible.
The next day, you’ll get a handful of highlight shots to share. Two weeks later, I’ll
 send over your edited previews so you can choose favorites and request any edits. When
 everything feels just right, you’ll settle the balance and I’ll deliver your final
 gallery.
