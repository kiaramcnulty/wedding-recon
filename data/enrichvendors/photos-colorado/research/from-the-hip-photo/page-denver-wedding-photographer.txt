Popular Denver Wedding Venues
We&#8217;ve photographed weddings at hundreds of Colorado venues. Here are some of our favorites — and we&#8217;d love to add yours to the list.
Moss Denver 
An industrial-chic event space near RiNo with exposed brick, soaring ceilings, and huge windows that flood the space with natural light. Its urban courtyard and nearby street art create versatile backdrops for intimate to mid-size celebrations.
Spruce Mountain Ranch 
A stunning 300-acre ranch in Larkspur with rolling meadows, towering pines, and panoramic Front Range views. The elevated ceremony site and rustic barn reception space offer endless photo opportunities across varied mountain terrain.
Denver Botanic Gardens 
Our official photography partner venue spanning 24 acres of cultivated gardens and water features. From the Japanese Garden to the tropical conservatory, seasonal landscapes create unique and colorful backdrops year-round.
Denver Museum of Nature & Science 
An architecturally striking venue with sweeping views of City Park and the Denver skyline from its terrace. Grand interior spaces and dramatic natural light pair with panoramic outdoor vistas that are especially breathtaking at sunset.
Arrowhead Golf Club 
Nestled among ancient red rock formations in Littleton, this venue is dramatically and uniquely Colorado. Manicured fairways contrast with towering sandstone while the clubhouse frames the rocks through floor-to-ceiling windows.
Grant-Humphreys Mansion 
A 1902 Beaux-Arts mansion featuring a sweeping staircase, ornate woodwork, crystal chandeliers, and manicured grounds. Rich historic interiors and garden terraces with Denver skyline views make every angle feel elegant and timeless.

Wedding Photography FAQ
How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?
We recommend booking 8 to 12 months before your wedding date, especially for peak season (May through October) and popular Saturday dates. That said, we have five photographers on our team, so we can often accommodate shorter timelines. Reach out and we&#8217;ll check availability — you might be surprised.
Do you offer engagement sessions with wedding packages?
We do! Many couples add an engagement session to their wedding package. It&#8217;s a great way to get comfortable with your photographer before the big day, plus you&#8217;ll get amazing photos for save-the-dates and your wedding website. Ask us about bundled pricing when you book.
How many photos will we receive from our wedding?
It depends on the length of coverage, but as a rough guide: a 4-hour wedding typically yields 300-500+ images, 6 hours delivers 500-800+, and 8 hours with two photographers can produce 800-1,200+ images. Every single photo is individually retouched — we never dump unedited files on you.
Do you travel for destination weddings in Colorado?
We sure do. We&#8217;ve photographed weddings from Estes Park to Telluride, Aspen to the Great Sand Dunes, and everywhere in between. Travel within 45 minutes of our Denver studio is included in every package. For mountain venues and destination weddings farther out, we&#8217;ll provide a custom quote that covers travel — it&#8217;s usually very reasonable.
What happens if our outdoor wedding gets rained out?
Colorado weather keeps things interesting, and honestly, some of our most dramatic wedding photos have come from stormy skies and unexpected rain. We always work with your venue&#8217;s backup plan and adapt on the fly. Overcast light is actually gorgeous for portraits. If conditions are truly dangerous, we&#8217;ll work with you on next steps — but in 17+ years, we&#8217;ve never missed a wedding.
When will we receive our wedding photos?
Your full, professionally retouched gallery will be delivered within approximately four weeks of your wedding date. We&#8217;ll send you a private online gallery where you can view, download, and share every image. We know the wait feels long when you&#8217;re excited, but the individual editing on each photo is worth it.
Do you offer albums or prints?
Full printing rights are included with every package, so you&#8217;re free to print anywhere you&#8217;d like. Our Gallery system has print ordering fully integrated for you, your family, and guests. We offer every print style you could want and only partner with top-tier commercial photo labs for high-quality archival prints. We also provide custom wedding album design and printing, so you never have to do any additional work. The images are yours — no extra licensing fees, no restrictions, ever.
Ready to Book Your Denver Wedding Photographer?
Your wedding day happens once. Let&#8217;s make sure the photos are as incredible as the day itself. Our team would love to hear about your plans. And after the big day, we&#8217;re still your photo team &#8212; reach out whenever the next chapter calls for professional headshots .
