Popular Denver Engagement Photo Locations
We know every corner of Denver and the Front Range. Here are some of our all-time favorite spots for engagement sessions.
Lost Gulch Overlook, Boulder 
Dramatic mountain panoramas at 7,000 feet. Stunning at sunset with sweeping views of the Continental Divide. One of Colorado&#8217;s most iconic engagement backdrops.
RiNo Arts District, Denver 
Colorful murals, industrial architecture, and urban energy. Perfect for couples who want engagement photos with personality and a distinctly Denver vibe.
Sapphire Point, Dillon 
A short mountain trail leading to an overlook above Dillon Reservoir. Gorgeous year-round with wildflowers in summer and snow-dusted peaks in winter.
Sprague Lake, Estes Park 
Mirror-still alpine lake in Rocky Mountain National Park. The reflections of surrounding peaks create a fairy-tale setting for your photos.
Clear Creek, Golden 
Creek-side trails with footbridges, aspen groves, and mountain backdrops just 20 minutes from Denver. Great for couples who love a casual, outdoorsy feel.
Denver Botanic Gardens 
Lush gardens, Japanese-inspired landscapes, and architectural details. A refined setting for couples who want variety in a single, beautiful location.

Engagement Photography FAQ
When should we book our engagement session?
We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance, especially if you want a weekend slot during peak season (May through October). That said, we can often accommodate shorter timelines — just reach out and we&#8217;ll see what&#8217;s available. Many couples book their engagement session at the same time as their wedding photography.
What&#8217;s the best time of year for engagement photos in Denver?
Denver is gorgeous year-round! Fall (September–October) offers golden aspen leaves in the mountains. Summer brings wildflowers and long golden-hour evenings. Spring has blooming gardens and dramatic skies. Even winter is stunning — snowy mountain sessions are some of our most magical work. We&#8217;ll help you pick the perfect season for your vision.
What should we wear to our engagement session?
Wear something that makes you feel confident and like yourselves. Coordinate colors rather than matching exactly — complementary tones look great on camera. Avoid large logos or busy patterns. Layers add visual interest, especially in outdoor settings. If you book the 90-minute session, bring a second outfit for variety. We&#8217;re always happy to help with outfit planning!
Can we bring our dog to the session?
Absolutely — we love photographing couples with their pets! Dogs (and other furry friends) add personality and genuine moments to your gallery. We&#8217;ll plan the session so your pup can be part of some shots and then handed off to a friend for the rest. Some of our most popular engagement photos feature four-legged family members.
How many photos will we receive?
Every location and session is different, so the final count varies — but here are some good expectations. A one-hour session typically yields 50–80+ professionally retouched images, while a 90-minute session delivers 80–120+. Every image is individually edited — we don&#8217;t hand over unedited files. You&#8217;ll receive your full gallery within about two weeks via a private online link, ready to download and share.
Will you help us choose a location?
Definitely! Location scouting is one of our favorite parts. We&#8217;ve photographed engagement sessions at hundreds of spots across Denver, Boulder, the mountains, and beyond. We&#8217;ll suggest locations based on your style, the time of year, and the vibe you&#8217;re going for — whether that&#8217;s an urban Denver backdrop, a mountain overlook, or a cozy indoor setting.
What if it rains or snows on our session day?
Colorado weather is unpredictable — and often that works in our favor! Overcast skies create beautiful, even lighting, and a dusting of snow can make photos magical. If conditions are truly severe, we&#8217;ll reschedule at no extra charge. We always keep an eye on the forecast and will be in touch ahead of time with a plan.
Can we use our engagement photos for save-the-dates?
Yes! Full printing rights are included with every session. Use your photos for save-the-dates, wedding websites, guest books, canvas prints — anything you&#8217;d like. The images are yours with no additional licensing fees, ever.
