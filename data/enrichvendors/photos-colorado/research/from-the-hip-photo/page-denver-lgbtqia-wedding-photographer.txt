Our Commitment to the LGBTQIA+ Community
From the Hip Photo has been a vocal and active ally of the LGBTQIA+ community since our founding. When Colorado legalized civil unions in 2013 and marriage equality in 2014, we were there documenting those historic first ceremonies. That work shaped who we are as a company and deepened our commitment to inclusive photography.
In 2018, when the Masterpiece Cakeshop v. Colorado Civil Rights Commission case went to the Supreme Court, From the Hip was part of the amicus brief supporting same-sex couples&#8217; rights to equal access under the law.
When potentially discriminatory bills have been brought to the state Capitol here in Colorado, From the Hip has testified in front of our representatives to ensure the LGBTQIA+ community would not be further marginalized or disadvantaged. We have even used our platform to have op-eds published in the Denver Post in support of marriage equality and in advocacy of LGBTQIA+ protections.
Our studio is located at 8842 E 47th Ave in Denver, Colorado 80238 , in the Northfield area of the Central Park neighborhood. We welcome all couples to visit for an in-person consultation. Get in touch anytime.

LGBTQIA+ Wedding Photography FAQ
Do you have experience photographing same-sex weddings?
Absolutely. From the Hip Photo has been proudly photographing LGBTQIA+ weddings and celebrations across Colorado for over a decade. Our team has documented same-sex ceremonies at mountain venues, downtown Denver rooftops, intimate backyard gatherings, and everything in between. We understand the unique moments that make each celebration special and bring the same passion and professionalism to every wedding we shoot.
How do you approach posing for same-sex couples?
We focus on connection and authenticity rather than rigid traditional poses. Our documentary style captures genuine moments of love, laughter, and emotion as they unfold naturally. For posed portraits, we guide couples based on what feels comfortable and natural to them, never forcing anyone into poses that feel awkward or inauthentic. Every couple is unique, and our approach reflects that. Reach out to learn more about how we work with couples.
What wedding venues in Colorado are LGBTQIA+ friendly?
Colorado has been a leader in marriage equality, and the vast majority of venues across the state warmly welcome LGBTQIA+ couples. Popular inclusive venues include rooftop spaces in downtown Denver, mountain lodges in Breckenridge and Vail, rustic barns in Evergreen, and garden venues along the Front Range. Our team at From the Hip Photo has shot at hundreds of Colorado venues and can recommend ones that will make you feel celebrated and welcome.
How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?
We recommend booking 8 to 12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season from May through October. Popular weekend dates fill up quickly, and early booking gives us time to scout your venue, plan your timeline, and ensure every detail is covered. Check our availability now to secure your date.
What is your photography style for weddings?
Our style is documentary and candid with a fine-art edge. We capture real moments as they happen — the tears during vows, the laughter on the dance floor, the quiet glances between partners. We blend photojournalistic coverage with beautifully composed portraits so you get a complete story of your day. Our images are warm, natural, and full of genuine emotion. See more of our wedding work to get a feel for our approach.
