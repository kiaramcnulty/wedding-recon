For Clients Who...
Welcome! 
I’m Sarah and I’m so happy you’re here!
I’m a wedding and portrait photographer with home bases in both Denver, Colorado and Milwaukee, Wisconsin. I primarily serve Milwaukee and Madison, WI, and Colorado's front range. However, no destination is too far - I love to travel! 
I believe in moment-driven photography and carry that philosophy into every genre I photograph. To me, the best images are the the ones that capture real, genuine moments, and evoke emotion.
Beyond capturing your story in the most authentic way possible, I am passionate about providing my clients with a stress free and positive experience. 
If this resonates with you, look no further!
