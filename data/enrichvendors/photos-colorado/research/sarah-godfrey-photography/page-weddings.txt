Welcome! 
One of my most memorable inquiries came a couple who revealed they were struggling to find a wedding photographer because they weren’t your “traditional, typical couple.” 
This was upsetting to hear, because a wedding photographer’s job should be to serve the couple… not the other way around. 
Too often, I see website statements that go a little bit like this… “you may be a **insert photographer’s name Bride** if you make time for the details because you know they matter.” 
That is so not my style. Although, I will definitely provide you with timeline recommendations to help your day run smoothly and am more than just some eyes behind a camera, I’m not here to artistically micromanage your wedding day or tell you how to be you! 
When you work with me, you are the one in the driver’s seat for your own special day. If the flat lays & million tiny detail shots are of utmost importance to you – then they will be to me as well. If you just want to soak in the emotions of your day and be present – I will be that ninja photographer in the background, capturing all those small, irreplaceable moments and blending into the crowd. 
Whatever your preference is, I do promise you this. 
Emotion + Personality.
My style of wedding photography is authentic and intentional. Because you deserve to truly see yourself when you look back at your wedding photographs and feel every single moment all over again . 
I'm Sarah, a wedding photographer, serving Colorado, Wisconsin and destinations worldwide.

The Process
My primary title is wedding photographer, but I’m also an experience guide. My job is to create a stress-free environment for you from inquiry all the way up to your wedding day! 
For every couple, there’s a "right fit" for a photographer. One who understands you, your wedding day vision, and cares about putting you first on your big day. After you inquire, I'm most passionate about getting to know you two as a couple. Let's make sure we are the perfect fit for each other! 
Let's Chat!
Once we determine we are the perfect fit for one another, it's time to lock in your date and schedule your engagement session. The majority of my wedding collections include an engagement session. While it's not mandatory, I highly recommend doing one. Engagement sessions are so important for us to get to know each other, for you to "practice posing" so you will be pros for your wedding day, and to receive amazing photographs for your save-the-dates, etc. 
Save the Date & Schedule Your Engagement Session
I'm here throughout the process....vendors, timelines and more! 
I don’t expect you to know the best timeline for your unique wedding day - that is where my expertise comes in handy. I will help you plan a timeline that allows for you to have amazing images, without feeling like you are spending your entire special day in a photoshoot. I want you to feel relaxed and be able to fully enjoy yourself on your wedding day.
