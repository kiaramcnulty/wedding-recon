Since starting my business in 2012 and moving here in 2015, I've been helping couples navigate the beautiful chaos of planning and photographing elopements across Colorado's Western Slope, San Juan Mountains, and Moab, Utah.
I believe your wedding day should feel as beautiful as it looks—which means comfortable, stress-free, and entirely true to who you are.
Whether you need comprehensive planning support, photography that captures the story and scenery of your day, or both—I'm here to make sure the experience is exactly what you hoped for.

Amanda Matilda Photography is a Grand Junction, Colorado-based elopement photography and planning business founded in 2012. I specialize in intimate celebrations across Colorado's Western Slope, San Juan Mountains, and Moab, Utah — including Crested Butte, Ouray, Telluride, Aspen, Silverton, Palisade, Grand Mesa, Arches National Park, and Canyonlands. I offer elopement photography, wedding photography, and standalone elopement planning services for LGBTQ+ and non-traditional couples. Leave No Trace certified and featured in 5280 Magazine, Offbeat Wed, Wandering Weddings, and the Visit Grand Junction visitor's guide.
