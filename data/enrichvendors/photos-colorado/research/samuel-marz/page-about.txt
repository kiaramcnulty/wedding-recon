For over a decade, I've photographed weddings with the belief that the best photographs happen when you're free to experience the day as it unfolds. Using a blend of digital and analog photography, I create imagery that feels effortless, refined, and enduring.
 Beautiful light, genuine connection, and the moments in between are what I'll always be drawn to. 
If this feels like the way you want to experience your day, I’d love to hear from you. 
I'm Sam, a Colorado-based wedding photographer documenting celebrations across the West, New York, Santa Fe, and beyond.
 I've been photographing weddings for over 12 years, working with both digital and analog film to create photographs that feel natural, thoughtful, and enduring.
 At home, you'll usually find me with my wife, our son, and our hound dog.
