I’m an adventure-loving wedding, elopement, and lifestyle photographer. I believe in capturing inclusive love stories for any humans. I am based in Denver, Colorado but willing and excited to travel where your love takes you. I have photographed hundreds of weddings and have over 9 years of wedding photography experience. 
A Destination Wedding Photographer based in Denver, Colorado .
I’m Maddy,
I have a few loves of my own. My family and my dog mean everything to me. They are the rock that anchors everything and they keep me going, cheer me on, and lift me higher than the soul can hope. I’m also obsessed with ice cream - I truly don’t discriminate when it comes to flavor but my favorite classics are vanilla with a fudge swirl or a really solid pistachio. I love to travel and explore (I’ve been to over 30 countries and always dreaming of the next!). Also, an honorable mention for my love of really bad reality tv. Sometimes, you’ve just gotta have it.
 I believe the best photographs come from a blend of beautiful light , genuine connection , and an easy, joyful energy —resulting in images that feel both candid and effortlessly editorial.

Capturing inclusive, heartfelt stories
No matter what you look like or who you love - I will do everything I can to accommodate and capture your love in an honest and respectful way. Tell me your pronouns! Tell me what you are comfortable with. Let me know what gets you excited about one another! What is your favorite trait about your partner? The more I know the better I will be equipped to really understand your love story and incorporate elements of your unique relationship on camera.
 I provide photography services for people of all races, abilities, faith beliefs, body types, sexual orientations, and beyond. You are welcome here.
Documentary Wedding Photographer
I primarily focus on documentary-style wedding and elopement photography. This means that I act as “a fly on the wall” just observing and capturing candid moments throughout a wedding day. I truly want to reflect your love back to you and let that be an organic style true to who you are and how your love story unfolds. This requires that you trust that I will be there to politely observe, enhance and capture moments as they happen in a candid and honest way.
 I’m inspired by the genuine moments that may seem mundane but really make the best date nights. Having one too many glasses of wine and learning to dance in the kitchen together. Hugging your parent. Admiring your lover on a long road trip when they didn’t know you were quite literally staring at them. Your pet waking you up licking your face. Experiencing a new culture for the very first time. The deep and meaningful times where a fleeting moment means everything.
