As soon as you know! Engagement sessions are typically booked a couple of months ahead, elopements anywhere from 2 to 18 months, and weddings usually 9–18 months in advance. I tend to book up quickly, so once your date and venue are secured, let's get you booked!
How far in advance should we book you?
While I can't meet in person before booking due to the volume of inquiries, I'm happy to hop on a call or FaceTime to get to know each other. Once you're booked, we'll meet for coffee or drinks before your big day. By then, you might even know my dogs and have been to my house!
Can we meet in person before booking?
That totally depends on your preference, but location planning is included in all packages. Once you book, I'll share my favorite spots, and we'll choose the perfect place that feels like you—whether that's a hidden gem or a classic Colorado backdrop.
