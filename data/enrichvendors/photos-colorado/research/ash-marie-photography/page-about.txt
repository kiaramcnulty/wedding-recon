I’ve always been fascinated how photos
 stop time and also rewind it.
—
 I bought my first real camera when I was pregnant with my son. I wanted to capture everything I could and those moments go so fast. After the birth of daughter I decided to take my “hobby” further and capture moments for other families. I started my journey into photography wanting to capture moments that I would want for myself. Sure, the smile at the camera moments, but also the raw, candid moments just being who you are. Those moments you can’t capture yourself. The silly, the fun. The little touches and giggles. And even the tears and frowns. 
 I pride myself in making photo sessions approachable and easy for families. It’s sometimes so awkward taking photos, and I don’t want it to ever feel like a chore. So I try and make them fun, little adventure sessions. I won’t pose you, or over-style you, I want you to be you - so you have those moments to look back on forever. It’s been such a fun adventure meeting new people and telling their story through a lens.
