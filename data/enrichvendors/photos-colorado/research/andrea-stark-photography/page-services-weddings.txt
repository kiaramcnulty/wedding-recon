Among the Best Colorado Mountain Wedding Photographers
For the last decade as a Colorado mountain wedding photographer, I’ve been capturing milestone moments in the mountains of Breckenridge, Vail, Beaver Creek, Aspen, Keystone, Crested Butte, Tabernash, Telluride, and Steamboat Springs. I have extensive experience at some of Colorado’s best destination wedding venues, including The Ritz-Carlton Bachelor Gulch, Four Seasons Vail, Devil’s Thumb Ranch, The St. Regis Aspen, Maroon Bells, and many luxury AirBnB’s (ask me about my favorites!). 
I’m known as a natural light Colorado mountain wedding photographer as well as a hybrid photographer. This means that I shoot in two fine art mediums &#8211; both film and digital formats, to deliver a level of diversity, warmth, airiness, emotion, and an all-encompassing story to each of my clients. It’s rare to find a film photographer in a digital age, but it’s important to me that I give my clients the best of both. Your day will be meticulously covered, masterfully captured, with fun and lots of smiles too. 
My style is photojournalistic to capture the moments that matter most and frame them in a wider story of your celebration while still embracing traditional portraiture. I offer simple and natural direction instead of heavy-handed posing so that my images better reflect the unique love between each of my clients. I care about your surroundings and why you’ve chosen to get married in the mountains. My images always have a sense of place to take you back. 
On your wedding day, I blend in with your guests to capture your love, the people, and all the beautiful details that make that day unique. I direct when needed, making sure to gather and coordinate the individuals for the photos you desire. Otherwise, I blend. I believe the unexpected moment is what makes an unforgettable lasting impression. 
I would be honored to be considered as your Colorado mountain wedding photographer.

How Much Does a Colorado Wedding Photographer Cost?
I offer full day wedding coverage in mountain areas throughout Colorado and beyond. This includes getting ready, the wedding ceremony, wedding party and family photos, newlywed portraits, the reception, and all the gorgeous details you’ve planned along the way.
If yours is a destination wedding like most of my clients host, there’s more than just the wedding day of opportunity for capturing the magic. I welcome the opportunity to document your wedding welcome party, rehearsal dinner, post brunch, excursions, and the entire wedding day. Every meal and outing can become an event worth documenting. You may even have your bachelor/bachelorette parties in the mountains.
If you’d like me to be a part of the entire wedding experience, I’m happy to discuss what that could look like for you. Custom quotes for full wedding weekends are available to suit your specific needs.
