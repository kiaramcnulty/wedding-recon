Breckenridge Engagement Photography
My Breckenridge engagement photography is some of my favorite work to produce. In this setting, we have the time to be fun and creative together while you bask in the glow of the excitement of your upcoming wedding. You direct the style, I create the setting. Formal or casual, you decide what you’d like the experience to be and what the images portray. I’ll make it memorable in each and every way.
My Colorado mountain and Breckenridge engagement photography sessions are perfect for couples who want to capture this special time but perhaps don’t plan on getting married in the mountains. This session gives you that magical setting in everyday clothes that you might frame more often than your wedding portraits. If you’re recently engaged and looking for beautiful outdoor imagery to share on a save-the-date or use at your upcoming wedding, then consider engagement photos in the mountains.

What are the best places for Colorado Engagement Photos ? 
There’s no better place for portraits, especially Colorado engagement photos, than in my scenic town of Breckenridge. I’ve explored nearly every trail and idyllic spot in the surrounding area in pursuit of the most gorgeous backdrops and views. I look for areas that are easy to access but somewhat hidden in order to avoid much foot traffic.
I seek out a variety of textures and colors for the backdrop, which helps to create striking photos. I pay attention to lighting at different times of day to know exactly when and where to set up for the best Colorado engagement photos. I set us up for success ahead of time so the day is one of enjoyment and magic.
I take into account your preferences for attire, activity, and hiking length. Do you have any pets you’d like to join you? Do you have a favorite item you’d like to include like a certain wine, blanket, or prop? I like to hear about how you met and what drew you to each other. I learn as much as I can about your personality as a couple to draw that out in my camera lens.
Once I know more about you, I make location recommendations accordingly. I photograph couples all around Colorado, including Breckenrdige, Vail, Beaver Creek, Aspen, Crested Butte, and Telluride.
