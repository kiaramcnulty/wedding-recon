Andrea Stark Photography specializes in capturing the breathtaking beauty of mountain destination weddings. As a mountain destination wedding photographer, I have honed my skills in capturing photos that make you feel the warmth and lightness of the sun cresting over a mountain peak, the captivating landscapes, peaceful, intimate moments, and heartfelt emotions that make these weddings truly extraordinary. With my refined eye for detail and expertise in photographing grand mountain celebrations, I strive to create timeless images that encapsulate the truth and beauty of your day.
