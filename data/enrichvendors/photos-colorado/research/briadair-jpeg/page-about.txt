Background
 Over the years, I’ve worn a lot of hats — logistics expert, project manager, team lead — but nothing has ever felt as right as holding a camera in my hands and getting to tell someone’s story through images.
 Since making the leap into full-time photography in 2024, I’ve had the honor of documenting everything from tearful first looks and mountaintop elopements to high-energy Jiu Jitsu rolls and heartfelt proposals. I’ve photographed hundreds of weddings and elopements, and I bring a calm, organized energy to every session — so even the most camera-shy people end up feeling confident and comfortable.
My work has been published, praised for its emotional depth, and known for capturing moments just as they are...no forced poses or awkward smiles. I hold a degree in business management, certified in project management and went through real life "photography bootcamp". I approach each shoot with a blend of structure and spontaneity. You’ll get guidance when you need it, and space to just be when the moment calls for it.
My style is: 
Natural. Moody. Honest. Thoughtful.
I love real emotion, in-between moments, and that quiet magic you don’t even notice until you see it in a photo.
My values are: 
 Authenticity over perfection.
 Connection over content.
 Experience over just photos.
 I’m here to be more than just another vendor. I’ll be your hype woman, your timeline wrangler, and the calm in the chaos when things get wild (and they always do — in the best way). If you're looking for someone who gets the little stuff and shows up fully for the big stuff, we’ll probably get along great.
