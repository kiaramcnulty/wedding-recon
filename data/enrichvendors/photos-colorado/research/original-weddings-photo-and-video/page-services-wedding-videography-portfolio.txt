FAQs
What are the differences between the Rustic and Timeless videography styles? 
Rustic Collection – A dramatic, cinematic wedding film with artistic transitions, slow-motion shots, and emotional storytelling.
Timeless Collection – A documentary-style video that presents your wedding in a natural, true-to-life sequence.
How do we choose the best wedding videography style for us? 
If you love cinematic wedding video showcases with an emotional feel, the Rustic style is for you. If you prefer a natural, chronological approach, the Timeless style is ideal. Some couples even choose both—a highlight reel in Rustic and a full documentary-style Timeless video.
What moments will be captured in our wedding video? 
Our wedding video gallery includes key wedding moments such as the ceremony, vows, speeches, dances, and candid interactions. The difference is in the editing: Rustic focuses on artistic highlights, while Timeless provides longer, natural footage.
Can we personalize our wedding video? 
Yes! Our best wedding videography examples allow full customization. You can add personal vows, speeches, and specific songs to make your wedding film uniquely yours.
