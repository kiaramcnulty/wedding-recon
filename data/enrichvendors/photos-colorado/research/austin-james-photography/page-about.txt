Meet Austin 
 Owner & Lead Photographer 
As much as I love the photography aspect of my job, nothing beats getting to hang out with my couples and have fun together! I mean what’s life without having fun and enjoying the moments? Whether it’s going out for a drink for our first consult or goofing around on the wedding day, I love building those relationships to the point where wedding day is so fun and easy! Being able to give my couples great memories to hold onto is just the cherry on top!
