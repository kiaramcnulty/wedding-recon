THE AJP WEDDING EXPERIENCE 
 -Colorado Wedding Photographer-
SO WHAT EXACTLY IS THE AJP WEDDING EXPERIENCE?
When it comes to the AJP Experience and choosing a Colorado wedding photographer, communication and trust are such a huge deal! I want you to have a good idea of what it would be like working with me before we even meet! With 5 years and 100+ weddings under my belt, you’ll have a good idea of my expertise when we chat and that’s where the trust comes in. 
 Working with you on the timeline and getting to know you better during the process will help you feel that I absolutely got this! So check out the rest of this page and you will have a better idea of what working with me will look like!
MY PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE
I know I’ve been going on about the experience and haven’t even talked about my photos! I’m sure you’ve noticed already, but I really love the timeless, true to life look! I feel with so many trends going in and out of the wedding scene this style is something you’ll look back on 30 years from now and won’t feel like your album looks dated. I’m a mixture of those classic poses with those fun, organic photos that really show of your personalities. I mean who doesn’t love variety right?

Plus you can do anything you like for your session, there are no limits!
Time to grab a drink! 
Let’s get together and hang out! This is where we can really connect and build that friendship I’ve been talking about! We can talk wedding stuff or ,if you need a break from planning, we can just hang. Step 5 can be repeated as much as we’d like.
Last minute details! 
We’re so close to wedding day now! The week or two before the wedding we’ll do a final consultation just to go over the timeline we’ve been working on and get those last minute details together. We’ve made it!! The day you’ve planning forever is finally here and all of us vendors are here so you can relax and have fun with your friends and family. I mean you should be able to completely enjoy your day right? The Final Product!

FAQs
We're all set! How do we reserve our date? 
First thing is you’ll want to fill out the form on the contact page . We’ll set up a meeting to get to know each other and see if we’re a good fit. If you’re ready to book I’ll send over a contract and it’s a $1000 retainer to reserve your date. Once the contract and retainer are in, we are good to go!
Where are you located? 
I am based out of Denver, Colorado but I do weddings all over Colorado and beyond! For anything out of state my travel fees typically only include plane ticket/gas and room & board. No extra frills involved!
How many photos will I get and when will I recieve them? 
When it comes to your wedding, I don’t like to put a cap on your photos so what I’m giving you is the best of the best. This is typically between 100-150 photos per hour, so an 8 hour wedding would be around 900-1200 photos! As far as turnaround time goes, typically you will receive them within 3 weeks but can go up to 6 weeks during the busiest time of the year. But don’t worry! You will be able to see a sneak peek shortly after your day as well as blog with up to 250 photos!
Do you back up our photos? 
I absolutely do! Making sure your wedding photos are safely backed up is a major priority for me! Not only do I use backup cards on wedding day, I’ll immediately back them up to two hard drives AND the cloud when I get home. I keep all of my couple’s wedding photos for life, so if anything happens where you need them again 10 years down the road, I’ll have them for you!
How do you deliver your photos? 
With USB starting to become a thing of the past, I do everything digital these days! You’ll receive a full online gallery that stays up for a year so you can download them at any time. The gallery also has a store for any prints, canvasses, or albums you may need!
Do we have to go through you to print our photos? 
Nope! Along with your gallery, you’ll receive a zip drive of all your high resolution photos and a print release so you can print them wherever you’d like. This way you don’t need to always go through me for every little 4x6. Typically most of my couples order wall art and albums through me as I go through a very high quality print company that will make sure you get the best of the best!
What can I expect from you on our wedding day? 
When I show up for your wedding, not only will I make sure to get you beautiful images but I will also help you as you navigate through the day. I like to think of myself as more than just a photographer on your big day. I am also a calming force, a coordinator, an emergency kit, and more! Along with that, I have a bridal guide I created just for you to help you throughout the planning process!
Do you use shot lists or will you replicate images from my pinterest board? 
Pinterest is a great tool for getting inspired and planning your wedding and I love seeing your vision. However, I won’t use your Pinterest board as a shot list (or any other shot list for that matter). Shot lists keep me from being fully present so I can capture genuine moments as they unfold, not try to recreate someone else’s moment for you. I’ll do my best to keep your preferences in mind, but I want you to feel confident and trust that you’ll love your own wedding photos even more than those ones that you saw on Pinterest. Now when it comes to sentimental photos specifically for you, that is something all together different and if you communicate those to me, I will make sure we get those images for you to have forever!
