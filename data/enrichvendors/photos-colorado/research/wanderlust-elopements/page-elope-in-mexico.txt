MEXICO ADVENTURE ELOPEMENTS
LOCATION
If you decide that a Mexican adventure elopement is right for you then the next step will be to choose your location (and probably your photographer, who can help with this). Mexican elopement locations are largely dependent upon the season (it can be too hot, or too stormy during certain times of the year), the level of remoteness (how far from an airport), and which amenities you would like. Are you looking for beach vibes, desert cacti, or jungle greenery? What kind of landscape would be the backdrop for your perfect day? There are so many good options and we are here to help you find the perfect one.
LOGISTICS
After considering the time of year and the environment you want to elope in, we can start to put together a plan. Questions worth considering are how far from a paved road would you like to be? What kind of physical shape is everyone in your party in (maybe hiking won’t be possible with Grandma..)? Will you need a rental car? Would you like to 4-wheel to a remote beach? How far from your lodging would you like to be? Once all these questions have been pondered then we can assist you in choosing the perfect location, and then we can easily help you plan the rest of your amazing day!
DETAILS
Alright! We’ve chosen the spot. Now we can hammer out the details. Do we need the help of some other vendors (i.e. florals, cake, accessories like tables or chairs, food)? Maybe a mariachi band?! What kind of transportation? Hiring transportation agencies to pick you and your guests up at the airport can be very convenient in Mexico. These are all things that your adventure elopement photographers can dial in for you! Once we have discussed your vision we’ll ensure that all of these details are set in stone so that your perfect day comes to life exactly as anticipated.

Our Favorite Baja Mexico Elopement Locations 
Todos Santos – Rancho Corazón : This eco-luxury ranch nestled between the Pacific Ocean and the Sierra de le Laguna Mountains offers a mix of desert charm and boho elegance, with wide-open skies, organic gardens, and quiet trails right outside your casita. Perfect for couples who want a little luxury, but chill vib es.
Sierra de la Laguna Mountains : The Sierra de la Laguna Biosphere Reserve is a protected area full of pine forests, granite peaks, and high-altitude oases right in the heart of Baja Sur. Perfect for adventurous couples who want to earn their ceremony spot with a hike. 
Bahía Concepción : Located on the Sea of Cortez side of the peninsula, Bahía Concepción is a stunning stretch of turquoise bays and white sand beaches surrounded by rocky desert cliffs. Perfect for water-loving couples looking for a beachy vibe.
Cabo Pulmo National Park : This protected marine park on the East Cape is one of Baja's best-kept secrets. It offers rugged coastal cliffs and quiet beaches that are perfect for an oceanfront elopement. The desert-meets-sea scenery is breathtaking. Perfect for couples dreaming of a wild, coastal ceremony.
