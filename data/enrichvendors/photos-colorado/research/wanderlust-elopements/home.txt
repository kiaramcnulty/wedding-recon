FAQ 
What is an adventure elopement? 
A day where you share vows in an epic location without all of the fussy details. This type of wedding is geared towards the experience and not the guest list. You do it your way. 
How many photos do I receive? 
All of the edited photos are included in the price. Typically we deliver around 50 photos per hour of shoot time. The final photos will be delivered to you via an online gallery that you can share with friends and family. 
What is included in your packages? 
All packages include unlimited planning with us. We work with you to find the perfect location, as well as providing vendor recommendations if needed (cake, hair & makeup, etc…). We communicate with you during the entire process, ensuring a seamless, stress-free experience the day of. 
We're camera shy. Can you help? 
Don’t worry, we get it. We’re awkward in front of cameras too. Getting to know you during the planning process allows us to help guide you while keeping you relaxed. We keep it easy-going, you won’t even know you’re being photographed. 
Are you willing to travel? 
We love traveling! It’s actually one of our favorite activities. If you want to get married on the moon, we’re in!
Can we customize a package? 
Absolutely! We are more than willing to work with you no matter what your plan is! We understand that everyone has a different vision and we love taking on unique projects.
