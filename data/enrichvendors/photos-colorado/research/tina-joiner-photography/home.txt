Hey, I'm Tina, 
I am the owner of Tina Joiner Photography. I describe my style of photography as elegant, romantic, and heartfelt. I connect best with couples who put a lot of thought and sentiment into the details of their wedding, but also realize that the most important part of this day is about building a beautiful marriage. 
I am passionate about details. I want to capture all of the special and memorable pieces of your day that make it unique to you as a couple. You will put a lot of time and effort into your wedding and it is my desire for you to be able to look back at your wedding photos and feel like you are reliving your big day all over again. I am excited to give you a wonderful experience from the first time we meet all the way to the delivery of your photos.
