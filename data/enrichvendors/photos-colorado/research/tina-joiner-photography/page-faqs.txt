Frequently asked questions
Booking 
Delivery 
Where are you based?
Based in Colorado Springs, Colorado, and available worldwide. I happily travel for destination weddings, elopements, and sessions—capturing your story wherever it unfolds. 
How do you describe your work?
My work style is rooted in storytelling. I focus on capturing real, unscripted moments as they naturally unfold, while gently guiding you when needed to create timeless, elevated imagery. The result is a balance of honest emotion and refined composition - photographs that feel effortless, romantic, and true to you. 
When should we book you?
We recommend reaching out at least a year in advance if possible. We are always happy to check availability for your date, so please inquire! 
Do you travel for destination weddings?
Yes, we do! Traveling is a part of who we are. Learning from the people and places around us makes life richer. We have travelled all over the US and abroad. Just say the word, and we will be on our way. 
Do you offer videography?
I do not offer videography services, but I’m always happy to recommend trusted videographers I love working alongside. 
Do you carry insurance?
Yes, I do carry business insurance. I am happy to coordinate with your venue on what certificates they need from me if they require vendors to have proof of insurance. 
How do I book?
The Booking Process 
1. Inquire 
 Fill out the contact form with a few details about your day. This helps me understand your vision and make sure your date is available. 
2. Connect 
 We’ll hop on a call to chat through your plans, expectations, and make sure we’re the right fit. This is a relaxed conversation where you can ask all the questions and get to know me. 
3. Secure Your Date 
 Once you’re ready to move forward, I’ll send over your contract and invoice. Your date is officially reserved once both are completed. 
4. Planning & Details 
 As your wedding day approaches, we’ll gather all the important details - timelines, locations, priorities, and anything meaningful to you - so everything feels seamless and stress-free. 
5. Wedding Day 
 You enjoy every moment while I focus on documenting your day as it naturally unfolds, capturing both the big moments and the quiet in-between ones. 
6. Gallery Delivery 
 Your fully edited, high-resolution images will be delivered via an online gallery, ready to download, share, and relive for years to come. 
What payments do you accept?
I can accept credit card payments through my secure booking site (get those points!), but I am happy to accept cash or checks as well.
