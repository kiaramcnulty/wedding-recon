I CAN’T WAIT TO MEET YOU! 
 When you hire me as your photographer, my goal is not only to create stunning imagery that captures the essence of who you are and the moment, but also to develop a friendship with you as well. 
 Hiring a photographer is a big choice, and I understand that you are entrusting me with photographing intimate, real moments of you and your loved ones. I do not take this lightly and it is my goal to make you feel at ease and comfortable with me. 
 From the smallest details to the big picture, I want to make my clients, or rather new friends, feel confident that I will capture everything they envision and more!

THE PROCESS 
 Inquiry 
 Once you fill out an inquiry form, I will always respond within 48 hours (unless an automated message states otherwise). I will ask for some more information from you prior to sending you my pricing guide. If you are interested in learning more about me and my process, we can set up an in person meeting or phone call to discuss your shoot! 
Inquire
LETS BOOK IT! 
 Once you decide to book your shoot, I will send over a photo agreement and request a 50% deposit which can be paid via electronic invoice to hold the date of your shoot. Once the agreement has been signed and the deposit has been paid, we can lock in the date. Depending on the type of shoot, I always send over a mood board for us to collaborate on and ensure we are on the same page. 
 If you are booking a wedding, I will send over a questionnaire so that I can craft the photographic timeline for your wedding and ensure I am on the same page as your planner. Once we are in agreement on the timeline, I will give you a phone call about 7 days prior to your wedding to discuss ALL the details.
