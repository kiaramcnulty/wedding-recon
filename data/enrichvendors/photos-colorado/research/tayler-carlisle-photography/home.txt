Manifesto
it's funny how day by day nothing changes, but when you look back, everything is different. c.s. lewis 
 after all these years of having a camera by my side, i've discovered that i most enjoy getting to document authentic connections, remarkable relationships and ALL the good stuff that happens in between. i find imagery to be incredibly powerful. looking at an image can genuinely take me back to THAT moment in time. i smell the smells, i hear the sounds, and i feel all the feels again. its just magical! and because life is inevitably evolving and changing, i think it is so important to document life as much and as often as you can! especially for those extra special times in life! this is what its all about for us! love & magic 
oh hiiiii! my name is Tayler!
I have been documenting and capturing humans and lovers for over nine years now. Before that, I was traveling the world with my camera as a flight attendant documenting my adventures and new friends. I have since settled in Evergreen, Colorado but will never stop traveling for work or fun. 
 There are plenty of photographers out there who will deliver good photos, but I want to give you more than that. I want to give you the space to be your true selves. For your time with me to be an experience you won’t forget instead of just another box to check off your list. I want to create beautiful imagery with you and for you. Let’s forget about what your photos “should” look like and together, let’s create beautiful imagery that captures these milestone moments in your life.
