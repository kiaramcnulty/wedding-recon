Morgan & Jordan
Morgan and Jordan were married on her father&#8217;s farm outside Longmont. They knew they wanted a Colorado wedding photojournalist, and when they saw pictures I had done for friends of theirs a few years ago they gave me a call. They are very sincere, grounded, and rather fun to be around. Their wedding reflected their personalities, an easy, laid back party with family and close friends by the side of a country creek. The ceremony and reception had everything you would want, with nothing superfluous. It seems to be part of a trend in the weddings I&#8217;m doing lately, where people  have pared back to the essential, focusing on what is truly important.
