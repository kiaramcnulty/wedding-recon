What to expect from my Wedding Photography Service? 
Free consultation 
From our very first video call, where we begin to understand your vision, to providing thoughtful recommendations on location and timelines, I want you to know that I'm here for you every step of the way. Whenever you need to talk or share your ideas, I'll be ready to listen and support you. Your wedding is important to me, and together, we can create something truly special. 
Professional color retouched 
the artistry of my signature film-style editing with professional color retouching that enhances every moment. Your images will receive the attention they deserve, bringing out their true beauty and vibrancy. Let me help you create stunning visuals that tell your unique story. 
Relaxed environment 
I prioritize establishing a relaxed and natural environment on your wedding day, enabling you to immerse yourself in the celebration rather than being preoccupied photography. My approach is designed to blend seamlessly into the day, capturing authentic emotions and candid moments as they arise. This allows you to fully engage in the festivities while I document your love story in a professional and unobtrusive manner. 
Guaranteed 30 -day turnaround. 
I have a guaranteed turnaround policy of 30 days, ensuring that all tasks are completed within this timeframe at the latest. However, I strive to deliver results even earlier than scheduled, prioritizing and client satisfaction. 
Fun, instead of awkward 
I focus on creating a relaxed and enjoyable atmosphere during photoshoots, allowing individuals to have fun rather than feeling awkward in front of the camera. By encouraging natural interactions and candid moments, I capture genuine expressions that reflect the true spirit of the occasion, away from overly posed pictures. My goal is to make each session a memorable experience, where everyone feels comfortable and at ease.
