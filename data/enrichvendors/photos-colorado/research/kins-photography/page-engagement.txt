What to expect from my Engagement Photography Service? 
Location Planning 
Instead of just finding picturesque locations, I believe in capturing the essence your story. Let's explore places that hold significance to you, where memories are made and emotions are felt. Together, we'll create beautiful images that reflect your unique journey. 
Professional color retouched 
the artistry of my signature film-style editing with professional color retouching that enhances every moment. Your images will receive the attention they deserve, bringing out their true beauty and vibrancy. Let me help you create stunning visuals that tell your unique story. 
Relaxed environment 
I focus on creating a calm and natural atmosphere during your engagement, allowing you to enjoy the moment without worrying about photography. Endless energy 
Wherever life takes us, I'll be right by your side. Whether we're hitting the slopes for an exhilarating snowboard trip, setting up camp under the stars, or enjoying a quiet coffee shop date, I'm committed sharing every adventure with you. Together, we embrace the thrill of the mountains and the peace of nature all while savoring those cozy moments over a warm cup of coffee. Just know that I'm always here, ready to go wherever you lead. 
Truly being yourself 
Every couple should have a distinctive engagement photoshoot that embodies their unique personalities. Regardless of whether your style is fun, playful, or romantic, I will carefully observe and capture the essence of your relationship in a professional manner.
