Elopement Photography 
Overwhelmed by the chaos of traditional weddings? Elopement may be the perfect way to privately celebrate with your loved ones. 
Let mother nature host your venue and freely dance in the woods. 
Bathing under the sunset in your special day and not have to worry about being rushed for your timeline. 
Why not embrace adventure your favorite hobby to celebrate the best day of your life? 
For couples seeking a truly magical experience, the Bay Area is also conveniently located near some of California's most stunning national parks. Imagine exchanging vows under the towering trees of Yosemite, or saying "yes" on a cliffside in Point Reyes National Seashore, with the Pacific Ocean as your backdrop. What about a first dance on your Snowboard? Whether you're looking for coastal cliffs, serene lakes, or ancient forests, California offers easy access to some of the state's most awe-inspiring natural beauty. 
Whether you choose to elope in the stunning nature of California or venture out into Sahara Desert in Morocco, I'm here to capture the authenticity and magic of your elopement in these unforgettable locations.

What to expect from my Elopment Photography Service? 
Location scouting 
I always scout locations before a photoshoot to ensure trail conditions and are suitable. I pay close attention to the timing of lighting and direction, regardless of whether it's my first or hundredth visit. Nature's beauty changes with each season, and I want to capture that unique essence every time. 
Professional color retouched 
the artistry of my signature film-style editing with professional color retouching that enhances every moment. Your images will receive the attention they deserve, bringing out their true beauty and vibrancy. Let me help you create stunning visuals that tell your unique story. 
Relaxed environment 
I focus on creating a calm and natural atmosphere during your engagement, allowing you to enjoy the moment without worrying about photography. My approach blends seamlessly into the experience, capturing genuine emotions and moments as they happen. This way, you can fully immerse yourself in the celebration while I professionally and unobtrusively document your love story. 
Endless energy 
Wherever life takes us, I'll be right by your side. Whether we're hitting the slopes for an exhilarating snowboard trip, setting up camp under the stars, or rock climbing in the canyon, I'm committed sharing every adventure with you. Together, we embrace the thrill of the mountains and the peace of nature all while savoring those cozy moments over a warm cup of chocolate by the tent. Just know that I'm always here, ready to go wherever you lead. 
Coordinator 
Hiring me as your elopement photographer will save you both time and money on finding a coordinator, especially if it's just the two of you. I will plan the locations and timeline to align with your vision based on my experience, ensuring that your special day goes off without hitch. 
Additionally, I will guide you through the permit to ensure we have no issues while capturing stunning moments in beautiful nature. ​
