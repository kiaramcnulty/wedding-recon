About Julie
Julie Adriansen is an award-winning, published watercolorist and seasoned wedding officiant based in Colorado. 
 Her dynamic painting style is characterized by vivid colors and bold imagery, infused with a touch of whimsy. Drawing inspiration from people, animals, and the energy they radiate, Julie uses the hues she perceives in her subjects to guide her palette. For Julie, paint is a medium to convey personality, emotion, and the joys of life through her art.
Portraiture forms the foundation of her artistic endeavors. Capturing the essence of both human and animal subjects through color and expression is where she finds the true enchantment of her work.
Residing in the Rocky Mountains and the Western Slope has deepened her connection with the Colorado art community, fostering both personal growth and a fresh perspective on her subjects and choice of colors. Julie's art is a celebration of the world around her. She is honored to have her original artwork featured on the 2024 Palisade Peachfest Poster and Promotional Items.
For Julie, community engagement is paramount. She believes that art has the power to unite people, enhancing the quality of life in our communities. Art, in its many forms—be it creation, instruction, or appreciation—offers Julie, and everyone, a chance to immerse in beauty, emotion, expression, and joy.
Julie Adriansen
Artist • Art Teacher • Wedding Officiant
