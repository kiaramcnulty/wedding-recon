Wedding & Elopement Officiant, Julie Adriansen
Julie Adriansen is a Colorado wedding officiant who leads marriage, elopement, and vow renewal ceremonies primarily in and around Vail, Aspen, Steamboat Springs, Breckenridge, and Palisade. If a hike or out of state travel is required to get to your dream wedding location, Julie is up for the adventure! Since 2009, Julie has helped clients say "I Do" in many beautiful places from the Colorado Rocky Mountains and vineyards, to Texas plains, to Hawaiian beaches . 
 Leading up to the big day, she greatly enjoys getting to know the couple and creating the ceremony that best reflects their unique personalities, their love story, and their spiritual or religious perspective. Julie has many years of experience with a wide range of ceremony types including civil unions, interfaith, single religion, and non-religious. 
 Ceremonies typically last less than 30 minutes and Julie will rehearse with the couple to make sure everything goes smoothly on the big day.
