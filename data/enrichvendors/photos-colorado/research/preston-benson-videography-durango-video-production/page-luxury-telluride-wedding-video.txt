This day was truly one of the most magical wedding days I&#8217;ve ever had the pleasure of experiencing in Telluride, Colorado. And this is why you want a cinematic wedding highlight video of your wedding day. From getting ready at the luxurious Peaks Resort & Spa in Mountain Village, to the Bridal photo and video shoot underneath Bridal Veil Falls, there was no shortage of moments and memories to capture in slow-motion. From a ceremony at San Sophia Overlook overlooking the San Juan mountains in every direction to the jaw-dropping sunset on the Telluride Golf Course, this wedding film was packed with gorgeous scenery. And finally from a first dance on the Mt. Wilson Terrace lit by pastels of the fading sun and a dance party concluding with pyrotechnics, this was a complete dream wedding! But even greater than all the beauty of that day, was the love between these two amazing people! It was such an honor to be able to experience and capture Vivek and Hillary&#8217;s love story. Cheers you two!
