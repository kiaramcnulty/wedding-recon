Purgatory Ski Resort Weddings
Posted by Preston Benson on Wednesday, May 28, 2025 &middot; Leave a Comment 
One of my favorite places to shoot wedding films is right up HWY 550 from Durango, Colorado at the one and only Purgatory Ski Resort . Beautiful mountain peaks form the panoramic horizon, with views easily rivaling that of Telluride and Aspen. With a total of three different ceremony sites and three reception areas, you can&#8217;t go wrong getting hitched a Purgatory. In 2024, I shot four beautiful weddings, at the Dante&#8217;s Ceremony site and the brand new Paradise Ceremony Site at the top of Lift 1. At the top of the page we have the dreamy wedding of Dylan and Abbey on top of the Paradise run. Which wedding film is your favorite?
