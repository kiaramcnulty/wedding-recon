About me 
I'm Andy Bowen. A Colorado based photographer and videographer specializing in creative and striking imagery. Located on the Western Slope of the Rocky Mountains in Grand Junction, Colorado. I work hard to add something unique to all my portraits, landscapes, adventure sport, wildlife, stylized, and studio photography. 
​ 
I have been behind a camera for the better part of my life. From the shooting to the editing, I am passionate about every aspect of photography and video. I love reflecting the special moments in peoples lives. I believe photography gives us a way to hold on to and cherish the very best of life. Nothing makes me more happy than to see our clients light up when I show them their pictures. 
​ 
I have always had an "eye" for taking quality photos. I combined this skill with a degree in Media, and 20 years of experience to ensure you get the very best photos and cinema grade video possible. I stay up to date on new techniques, trends and technology to offer cutting edge results. Above Alpine is dedicated to capturing the moments in your life that you want to remember. 
We still offer elopement services but are no longer covering full size weddings.
