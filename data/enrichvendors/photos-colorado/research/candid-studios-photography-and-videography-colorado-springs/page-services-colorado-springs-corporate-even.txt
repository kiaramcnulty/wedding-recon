Colorado Springs Event Videographer FAQ
What types of corporate event videos do you produce? 
We create event recap videos, keynote recordings, testimonial interviews, social media highlights, live streams, training documentation, and promotional videos for future events. Each deliverable is designed to extend your event&#x27;s impact long after it ends.
Can you provide live streaming for corporate events? 
Yes, we offer multi-camera live streaming with professional switching, graphics overlays, and lower thirds. We stream to any platform including Zoom, YouTube, LinkedIn Live, or your custom RTMP destination. Recordings are available immediately after the stream.
How many cameras do you use for event videography? 
Standard coverage uses 2-3 cameras: a wide shot, a close-up, and a roaming camera for candid coverage. Large events or keynotes may use 4+ cameras for complete multi-angle coverage. Each setup includes professional audio capture.
Can you capture high-quality audio at loud events? 
Yes, we use a combination of wireless lavalier microphones for speakers, shotgun microphones for panels, and board feeds from the venue&#x27;s sound system. This multi-source approach ensures clear audio even in acoustically challenging environments.
Do you produce same-day event highlight reels? 
Yes! Same-day edits are a powerful marketing tool. We edit a 60-90 second highlight reel during the event and deliver it before the closing session. It&#x27;s an incredible way to energize closing remarks or share on social media while buzz is highest.
How do you handle recording multi-day conferences? 
We create a detailed production schedule covering all sessions, develop daily highlight packages, and can deliver daily recap videos. Our crew rotates to maintain energy across long events. Multi-day packages include planning, coverage, and daily deliverables.
Can you interview speakers and attendees on site? 
Absolutely. We set up interview stations with professional lighting and audio for polished testimonial and thought leadership videos. We can work from your question list or develop interview guides that align with your content marketing strategy.
What about event video for internal communications? 
We produce videos specifically for internal use: town halls, training sessions, company updates, and culture videos. These can be distributed through your intranet, email, or internal video platforms. NDAs and access restrictions apply as needed.
How quickly do you deliver final event videos? 
Recap highlights are typically delivered within 5-7 business days. Full session recordings within 3 days. Same-day social clips are delivered during the event. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive post-event marketing campaigns.
Do you bring your own audio/visual equipment? 
We bring all necessary video, lighting, and audio equipment. For events with existing A/V setups, we coordinate with the venue&#x27;s technical team to tap into their systems for the best audio quality. We&#x27;re compatible with all major A/V platforms.
