HELLO, HELLO!
I'm Kaycee.
 I'm a boutique portrait photographer, but more than that, I'm a parent.
 This means I understand how important your children are to you and how quickly time passes. As a photographer for over 18 years, I know the tricks to put everyone at ease, make them look great in front of the camera, and how to entertain your kiddos and significant other. Most importantly, I make the experience FUN! I love taking the pressure off of you so that all you have to do is have fun and enjoy your family.
