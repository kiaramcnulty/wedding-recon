PLAN FOR SUCCESS
Let's Chat
Schedule a consultation call with me so I can get to know you and your partner, what's most important to you, and the vision you have for your unique wedding experience. 
Let's Get to Know One Another
We'll schedule your engagement photo session, which offers a completely unique experience from your wedding day; 30 days before your wedding, we'll meet up via Zoom to finalize your timeline and confirm all the important details.
We Hold Your Hand Through the Entire Process
Helping you with your timeline, finding out which portraits are the most important to you two, to designing your album. Whether you're creating a beautiful one-day event or an epic wedding weekend, I'll be there to capture every incredible moment as you celebrate your love for each other and make lifelong memories with the people you love most.
What Happens Next
Within two to four weeks, I'll deliver your much-anticipated wedding photos&hellip;and each time you share your heirloom wedding album - which is included in each collection - or enjoy the wedding portraits displayed in your home, you'll relive the best day of your life in stunning detail and feel the love of your favorite people coming through each photo
