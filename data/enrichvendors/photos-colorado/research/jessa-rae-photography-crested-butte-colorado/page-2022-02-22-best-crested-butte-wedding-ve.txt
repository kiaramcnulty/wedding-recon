Best Crested Butte Wedding Venues
February 22, 2022
Are you looking for the perfect outdoor wedding venue to host your Crested Butte Wedding? You&#8217;ve probably come across many websites that claim to provide Crested Butte venues, but actually suggest venues that are no t in Crested Butte. I promise you these venues are all in Crested Butte and I think you&#8217;ll find what you&#8217;re looking for. 
Ten Peaks and Umbrella Bar 
Would you like to get married on a ski slope? You can do just that in Crested Butte! Here are a few reasons why this venue is one of my absolute favorites. Firstly, Ten Peaks provides one of the best views of &#8216;the Butte&#8217; and Gothic valley. Secondly, the venue can accommodate a large tent. And Thirdly, the Umbrella bar has a full service bar! All of these features make it ideal for larger weddings.
Ten Peaks 
Crested Butte Mountain Wedding Garden 
This is a gorgeous little venue with a beautiful garden area surrounded by a variety of flowers, bushes and trees. There is also a nice pavilion that can be used for the reception. It&#8217;s a really great venue. In fact, it&#8217;s such a great venue that I had my own wedding here!
Mountain Wedding Garden 
Crested Butte Land Trust 
The Crested Butte Land Trust offers three fabulous outdoor wedding venues, all surrounded by gorgeous mountains. You can choose from Peanut Lake, Woods Walk or Gunsight Bridge. Good luck choosing!
CB Land Trust 
Rocky Mountain Biological Laboratory
Rocky Mountain Biological Laboratory is only available for weddings in September and October. Thankfully, Crested Butte is amazing in the fall! If you are wanting an autumn wedding in one of the most unique and stunning locations in Colorado, check this out! 
RMBL 
Town Ranch
Town Ranch is a beautiful venue in a field right in the heart of downtown Crested Butte. It is surrounded by mountain views and easily walkable from Elk Avenue or anywhere else in town. 
Town Ranch
