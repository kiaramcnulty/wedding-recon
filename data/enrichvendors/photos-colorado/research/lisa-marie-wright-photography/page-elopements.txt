Intimate Elopements, Elegantly Captured
Telluride, Colorado Elopements
Your Telluride elopement is more than a ceremony — it’s a once-in-a-lifetime experience, designed with intention, beauty, and meaning. Whether you’re exchanging vows on a mountaintop, tucked away in an alpine meadow, or beside the dramatic peaks that define Telluride, this day deserves to feel effortless and unforgettable.
You’re choosing a celebration that’s deeply personal — a day focused on connection, adventure, and the quiet magic of being fully present together. And while eloping can feel simple in theory, planning a breathtaking Telluride elopement can still come with a surprising amount of pressure.
That’s where I come in — not only to photograph your day with timeless elegance, but to help you feel calm, confident, and cared for every step of the way.
The essence of your elopement captured with timeless elegance
At the heart of your Telluride elopement is the story you’re beginning together — the promises you’re making, the adventure you’re stepping into, and the legacy you’re building. You deserve photographs that don’t just document what happened, but preserve how it felt.
Images that reflect your connection: passionate yet tender, elevated yet natural, intentional yet effortless.
Your elopement deserves to be remembered not for its simplicity, but for its beauty — the wind in your hair on a mountain ridge, the quiet smiles exchanged before your vows, the way the San Juan peaks glow around you at golden hour, and the deep joy that settles into every moment.
My goal is to create a luxury photography experience where you feel fully present, deeply celebrated, and truly seen — so your images feel timeless for decades to come.

Once you’ve chosen your date, I’ll send over a proposal with your custom package details and a booking link. A signed contract and retainer officially reserve your date — and then the fun begins as I plan your timeline and photography details together.
What happens next if we’d like to book?
You can expect around 600–800 carefully edited images from a full wedding day. A sneak peek gallery is delivered within a few weeks, and your full gallery arrives within 8–10 weeks, ready to download and share.
How many images will we receive, and when?
One hundred percent. Most of my couples aren’t models — they’re real people who just want to feel relaxed and enjoy the day. I offer gentle direction and focus on helping you connect with each other so the images feel authentic and full of life.
Will you help us feel comfortable in front of the camera?
My approach blends editorial composition with genuine emotion — a mix of timeless portraits and candid, unposed moments. I work primarily with natural light to create soft, luminous imagery that feels effortless and refined.
How would you describe your photography style?
Absolutely. I photograph destination weddings and elopements across Colorado, the U.S., and Europe — including Telluride, the San Juan Mountains, and beyond. Travel logistics are customized for each celebration, and I make the process seamless from start to finish.
