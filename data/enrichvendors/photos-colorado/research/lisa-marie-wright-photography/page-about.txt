ALL ABOUT Lisa marie Based in Telluride, Colorado, I photograph weddings throughout the San Juan Mountains, Beaver Creek, Vail, Aspen, Colorado and surrounding alpine destinations. 
From intimate elopements to refined multi-day celebrations, my work is inspired by the natural grandeur, light, and sense of place that make Telluride weddings so unforgettable.
Telluride Wedding Photographer for Elegant Mountain Celebrations
My clients are ambitious, adventurous, and visionary. They appreciate refined details, world travel, natural beauty, and the quiet thrill of bringing an extraordinary celebration to life.
I am here to transform your wedding photography dreams into an exquisite reality. As a Telluride local, I understand how to highlight the dramatic majesty of the San Juan Mountains while preserving the calm, intimate atmosphere that makes mountain weddings so unforgettable.
Your life—and your love story—are nothing short of exceptional. It is a true privilege to document them with intention, artistry, and care.
