Your Telluride wedding is more than a celebration — it’s an experience set against some of the most breathtaking mountain landscapes in Colorado. From intimate mountaintop ceremonies to refined resort weddings in Telluride, Mountain Village, and the surrounding San Juan Mountains, your day deserves to be documented with artistry, intention, and timeless elegance.
I specialize in luxury Telluride weddings, capturing refined celebrations with an editorial eye and a deeply personal approach. Every image is crafted to feel effortless, emotive, and enduring — preserving not just how your wedding looked, but how it felt.

Telluride, Colorado Luxury Weddings
Your wedding day is a whirlwind of anticipation and emotion—exhilarating, beautiful, and undeniably full. Between final timelines, family expectations, luxury details, and the quiet pressure for every moment to unfold perfectly, it’s easy for the day to move faster than you can truly take in. 
You want the atmosphere to feel graceful, intentional, and refined… yet the pace and the weight of the moment can make you wonder if you’ll be able to fully experience it all.
The Beauty of Your Day Captured with Timeless Elegance 
You value excellence, legacy, and meaning, and you’ve curated a celebration that reflects exactly that. But beneath the polished surface, there’s a quiet desire for the world to slow down—just enough for you to breathe it in, to feel anchored, and to remember how extraordinary this day truly is.
Because at the heart of this moment, you’re not just hosting a wedding—you’re beginning a marriage built on partnership, passion, and shared purpose. You envision photographs that are elevated, honest, and deeply emotive. Images that don’t just document the events, but reveal the essence of who you are together: powerful yet tender, confident yet connected, timeless yet full of life.
You deserve to remember your wedding day not for its pace or pressure, but for its elegance—the laughter, the tears, the quiet, treasured moments, and the radiant joy that passes in an instant. Your photography experience should honor that elegance… allowing you to be fully present, deeply understood, and exquisitely celebrated.

What happens next if we’d like to book?
You can expect around 750–1000 carefully edited images from a full wedding day. A sneak peek gallery is delivered within a week, and your full gallery arrives within 8–10 days, ready to download and share.
