Picturesque Photography specializes in outdoor portraits of families and couples — Locally owned and operated since 2019
Based in picturesque Telluride, Colorado
Hi, I'm Wren Lupine!
 I am a Telluride local photographer and I love exploring my backyard in the San Juan Mountains, chasing sunsets and the best views.
 Picturesque Photography specializes in outdoor mountain adventure portraits - celebrating love, families and milestone moments in the gorgeous landscapes of Western Colorado! My goal as your photographer is to capture the energy and emotions of the day, while encouraging playfulness and laughter with an organic, natural approach to posing.
4x4 adventure photography sessions — taking you to local spots with the most picturesque backdrops in the Telluride area. 
Our packages include all of your photos delivered in a modern online gallery; to easily share on social media, order professional quality prints and allow download access for friends and family.
Portrait sessions are typically 1-2 hours, mid-morning or before sunset (depending on location) for the best lighting.
We offer styling and outfit inspiration for every season and professional insight on how to look and feel your best for your shoot!
We are located in Telluride, Colorado but can travel to Ridgway, Ouray and surrounding mountain areas.

Our story
 Raised in the southwest desert of Tucson, Arizona - I moved to San Diego after graduating college in Northern Arizona with my Bachelor’s degree in Photography. After 6 years of shooting weddings across the California coast, I left the beach life behind and moved to the mountains. In 2017, I relocated to Denver and a few months later I met my husband Corey. In 2018 we moved to Telluride and never looked back! Our favorite activities include skiing, hiking, boating and playing with our golden retriever Finn. Our latest adventure is parenthood and raising two little girls in the mountains.
We love to meet people who share our passion for adventure and document the moments that add meaning to our lives.
