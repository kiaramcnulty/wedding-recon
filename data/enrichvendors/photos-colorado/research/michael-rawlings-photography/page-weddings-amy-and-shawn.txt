Steamboat Springs Wedding &#8211; Amy and Shawn
IslBG 
Ski Town Sweet 
 Date: September 25, 2017
Audience: About 60 people
Attendants:  Six bridesmaids, six groomsmen
Location: Yampa River Botanic Park in Steamboat Springs, Colorado | Reception was in the Olympian Hall at the base of the ski jumps at the Howelsen Hill Ski Area in Steamboat.
Early in the morning, the sky was overcast with occasional light rain. The ceremony started at 11am and the couple wanted nearly all of the photos completed before the ceremony. After photographing Amy getting ready indoors, she went outside to meet Shawn for a First Look. We then walked around the Park, visiting various predetermined locations. Similar to the Betty Ford Alpine Garden in Vail, the Yampa River Botanic Park is beautifully scenic with stone paths, waterfalls and an obvious abundance of trees, bushes, and flowers. The highlight of the park is the huge grassy area next to a small pond - perfect for weddings. It was the first time I had photographed a wedding there, so that added to my excitement. Shortly after I finished photos of the bride and groom and before we were to start the posed photos of family and friends, the sun broke through the clouds and it remained sunny the rest of the day.
Getting the reflection in the pond of the ceremony was another highlight, as well as the fun, easy-going nature of the couple, the wedding party and the family.
The schedule also allowed me to spend some time with the bride's 100-year-old grandfather, a World War II veteran.
