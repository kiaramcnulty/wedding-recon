Have we met? I’m Alton. 
I split my time between Boulder & Fort Collins, Colorado. 
I’m a climber, skier, fly fisherman, film photographer and veteran vanlifer.
 I grew up in California, skateboarding of course and eventually found my way to Colorado, camera in hand. I’ve spent the last two decades in the mountains and it’s shaped how I engage with the world; how I see people and moments unfolding.
 I’m not here to stage your wedding, I’m here to document it honestly. If you connect with my work then I’d love to connect with you.
 Available for weddings & elopements in Colorado, Wyoming, New Mexico & travel elsewhere.
