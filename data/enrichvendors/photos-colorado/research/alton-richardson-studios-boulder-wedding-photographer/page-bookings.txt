What are your rates? 
Local (Front Range Area) photography coverage starts at $2 200 and Elopement/Micro Wedding coverage starts at $900. I also have options for engagement and couples sessions. Reach out via my contact page to learn more.
 *Alton Richardson Studios believes in supporting local communities by offering a “per couple” discount to active Military, K-12 educators & First Responders. Please reach out for more information.
What's your approach? 
I operate minimally, often working by myself and with a minimal amount of gear. It’s important that the experience of being photographed is not only comfortable and fun, but that it also feels natural. No mess of gear everywhere and repeated poses over and over. I like to work quickly when needed and maximize your time with your guests. 
Do you have reviews? 
Yes, on the main wedding vendor sites like The Knot , Wedding Wire & Zola as well as on Google , which is where most of of my reviews are. 
More about Booking 
After we’ve had a phone or video call to determine if we’d be a good fit to work together, you’ll receive a custom proposal from me. This will be an online payment portal where you can view package options, sign the contract and make your deposit and additional payments via credit card or ACH through a secure portal system.
