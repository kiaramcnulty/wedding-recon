Every important moment captured.
Much of the body of work that I present both within the gallery below & on social media, reflects the images that I feel best represent my current work as well as excite me the most. Symmetry & intentional composition paired with the use of in-camera techniques like reflections, rings of fire and double exposures.
 However these creative elements within my work simply serve as a bridge. To the sometimes chaotic, often emotional and always a ‘damn good time’ moments that make up each and every wedding day.
 Your day is just as unique as you are. And your pictures should be too.
