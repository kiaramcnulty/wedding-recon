ABOUT THE PHOTOGRAPHER
Meet Jolie
Hi there, I'm Jolie Rodriguez, a family and senior photographer in Denver, Colorado.
 My passion for photography came to be when as a young mother of 4 my husband died suddenly. We were lost and looking for something to make us whole again. Little did I know that booking our family pictures would put us on the path of healing.
 The pictures from that day showed me that we were still a family, we were strong and we could move forward. I still look at those pictures today and remember our unbreakable bond. My experience has kindled a passion within me for helping families create beautiful family portraits to remember their journey and strengthen their family.
