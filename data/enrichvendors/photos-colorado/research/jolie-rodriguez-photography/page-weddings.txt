MEET OUR WEDDING SPECIALIST 
 Elisa Fisher
 Associate Photographer
Hey there, nice to e-meet you! My specialty in photography is elopements and weddings. I remember being asked as a five-year-old what I wanted to be when I grew up, and I said, “an artist!” 
 That desire stuck with me, and in 2012 I received my BFA in Studio Art with a Photography Emphasis. My work comes from an artistic and conceptual background, but I also work hard to capture classic and timeless shots and special candid moments. It is truly an honor for me to photograph the big and the small moments during a couple’s special day. 
Read more 
I am originally from Dallas, Texas but my husband and I moved to Colorado in 2021 and absolutely love it! We enjoy the activities and the lifestyle that the mountains bring. Besides being a photographer, I am many other things… traveler, foodie, yogi, sarcasm-master, music enthusiast, hiker, cat-mom, plant lover, and more. 
 I look forward to meeting you and creating images that you will treasure forever!
