JOLIE RODRIGUEZ PHOTOGRAPHY 
 Who We Are 
NICE TO MEET YOU
Hello, I’m Jolie!
My passion for photography came to be when as a young mother of 4 my husband died suddenly. We were lost and looking for something to make us whole again. Little did I know that booking our family pictures would put us on the path of healing.
 The pictures from that day showed me that we were still a family, we were strong and we could move forward. I still look at those pictures today and remember our unbreakable bond. My experience has kindled a passion within me for helping families create beautiful family portraits to remember their journey and strengthen their family.
 I am the girl behind the camera. I love creating art that is meaningful to my clients. My passion thrives on capturing moments, emotions, and connections through photography. I am a lover of the mountains – hiking is my favorite. Family comes first for me and I’m a Mama to many. My husband also lost his wife and we combined our families together and now have 9 kids! I am most at peace with a good hot cocoa and a walk in the woods. I would love to meet you and see if we are a good fit.
Associates
 Sometimes it happens - Jolie's already booked for the date you need. Enter my associates, Rebecca Berra, and Elisa Fisher!
 Rebecca and Elisa are consummate professionals and I know you will love working with them as much as I do. Now when you book a session with us, you get the choice of three photographers!
YOUNG FAMILIES & CHILDREN
Rebecca Berra
Hello! Ever since my dad gave me a camera and a roll of Kodak film as a child, I have loved photography! From developing black & white pictures of friends in the darkroom in England to making family albums from travels around the world, I cherish the memories captured by my cameras over the years.
 I couldn't be happier to share this lifelong passion with family, friends, and clients in sunny Colorado, my home for over ten years now. The prairie grasses dotted with flowers in the summer, the rocky canyons, and yellow aspens in the fall, and the mountains covered in pine trees and snow in winter and spring all provide beautiful backdrops for portraits.
 Let's go make memories together!
ELOPEMENTS & WEDDINGS
Elisa Fisher
Hey there, nice to meet you! I remember being asked as a five-year-old what I wanted to be when I grew up, and I said, "an artist!"
 That desire stuck with me, and in 2012 I received my BFA in Studio Art with a Photography Emphasis. My work comes from an artistic and conceptual background, but I also work hard to capture classic and timeless shots and special candid moments. It is truly an honor for me to photograph the big and the small moments during a couple's special day.
 I am originally from Dallas, Texas but my husband and I moved to Colorado in 2021 and absolutely love it! We enjoy the activities and the lifestyle that the mountains bring.
 Besides being a photographer, I am many other things... traveler, foodie, yogi, sarcasm-master, music enthusiast, hiker, cat-mom, plant lover, and more.
 I look forward to meeting you and creating images that you will treasure forever!
PROFESSIONAL STYLIST
Lauren Levin
Excited for your upcoming family photoshoot but feeling overwhelmed by the thought of what to wear? I get it!
 As a mom of four, I understand the challenges of coordinating family outfits.
 I specialize in creating harmonious looks that reflect each individual's personality while creating a cohesive and beautiful look for your photos. I can help you utilize pieces you already own or find new additions to make sure everyone looks and feels their best.
 Let me take the stress out of photoshoot prep so you can focus on making memories that will last a lifetime.
 I'm excited to help you look and feel your best!
