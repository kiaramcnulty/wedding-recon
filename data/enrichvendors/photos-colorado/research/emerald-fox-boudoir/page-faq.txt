Because I want you to know EVERYTHING
It&#8217;s totally understandable to have questions going into this process!
Whether you&#8217;ve had boudoir sessions before, or are a total newbie, this list will help get you familiar with how things work at my studio.
If you don&#8217;t see what you&#8217;re looking for here, don&#8217;t worry! Head over to the contact page, and pop me your question there. I&#8217;ll get back to you as soon as I can!

Do I need to bring my own clothes? 
Nope! I have a client closet available to you with lingerie pieces from in size small to 5X. Bring a black or a nude thong to wear underneath my garments. You are definitely allowed to bring your own garments though! For inspiration on what to wear, check out my blog: https://www.emeraldfoxpinup.com/blog/whattowear
Can I bring props? 
Yes! Bring anything that you can see yourself incorporating into your session &#8211; cowboy hat/boots, crystals, His shirt, a special cup, anything that you can think up!
What sort of products do you offer?
Albums, glass heirloom boxes, metal wall art, calendar and digital images! For complete pricing information, schedule a phone consult with me HERE .
Do I need to know how to pose? 
Absolutely not!!! I will tell you what to do, where to put your toes, your fingers, everything! All you have to do is show-up!
Do I do my own hair and make-up?
Nope! We bring in a professional artist dedicated to making you feel and look gorgeous!
Do you offer payment plans? 
Yes! We have VERY flexible payment plans, actually! They start at just $50/month and you will actually get to receive all your products and images  before you are paid in full! 
I don't think I want my photos shared, will you keep them private? 
Of course! I take client privacy very seriously and will only share images if I have gotten express permission. If you prefer to keep them private, I am more than happy to keep them that way.
Do I need to get naked? 
Nope! I shoot in everything from flow-y dresses and chunky sweaters to fully nude! I am completely happy with whatever you are comfortable with and will never ask you to go beyond that place.
The women on your website are absolutely stunning and look like models. There is no way that I'll look that. 
Believe it or not, this is my most commonly asked question! Many of these women featured here have said the same thing! I hear so often, &#8220;I need to lose 20 pounds before my shoot.&#8221; or &#8220;I&#8217;m not pretty enough for boudoir.&#8221;, or even, &#8220;I&#8221;m ugly.&#8221; I&#8217;m here to tell you that boudoir is for EVERY women. There is not best body type, best skin color or age for boudoir. I want to show you that you are, just like these other women who had the same though-process, beautiful. You are beautiful. Let me show you.
Do you have terms and conditions?
Yes. The most important is our no-show, no-call policy. We are very flexible on rescheduling, but If you can no longer attend your session, please be courteous and let us know.
The Experience
My images are full of dramatic lighting and have a more sensual feel. I have an entire pose flow that I go through with each of my clients and I personally demonstrate each pose for my clients myself first &#8211; which is hilarious, since I&#8217;m in my comfy shooting clothes! And then walk my client through the pose stey-by-step when it is their turn! I especially give direction on facial expression. I&#8217;m not gonna look at someone and say, &#8220;now&#8230; be sexy!&#8221; and leave it at that! I will go through how to shape your lips, your eyes, everything!
