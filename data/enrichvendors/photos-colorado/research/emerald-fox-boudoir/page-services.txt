I offer two types of sessions
But you can always go with both, so you don&#8217;t have to choose!
I offer two different types of sessions: Bridal, and boudoir. The photos that result from these sessions are totally different &#8211; but the feelings and process during each are pretty similar.
Boudoir gallery 
Boudoir Photography 
Boudoir photography at Emerald Fox is all about the chance to realize you are beautiful! It&#8217;s a chance to see yourself through the lens of someone who loves you for who YOU are &#8211; like your spouse or best friend!
Oftentimes, women tend to look in the mirror and instantly see their flaws. A boudoir session is the chance to see yourself in a new light!
Each boudoir session includes professional hair and make-up, access to our client closet, 60 &#8211; 90 minutes of shooting time, pose and facial expression coaching and professional retouching.
Bridal Gallery 
Bridal  Photography 
Bridal boudoir photography is the perfect way to celebrate your beauty and confidence before your big day. It’s a chance to create a meaningful, intimate gift for your partner while capturing this special moment in your life.
Many brides choose a bridal boudoir session to feel empowered, radiant, and connected to their wedding journey. With soft, romantic styling and elegant details, your photos will reflect both your personality and bridal glow.
Each bridal boudoir session includes professional hair and make-up, access to our client closet, 60 – 90 minutes of shooting time, pose and expression guidance, and professional retouching. From delicate lace to personalized themes, we bring your vision to life in a timeless, beautiful way.
Emerald Fox Boudoir
At Emerald Fox, we believe every women deserves to feel confident, beautiful, and empowered. Our boudoir and bridal boudoir photography sessions are designed to be fun, personalized, and stress-free, whether it’s your first experience or your fifth.
