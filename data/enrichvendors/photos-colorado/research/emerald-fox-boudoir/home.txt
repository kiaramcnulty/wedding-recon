Frequently Asked Questions   
Do I need modeling experience?
Absolutely not!!! Most of our clients have never done anything like this before. We guide you through every pose and expression, so you’ll always know exactly what to do.
How do I book a session?
You can book by submitting the contact form on our website. After that, we’ll schedule a consultation call to go over details, answer questions, and secure your session date with a retainer fee.
What is included in the session fee? 
Your session fee includes professional hair and makeup, guided posing, studio time, and your Image Reveal appointment. Images and products are purchased separately.
How long does a session last?
Most sessions last approximately 2–3 hours, including hair and makeup. This allows time for multiple outfits, set changes, and a relaxed, unrushed experience.
When will I receive my final images?
After your ordering appointment and product selections are finalized, digital images and printed products are typically delivered within a few weeks, depending on your order.
Can I bring a friend or partner?
You’re welcome to bring a supportive friend if it helps you feel comfortable. We do ask that guests step out during the actual shooting time. They are more than welcome to hang out during hair and make-up and come back with you for your Image Reveal.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. We offer flexible payment options for image collections and products to make your investment more manageable.
What if I need to reschedule?
We understand that life happens. Rescheduling requests must be made within the   timeframe   outlined in your contract. Retainer fees are non-refundable, but may be transferred to a new date, if you make that request with enough time before your session.
