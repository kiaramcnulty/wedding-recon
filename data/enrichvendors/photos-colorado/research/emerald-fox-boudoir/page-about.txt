Annette Hullender 
You might be feeling a little nervous right now — most of my clients are. The idea of posing in lingerie (or even just being seen) can feel intimidating. But here’s what I can promise you: the awkwardness doesn’t last.
I guide you every step of the way — from hair and makeup, to choosing your outfits, to showing you exactly how to pose and what facial expressions to make. And somewhere between the first click and your reveal, something shifts. You start to see what I see: confidence, power, beauty — all yours.
That transformation? That’s why I do this.
I’m Annette, and while I have a background in music, accounting, and children’s theatre (yes, really), I found my true calling when I picked up a camera in 2018 and started photographing women like you — women who are ready to step into a version of themselves they didn’t know was possible.
Your session isn’t just about beautiful photos — though you’ll absolutely have those. It’s about remembering how it felt to see yourself that way. It’s fun. It’s freeing. And it’s unforgettable.
I can’t wait to show you what everyone else already sees in you.

Ali Watkins 
“Wow, that doesn’t even look like me!” 
That is the most common phrase I hear when showing women their boudoir photos. To that I say, the beautiful, powerful and unique woman in that photo is you! 
Unlike other art mediums, a photograph can only be of what’s in front of the camera. That authenticity has resonated with me since I was a kid running around with a disposable camera. 
My favorite part about boudoir is before I even click the shutter; It’s getting to know your story, `and how I can guide you towards the self-expression you envision.  
No one is unphotogenic, and I can’t be convinced otherwise! Whether you are a seasoned model or a photoshoot firstcomer, we all just need a little direction. 
About me 
I’m a Colorado-raised boudoir and wedding photographer. I started my portrait photography journey over 10 years ago. It led me to working for local newspapers, opening a wedding photography business and joining the Emerald Fox team! 
Whenever I’m not in the boudoir studio I’m most likely spoiling my cats Oliver and Wednesday, playing table top games, exploring our beautiful state and/or overly caffeinated.

Sophia Woodward 
I’m Sophia — a boudoir photographer who believes that this experience isn’t about becoming someone different. It’s about remembering who you already are. When you walk into the studio, my goal is to make sure you feel seen, supported, and completely at ease. You deserve to take up space, to feel powerful, and to feel beautiful without apology.
As someone in the LGBTQ+ community myself, I understand what it means to step into a space where you truly feel safe and affirmed. Here, you don’t have to perform or hide parts of yourself. You get to be real. You get to be you.
During your session, I’ll guide you through every pose and expression so you never have to wonder what to do. You’ll be cared for, encouraged, and celebrated throughout the entire process — because this experience is about more than photos. It’s about stepping into your confidence and seeing yourself through a kinder, more loving lens.
