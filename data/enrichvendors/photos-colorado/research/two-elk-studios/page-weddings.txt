WEDDINGS AND ELOPEMENTS 
​ 
At Two Elk Studios weddings, we believe that the only true way to photograph a couple authentically is by discovering the real reasons why they are making this commitment to each other. Seeing each individual through the other's eyes allows us to capture what really matters. How could we NOT photograph you with more passion if we knew exactly what it is you love so much about your partner? That look they give you that says "Everything will be OK." The way they always make you laugh when you've had a rough day at work. How when they hold you in their arms, nothing else matters. For your Vail wedding photography, we will take you on a transformational journey in which we will celebrate the connection between you and make you smile along the way.

COLOR STYLES 
While we have a signature style, we understand that certain venues lend themselves better to a different look. Because of this, we offer a few alternatives you can select from if desired. Read the descriptions below and check out the examples to get an idea. At your pre-wedding questionnaire, you will tell us your selection so we edit your wedding photographs as desired.
*UNDER CONSTRUCTION! 
SIGNATURE STYLE 
Our Signature Style is a modern interpretation of film photography. It provides a depth of character and evokes an organic feeling with muted colors and film grain. The shadows and blacks are a bit faded to provide less of a "digital" look. Natural greens are pushed towards a lovely sage color and skin tones remain warm and bright. This is our standard style seen in our portfolios and is the one to go with if you don't have a strong preference towards something different.
MODERN BOLD STYLE 
The Modern Style is colorful and bright, with saturated colors and a good amount of contrast. Shadows are black and highlights are white. This style attempts to more accurately reproduce the colors and tones we see with our eyes. If you are looking to bring out bright, saturated Fall colors in Aspen trees, for example, this style may be right for you.
MOOD STYLE 
The Mood Style is an alternative take on our Signature Style that is warm, organic, boho, and wanderlust.
