Frequently Asked Questions
 FAQs
 Hablas espanól?
&#8211; Sí! Yo aprendo espanól. Es no muy bien pero ayuda mi a servirte y tu familia mexicana mucho mejor! 
Which package is right for me? 
&#8211; Weddings over 50 people I suggest the Party Hard Package
&#8211; Weddings under 30 people I suggest the Keep It Simple Package
&#8211; Intimate weddings with less than 20 people, I suggest the Elopement Package with custom a la carte additional hours added to fit your timeline
&#8211; Elopements with just the couple, the Elopement package is a great start, and we can always add hours of coverage if needed
On average how many edited images will I get upon delivery? 
&#8211; Wedding days 500+ high resolution images
&#8211; Elopements 150+ high resolution images
&#8211; Adventure session 80+ high resolution images
&#8211; Local session 50+ high resolution images
Delivery times vary depending on the time of year, and I always strive to deliver as soon as possible. All weddings and sessions are delivered within 16 weeks.
Can I print my photos anywhere I want? 
&#8211; Yes, absolutely. You have printing rights. I also offer an automated gallery store with a multitude of products for easy ordering straight from your gallery, or you can take your photos to any online or in-person store. 
Are there any other fees? 
&#8211; Nope. All my travel fees come out of the package price. If you have a wedding outside the state of Colorado I suggest the Party Hard for the western USA, or the Whole Freakin Weekend Package for eastern USA, Canada &#038; Mexico. For Europe or South America I suggest the Worldwide Package. 
May I schedule an engagement session also? 
&#8211; Absolutely! An engagement session is a fun way to capture the fiancé chapter, get photos for your announcements, and get to know me before your wedding. If you book any full day wedding package your engagement session will be included or discounted. Any engagement sessions booked individually are $800. 
What if I want both color and black and white images? 
&#8211; All galleries are delivered in color, with some images in black and white at my artistic discretion (average 10% of the gallery). You may add a full black and white gallery to any package for $600. 
Can I have all the raw unedited images? 
&#8211; Yes, after you sign a release agreement and pay the Raw Image Release Fee. The Raw Image Release Fee is $2000. Once the release is signed and fee is paid, I will load all of your RAW files onto a quality external disk and insured mail it to you.
Do I have to sign a contract and is there a retainer fee to shoot with you? 
&#8211; Yes, there is a straight-forward contract to sign, and a $1000 retainer to hold your wedding, elopement, or engagement date. Couples or family sessions require a 50% deposit ($400 for Adventure Sessions, $300 Local Sessions).
