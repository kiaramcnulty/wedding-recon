Frequently Asked Questions
What size weddings do you photograph? 
I mostly photograph weddings with under 100 guests, but I don&#8217;t have a hard limit. If you&#8217;re excited to have everyone there, I&#8217;m excited to photograph them! 
What if we don&#8217;t have a date or venue yet? 
While I can&#8217;t book weddings without first having a date for our contract, I&#8217;m happy to share my available dates if you want to make sure our schedules match up. 
You should also lock down a venue before booking any other vendors—except for a wedding planner, who can help you find the right venue.
Do you charge travel fees? 
I include travel within a 2h drive from Boulder, CO in all my collections. For locations further away, please reach out for a custom quote as travel pricing varies by both location and season. 
How will you help us with planning? 
I help my couples with photo locations and creating a photo timeline for the day, along with any other photography-related questions you may have.
I am not a wedding planner though, and highly recommend that you hire someone who can help you create a budget, book vendors, create a full timeline for every part of the day, coordinate your vendors on the day of the wedding, get everyone lined up for the ceremony, help transition the venue from dinner to dancing, and a million other things that make wedding planners magicians.
If you haven&#8217;t already booked a planner, I&#8217;d love to share my favorites with you!
How do we book you? 
Get in touch via my contact form , and tell me where and when you&#8217;re getting married. If I’m available on your date and within your budget, we’ll schedule a video call to talk more about your plans and how I can help you. This part is super important! The more comfortable you are around your photographer, the better your photos will be, so let&#8217;s make sure we&#8217;re a good fit before you book.
If you decide to book, a signed contract and $3,000 non-refundable initial payment will save your date and make it official. 
Do you shoot film or digital? 
I primarily photograph weddings on digital cameras, but I also use an Instax camera and a 35mm film camera for highlight photos throughout the day. Because film is unpredictable, I don&#8217;t guarantee a set number of film photos—but if you love film as much as I do, tell me and I&#8217;ll take as many as I can. You can read more about my approach to film photography here .
When do we get our photos? 
You&#8217;ll get 5-10 sneak peek photos within two days after your wedding, and your full gallery will be ready within six weeks.
Your photos are delivered in a digital gallery that you can easily share with family and friends. You can download your photos as many times as you want, as high resolution and web resolution files without watermarks. 
How many photos will we get? 
The exact number of photos will depend on how many hours I&#8217;m there for, and what&#8217;s happening throughout the day, but around 50-70 photos per hour of coverage is normal. You&#8217;ll get all the good stuff, without any unflattering photos or lots of duplicates of the same thing.
How do you use AI in your business? 
I will never use AI to cull or edit your photos, and every word on this website and my social media was written by myself. Many photographers tout AI as a way to speed up their workflow, but I take a lot of pride in doing the work myself. 
Do you offer prints and albums? 
An heirloom wedding album is included with most of my photography collections. You can order high quality prints, wall art, cards, and more directly from your online gallery. All products ordered from your gallery come from my preferred printing labs, and are shipped directly to your home.
You&#8217;ll also get a print release that allows you to print your photos anywhere you&#8217;d like, but I can&#8217;t guarantee the colors and quality on any prints and products ordered outside of your gallery shop.
