Your Personal Local Expert
 Colorado and Utah are more than just beautiful backdrops for me; they&#8217;re home. The mountains and deserts play host to my everyday adventures, making me the local you need. Whether it&#8217;s hiking in the Rockies or exploring the red rock canyons of Utah, I&#8217;ve got the experience to lead you through both the natural wonders and the perfect shots.
 Although I’m not originally from Colorado, the mountains called to me and now I call them home. I grew up living that small town life in Southeastern Pennsylvania – a little place you’ve never heard of called Fawn Grove. There wasn’t a single traffic light and I lived off of a dirt road. I spent my childhood playing in the woods, digging in the dirt, exploring creeks, and never wearing shoes! Adventure was always a natural state of being to me, and to this day I still prefer being barefoot.
 This business has become so much more than a job and I feel truly honored to call so many clients my friends. It would be a pleasure to work with you and your soon-to-be spouse as we plan and execute your dream elopement in the places I now call home.
 Hover to learn more Colorado isn't just where I live; it's where dreams take flight. My first visit in 2016 was a pivotal moment. The breathtaking landscapes and outdoor adventures called to me, and ever since then —Colorado has my heart.
 What keeps me anchored here? The sheer beauty, thrilling slopes, and the joy of sharing this state with incredible couples. Together, we explore its wonders and create lasting memories amid picturesque landscapes.
Moving to Grand Junction, CO just 30 miles from the Utah border, was a deliberate choice to be closer to Moab. The sheer grandeur of Utah's pristine landscapes is so encompassing that I often find myself yearning to become one with it, to wither away and be a part of this breathtaking tapestry forever. It's a feeling I've never had elsewhere—a feeling that the desert, with its indescribable beauty, evokes in me. It's as if the landscape itself beckons me to join its eternal dance.
