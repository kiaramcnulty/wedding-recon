CAROLINE & DAVID
Time of Year: First weekend of September
Venues: Allred’s Private Dining Room, Tomboy Tavern, San Sophia Overlook & Crystal Room Combination
Guest Count: Rehearsal dinner – 24 / Welcome Party, Ceremony & Reception – 100
View Vendor & Venue Details 
This fun-loving couple from Boulder, CO gathered their friends and family in Telluride the first week of September. They kicked off the celebration with an intimate rehearsal dinner at the Allred’s Private Dining Room with their closest family members. From there, they took the gondola to Tomboy Tavern for their Welcome Party where they met the rest of their guests for a Southern-style buffet and customized margarita bar.
The following day, C&D exchanged vows at San Sophia Overlook with a delicate ground arch framing the couple against the spectacular mountain backdrop. It was a warm summer day without a hint of the approaching autumn until sunset brought cool temperatures. Following ceremony, they headed back down the gondola and reconvened at The Peaks Crystal Room Combination for cocktail hour, dinner & dancing.

KELLY & KYLE
Time of Year: Mid-March
Venue: Gorrono Ranch
Guest Count: 115
Theme: Western Chic
View Vendor & Venue Details 
This adventurous couple from Fort Collins, CO opted for a winter wedding at Gorrono Ranch. Blessed with a bluebird day, the sun (and excitement) kept them warm for their first-look photoshoot up at San Sophia Station. While they skied down to their ceremony from the top of the gondola, guests were picked up by an open-air sleigh drawn by a snowcat from the base of the mountain.
K&K said “I do” on the main deck with Mt. Wilson as their backdrop and their furry friends at their side. The group then moved inside for a seated dinner featuring classic Colorado cuisine at tables named after their favorite ski runs in Telluride. With light-up accessories accompanying their dancefloor and photo booth, everyone stayed warm grooving by the fireplace.

KASSIDY & CASSIDY
Time of Year: Last Week of June
Venues: San Sophia Overlook & Tomboy Tavern
Guest Count: 30
Hometown: Nacogdoches, TX
View Vendor & Venue Details 
Kass & Cass proved that an intimate wedding doesn’t mean you have any less fun! On this springy Saturday in late June, everyone rode the gondola to meet at San Sophia Overlook for a mid-afternoon ceremony. Their modern, pyramid-shaped arch mimicked the mountains surrounding the site.
Afterward, they took the gondola down to the heart of Mountain Village where they were met with their favorite cocktails (lavender-gin lemonades & spicy margaritas). K&C and their guests also enjoyed an assortment of passed and stationary appetizers on the patio at Tomboy Tavern. Following their sunny cocktail hour and a celebratory champagne sabrage, guests moved inside for dinner and dancing. They kept their decorations simple, highlighting the already unique furnishings inside Tomboy Tavern, but also made sure everyone got in on the photobooth fun!

ANAM & SETH
Time of Year: Third weekend of September
Venues: San Sophia Overlook & Allred’s
Guest Count: 100
Hometown: Colorado Springs, CO
View Vendor & Venue Details 
Anam & Seth brought their friends and family from all over the world to Telluride to their unique and personalized celebration. They incorporated their mutual love of video games in the table settings and honored Anam’s Indian heritage with the ultimate dance party. Their guests rode the free gondola to San Sophia Station before the short walk to the Overlook for ceremony. Their autumn florals enhanced the vibrancy of the aspen trees starting to change color. After walking back to Allred’s for reception, A&S asked their friend groups to perform their own choreographed dance numbers to kick off the party. Having worked up an appetite, the group enjoyed Allred’s staples of Alaskan halibut and Angus beef tenderloin, along with a custom vegetable curry.

FAWN & TED
Time of Year: First Week of September
Venue: Palmyra Lookout
Guest Count: 60
Hometown: Denver, CO
View Vendor & Venue Details 
This couple brought their loved ones up the mountain to Palmyra Lookout for their intimate Telluride wedding. Embarking on a day of adventure, their guests took the gondola up to meet 4WD Jeeps that would carry them the rest of the way up the mountain road. Just 15 minutes took them to another world all their own – this part of the mountain is closed to the public in summertime. After exchanging their vows, F&D enjoyed the beautiful day with a cocktail hour under the umbrella, offering guests a wide selection of wine, beer, and N/A beverages.

ASHLEY & PAN
Time of Year: First Week of July
Venue: Crystal Room Combination
Guest Count: 90
Hometown: Dallas, TX
View Vendor & Venue Details 
This stylish couple kept everything in one place, with ceremony flowing easily into reception in the Crystal Room Combination at the Peaks Resort. Rental “ghost” chairs and a classic wedding arch brimming with white flowers greeted guests as they headed out to the Mt. Wilson Terrace for ceremony with a flute of champagne in hand. As soon as A&P were announced as husband and wife, trays of Aperol Spritz and Ranch Water appeared with passed canapes on the Terrace.
After a beautiful cocktail hour, everyone was invited into the Legends dining room for a seated dinner to fuel up for an amazing party. Though first dances took place inside the Crystal Room, Mt. Wilson loomed in the background through the floor-to-ceiling windows. The rest of the night included sequin cowboy hats, a Veuve champagne tower, espresso martinis, late night snacks, and some serious dance moves. Many guests headed upstairs to their suites in the hotel while others took the party to town for a nightcap.
