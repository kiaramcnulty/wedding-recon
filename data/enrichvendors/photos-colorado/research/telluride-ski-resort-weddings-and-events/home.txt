REAL TELLURIDE WEDDINGS 
See how other couples tied the knot at our venues! 
ALLRED&#8217;S
Reception venue for up to 130 guests, or 150 with no dance floor
Available June-October (Summer Season) and December-March (Winter Season)
Elevation: 10,551 ft.
Credit: aliciapfaffphotography.com 
 Credit: Jason and Daris Photocinema 
 Credit: Jason and Daris Photocinema 
 Credit: abielivesay.com 
 Credit: lisamariewrightphotography.com 
 Credit: graciemariephoto.com 
Offering culinary excellence and an award-winning wine list, this unique venue provides guests with exquisite views and impressive ambiance. Allred’s is located at the top of a free gondola, accessible from both the Town of Telluride and Mountain Village. The floor-to-ceiling windows showcase views of the 13,000-foot peaks that frame Telluride to the North as well as the twinkling lights and rooftops of the historic town of Telluride 1,800 feet below.
The 12,000 square foot restaurant also features an arched foyer and hallways reminiscent of old European wine cellars with stacked rock walls, custom-designed wine cabinets, and stone fireplaces. For more intimate celebrations, a private dining room with its own fireplace and mountain view is available for groups of up to 30 guests.
GORRONO RANCH
Ceremony & reception venue for up to 200 guests
Available June-October (Summer Season) and December-March (Winter Season)
Elevation: 10,100 ft.
Credit: Jason and Daris Photocinema 
Credit: Jason and Daris Photocinema 
 Credit: Lisa Marie Wright Photography 
 Credit: Lisa Marie Wright Photography

Perched on the side of our mountain, Gorrono Ranch elevates the Western style with wow-factor. Enjoy jaw-dropping views of Mt. Wilson during ceremony or cocktail hour on the ranch’s multiple outdoor decks. Within the main lodge, there are two large dining rooms, cozy stone fireplaces and plenty of space for your band or DJ and dance floor. This venue can accommodate groups of up to 200, but still feels just right with a more intimate crowd of 100 guests. In addition, there are two smaller historic cabins included for late-night lounging, bourbon tasting by the antique wood stove, and lantern-lit romantic photos.

PALMYRA LOOKOUT
Ceremony & cocktail hour venue for up to 150 guests
Available mid-June through September
Elevation: 11,200 ft.

In Telluride, seclusion among the mountains is never too far away. Travel up to a clearing in the forest to make your vows before the resplendent Palmyra Peak. This newly-built ceremony site is located at the top of Lift 5, the Polar Queen Express, and features a natural stone aisle and platform that blends into the scenery.
SAN SOPHIA OVERLOOK
Ceremony venue for up to 250 guests
Available June-October (Summer Season) and December-March (Winter Season)
Elevation: 10,540 ft.
Credit: Jason and Daris Photocinema 
 Credit: Gracie Marie Photo

Accessed by the Gondola and located near the top of Lift 7, this extraordinary mountain-top ceremony site overlooks the Town of Telluride while dazzling with 360-degree views of the surrounding peaks. A short nature path connects the Gondola station to the site. San Sophia offers a flagstone aisle leading to a wooden platform, placed perfectly on the ridge, and a grass amphitheater that can accommodate seating for up to 250 guests. The beauty and majesty of the Overlook cannot be put into words, but it never fails to fill the hearts of wedding guests from around the world.

Reception venue for up to 75 guests seated, 120 guests cocktail

With a slope-side patio and a fully remodeled interior, Tomboy is a contemporary take on the classic tavern. Its character is warm and inviting without sacrificing elegance or quality, and the venue is ideal for welcome parties, rehearsal dinners & intimate wedding receptions.
ALPINO VINO
Ceremony & reception venue for up to 24 guests
Available December-March (Winter Season)
Elevation: 11,966 ft.

Alpino Vino is a European-style chalet restaurant featuring incredible Italian cuisine. Our sommelier team carefully selects the finest wines to pair with each sumptuous course and create an experience that is as unforgettable as Alpino Vino’s location. The restaurant sits at the top of the Telluride Ski Resort; you and your guests will arrive by snowcat-drawn coach, winding up through pine forests on the legendary See Forever ski run. You may choose to hold your ceremony on the Alpino Vino deck as the sun sets behind you over Mt. Wilson, or begin right away with a prosecco toast. On clear nights, the stars light up the white snow outside your cozy reception. This venue is just right for groups of 24 or fewer, making for an intimate and truly unique celebration.
THE CRYSTAL ROOM COMBINATION
Ceremony & reception venue for up to 200 guests
Available June-October (Summer Season) and December-March (Winter Season)

Located at the world-class Peaks Resort & Spa, the Crystal Room Combination features a contemporary aesthetic and versatile indoor-outdoor event capability. Comprised of three distinct but unified spaces, weddings flow effortlessly from the Mt. Wilson Terrace for ceremony and/or cocktail hour, to the Legends Dining Room for a seated or stations-style dinner, to the Crystal Room for dessert and dancing.
For further information regarding weddings and other special events with Telluride Ski & Golf Resort, please contact our Wedding & Events team at (970) 728-7447 or weddings@tellurideskiresort.com
