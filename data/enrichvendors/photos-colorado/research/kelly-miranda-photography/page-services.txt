Frequently Asked Questions
What does my session fee cover? 
The session fee pays for the reservation of your session time and date, the session time, location and outfit planning, and the photographer’s creative fees and artistic talent. It also includes a print/product credit that you can use to purchase wall art, digital files, prints, albums, and more. Contact Kelly for print and product pricing. 
What's the best location? 
There are endless beautiful places in Durango and the surrounding areas. After your book your session, you will receive a link to my curated list of locations with galleries from each location to help guide you to the perfect spot. The location list has suggestions for every season and time of day so you can choose what works best for your session.
What should we wear? 
Contact me to receive my style guide and links to my Pinterest boards for outfit inspiration and color palettes for each season. After you book your session, I will guide you every step of the way to ensure your outfits look amazing from head to toe! I can help you coordinate your family outfits with style and ease so you have one less thing to stress about!
Should I print my photos or just get digital files? 
While I offer both digital AND printed products, I firmly believe in the importance of printed photographs. The tangible portraits will be passed down for generations as the digital world continues to change. Your albums, prints, canvases, and metal art will stand the test of time. Plus, you get a free social media sharing digital file of every print you order! Digial files are a temporary means of storage but printed artwork is what will be passed down for generations. 
Should I wait until my kids are a older before investing in a professional portrait? 
Life moves fast. Once your children grow, you can never get that time back. Capturing your precious little ones through professional portraits is the best way to enjoy those moments and details in your home every day, even after the time has gone. Don’t wait until your kids are older and can “sit still” for a session. Don’t wait until you’ve “lost the baby weight”. These moments are something we can never get back and our kids will thank us for capturing it all! I can make magic happen with ALL ages and stages.
I'm interested in booking a session. What do I do next? 
The first step would be to fill out the contact form here and I will get in touch with you. You will receive an email with a link to my print and product pricing guide as well as a link to schedule your initial phone call so we can get the planning started.
