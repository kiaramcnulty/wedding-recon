Hi, I'm Kelly- Durango, CO Photographer
As part of the vibrant community of Durango photographers, I’m the owner of Kelly Miranda Photography, specializing in maternity, newborn, and family portraits. With a passion for creating beautifully printed art, I focus on capturing genuine emotions and organic moments that tell your unique story. Beyond photography, I’m a mom, outdoor enthusiast, animal lover, and self-proclaimed tea snob.
Your Portraits As Art
As a Durango, CO photographer, I believe in the power of photography to preserve cherished memories, and I take pride in delivering stunning artwork that families can treasure for generations. Digitals are temporary, art is forever.
Mom Is My Favorite Job Title
While being a Durango photographer is my career and passion, being a mom is my favorite title. My life’s greatest gift is to watch my son grow and raise him in this beautiful landscape. As a family of 3, we love to mountain bike, hike, camp, ski, and take in all that nature has to offer us.
We're a Family of Entrepreneurs
My husband, Casey, owns and operates a thriving window cleaning company here in Durango called Squeegee Monkey Window Washing. It’s a wild and hectic life but we wouldn’t have it any other way! The self-employed life allows us to prioritize our most valued treasure: family.
Durango Native
While I was technically 5 when I moved to Durango, I consider myself a native, having lived here for nearly all of my life. I left for 5 years from 2008-2013 to obtain my photography degree in Austin, TX, but Durango pulled me back in and I can’t imagine living anywhere else. I am so proud to call this place home!
Expertise
I obtained my degree in photography in Austin, TX in 2012 but opened my portrait business before I graduated, in 2010. I’ve been a family photographer in Durango for over 12 years and have continued my education through online courses in newborn safety, lighting, and posing. I’m always looking to learn and grow as an artist.
Licensed, Insured, and Professionally Equipped
Wether we are photographing in 2,400 sq ft studio conveniently located in Durango, or trekking up into the mountains, I’ve got all the equipment needed to provide top-notch portraits, from portable lighting to backdrops, maternity gowns, baby outfits, props, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions
Should I print my photos or just get digital files? 
While I offer both digital AND printed products, I firmly believe in the importance of printed photographs. The tangible portraits will be passed down for generations as the digital world continues to change. Your albums, prints, canvases, and metal art will stand the test of time. Plus, you get a free social media sharing digital file of every print you order! Digial files are a temporary means of storage but printed artwork is what will be passed down for generations. 
Should I wait until my kids are a older before investing in a professional portrait? 
Life moves fast. Once your children grow, you can never get that time back. Capturing your precious little ones through professional portraits is the best way to enjoy those moments and details in your home every day, even after the time has gone. Don’t wait until your kids are older and can “sit still” for a session. Don’t wait until you’ve “lost the baby weight”. These moments are something we can never get back and our kids will thank us for capturing it all! I can make magic happen with ALL ages and stages.
What's the best location? 
There are endless beautiful places in Durango and the surrounding areas. After your book your session, you will receive a link to my curated list of locations with galleries from each location to help guide you to the perfect spot. The location list has suggestions for every season and time of day so you can choose what works best for your session.
What should we wear? 
Contact me to receive my style guide and links to my Pinterest boards for outfit inspiration and color palettes for each season. After you book your session, I will guide you every step of the way to ensure your outfits look amazing from head to toe! I can help you coordinate your family outfits with style and ease so you have one less thing to stress about!
I'm interested in booking a session. What do I do next? 
The first step would be to fill out the contact form here and I will get in touch with you. You will receive an email with a link to my print and product pricing guide as well as a link to schedule your initial phone call so we can get the planning started.
