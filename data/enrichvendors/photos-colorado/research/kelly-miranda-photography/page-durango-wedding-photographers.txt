Elevate Your Wedding Day: Durango Wedding Photographers 
 Kelly Miranda Photography captures couples that aren’t afraid of doing something different, like climbing to the top of a mountain at sunrise, skiing downhill after saying, “I Do!”, trekking to high altitudes for the perfect view, or taking the plunge, literally, into a lake or river. Your wedding should be the most epic day of your life, and you deserve to have all the adventure captured in the most incredible photographs of all time. Kelly Miranda Photography is the best of Durango wedding photographers, serving the entire 4 corners area and beyond.

When it comes to wedding photography, Kelly Miranda Photography truly stands out as the premier choice. With over 14 years of experience as renowned Durango wedding photographers, Kelly Miranda Photography blends artistic portraiture with a documentary style, capturing the essence of each couple's special day. My familiarity with all the local venues allows me to identify the perfect spots for stunning shots, whether in a picturesque outdoor setting or a charming indoor space. Every laugh, tear, and joyful moment is beautifully documented, ensuring that the magic of your wedding day is preserved for a lifetime. If you're looking for Durango wedding photographers who deliver exceptional quality and a personal touch, I would love to work with you!

Featured Durango Wedding Venues
Riverbend Ranch
Riverbend Ranch offers a picturesque setting for wedding, surrounded by stunning mountain vistas and lush landscapes. Its rustic charm combined with modern amenities makes it an ideal location for creating unforgettable memories. I have years of experience photographing at this venue and it’s one of my favorites!
Riverbend Ranch Gallery 
Ridgewood Events Center
Ridgewood Events Center provides a captivating backdrop for any wedding, nestled among the serene beauty of the mountains. With elegant indoor and outdoor spaces that showcase the surrounding flower field, creek, and mountain views, it has a beautiful indoor reception area. I have been photographing at this venue for a long time, and it consistently stands out as one I love!
Ridgewood Events Center Gallery 
Dante's Peak and Purgatory Resort
Dante's Peak offers breathtaking panoramic views of the San Juan Mountains, making it a stunning backdrop for a wedding. Purgatory Resort complements this natural beauty with its elegant venues and exceptional service, perfect for couples seeking a memorable mountain celebration. I’m very familiar with this venue and the views just can’t be beat!
Dante's Peak and Purgatory Resort Gallery

Wedding Photography Frequently Asked Questions 
How do we reserve our date with you? Is there a deposit? 
You will receive a link to book your wedding photography package online. You will pay a $500 retainer fee to hold your wedding date and that amount is applied toward your total package price. 6 months before your wedding, 50% of your package is due. 30 days before your wedding, your remaining balance is due. You will receive invoices to pay each of these payments conveniently online.
How long does it take to get our photos? 
Your wedding photos will be ready within 6-8 weeks. You will receive a link to your online gallery where you can download all the photos, share, and order prints. A sneak peek is often sent about 1 week after your wedding.
How many photos will we get? 
I usually deliver between 50-75 photos per hour of photography.
How long have you been photographing weddings? 
I’ve been photographing weddings since 2010. I have captured over 300 weddings. Experience matters more than anything. There are a lot of Durango wedding photographers but none with the extensive training, degrees, and actual wedding experience that Kelly Miranda Photography brings. You can’t trust just anyone to capture your most important day!
Do you have experience working in various lighting situations? 
With over 14 years of wedding photography experience, I’ve seen it all, from pitch black outdoor receptions, and creative and colorful indoor lighting, to bright sun or heavy snow storms. I bring 3 portable lights with me that allow me to get create my own light no matter what I run into.
Do you have a replacement photographer in case of an emergency? 
In all my years, I’ve never missed a wedding! However, I do have a solid backup plan should something ever go wrong. I have a carefully curated list of other professional photographers whom I would trust to cover for me in the event of an emergency. I will make sure that you have someone amazing there to capture your day!
