Day of 
If you need a helping hand during your wedding weekend...this is for you! We manage the day and ensure everything you have planned is perfectly executed. 
We handle all of the details, manage your vendors, and coordinate the flow of 
your wedding from beginning to end. Simply relax and enjoy the hard work you have put in during the planning process! 
Month of & More 
The Month of & More is for those of you who would like to plan your wedding but need a little help getting there. We assist you with vendor selection and have a consultant available to you to answer any question at any time. With this package 
we become your personal wedding planning consultant! 
Partial 
The Partial Plan is perfect for those of you who have a vision and need help making it a reality. We become you're planning partner and guide you through the process step by step. We help 
currate and recommend a list of vendors according to your specific needs. In addition, you will receive extensive design assistance, and unlimited 
professional advice to ensure a stress free planning experience . 
Full 
This is the ultimate service for any couple that anticipates the extensive number of details involved in planning and orchestrating a wedding. We recommend this 
service for the couple who is busy with work / school / life and needs a more hands on wedding planner to lead. With your direction we will guide you 
through the whole process from start to finish by designing, coordinating and 
managing your dream wedding. We manage your budget, your vendors, and everything in between.
