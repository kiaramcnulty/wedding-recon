My name is Janna and I am the face behind the lens here at Janna Lynn Photography. My journey as a photographer started in 2007 when I attended the Academy of Art University. While in school, I began working with a local wedding photographer and started working with newborns and children every chance I could! Naturally, when my son was born, I started photographing him like crazy. I found that I had a passion for capturing those moments that they so quickly outgrow. Those little toes, tiny fingers, perfect lips, and sleepy eyes disappear more quickly than I would like. My desire is to hold those moments in time so others can cherish them forever. That is when I decided to create Janna Lynn Photography and I have loved every moment of it!

Pricing
I take pride in capturing and preserving special moments that only happen a handful a times in your life. Custom photography is more than posing and pressing a button. It is taking the time to create a perfect moment that will come together to create an album full of memories to pass down from generation to generation. Taking these first fleeting moments of your little one’s life and turning them into art. My collections offer beautiful heirloom products for you to cherish for a lifetime. I take the time to find what you’re looking for and create timeless art and memories for your family. Within two weeks of your session, we will sit together for your reveal! At this time, you will select which collection you would like. My pricing starts at $300 but varies depending on what you're looking for. Please contact me for the complete pricing guide which will guide you through my collections.
I do take a very limited number of sessions per month, therefore please inquire at your earliest convenience. Maternity sessions are recommended between 32-34 weeks. Newborns are photographed typically between 7 to 10 days old. I recommend calling to book your newborn session during the second trimester so I can ensure availability. 
All major credit cards are accepted. Payment plans are available upon request.
