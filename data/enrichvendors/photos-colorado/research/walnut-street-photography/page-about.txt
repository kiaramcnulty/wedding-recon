Our Team
Noah 
 My name is Noah and I’m the owner at Walnut Street Photography. My wife and I started Walnut Street 11 years ago when we were living on Walnut Street in Denver and although we’ve moved a few times around Colorado since then the name has stuck with us :)
 For the past several years I’ve mostly been photographing small weddings and elopements, about 150-200 per year, but still love a good large wedding as well! I also still do family sessions for all the families we’ve met over the past 11 years.
 Let us know if you have any questions or would like to book us for your wedding or portrait session!
Jules
 My name is Jules and I am a photographer here at Walnut Street Photography! I was born and raised in Boulder, Colorado, I moved around a few times through the years but I always felt the need to come back home.
 For the past 3 years I have been focusing on photographing people and capturing their raw emotions, I strive to capture the details that make each moment unforgettable whether it’s a joke that was told during a family shoot or the words said by the groom that brings the bride to tears. I believe every photo is a chance to create art and preserve memories, and I am dedicated to making my clients feel comfortable, inspired, and celebrated. 
 I am excited to create images that you will cherish for a lifetime :) 
Kyrie
 Hi, I'm Kyrie Fuqua!
 (pronounced like "Kylie" with an "r" and "Few-kway")
 Colorado is my home state and after spending the last decade in Oregon, I am so happy to be here again! I have careers as both a high school math teacher and photographer, but I am currently taking some years off from the former to be with my toddler son and newborn daughter. In my free time I enjoy rock climbing, traveling, reading, drinking matcha, and spending time with family and friends.
 I've been taking photos of people and places since middle school and professionally for the last five years. Pictures allow you to revisit moments/stories/feelings—you truly can't have too many! My passion is capturing love, joy, and milestones for couples, families, and individuals. I especially love the beauty and intimacy of elopements and can’t wait to be part of yours!
