ASHLEY RIDGE WEDGEWOOD WEDDING PHOTOGRAPHERS
We love the high ceilings and glamour high on the hill over Countyline Road, south of Denver. Along with glamour, it’s possible to have portraits in a natural setting if we begin with you across the road at Fly-N-B Park. We have ideas, and we follow well, too. Are you having a big church ceremony offsite? We’ve built a package for that. 
 Fitting photography smoothly in your wedding day is our speciality, and our partnership with Wedgewood assures we're in the loop with coordination decisions. When things change (like weather) we're problem-solvers who happily capture great images — no matter the challenge.
 On delivery of your photography, you will see 60-110 images per coverage hour, showing the story of a day when two families came together. Images are available for viewing and download 3 weeks after wedding day (with rush processing available ). You can give family and friends online access to view and purchase the images at their leisure — for a full year, up to your first anniversary. Our clients have copyright to download all the large images for safekeeping beyond that. 
 We’re talented, flexible and proven. Let’s talk about your hopes for capturing the Big Day with professional images that meet the proud brand standard of Love & Lens.
