Family Portraits | Micro Wedding & Elopements | Engagements | Events | Senior Portraits 
 Based in Arvada, serving families and couples across Denver and the Front Range
Based in Arvada, Colorado for the past 15 years and originally from North Carolina, hence my love for front porches, warm hospitality, sports and a preference for “y’all” and “folks”
 I’m a proud dad to our amazing infant son Kellen, companion and hiking buddy to our soulful dog Kya, and loving husband to my beautiful wife and best friend Lindsay. Their love, adventurous spirits and steady support continue to be my north star. We are all fire signs . We burn bright brimming with deep passion and curiosity for the the world around us. 
 Oh yeah, and I guess I’m also an international award-winning, National Geographic published and accomplished photographer.
 I’ve always been drawn to people, to their stories and what makes them who they are. Photography grew out of that curiosity. I don’t merely chase spectacle, but meaning and stories. 
 With over a decade of experience and a background photographing and guiding in 70+ countries, I bring a calm, confident and playful presence to every session. 
 I’ve led and documented trips ranging from taking blind students over 19,000 feet in the Peruvian Andes to being a member of the first organized group to complete the Zagros Mountain Trail trek across northern Kurdistan, Iraq. (Peru is my 2nd home, so if you’re ever looking to elope or go on a guided trip, please reach out!)
 Hablo espanol, mi apodo es “Medio Gringo".” I’ve lived in Peru and Argentina and worked across Latin America for 16 years.
 I believe trust is the foundation of meaningful photography . Sharing intimate and vulnerable moments requires feeling comfortable enough to truly be yourself. The experience should feel fun and human, where laughter, a little bit of chaos and serendipity are encouraged.
 I specialize in helping people feel at ease in front of the camera with a down to earth energy. No stiff posing or forced feelings. No need to overthink or stress it. I’ve got you. 
 My work blends rich color and genuine emotion to create timeless images with depth and real, raw emotion.
 I live simultaneously somewhere between my 80-year old self — content on the porch in a cardigan, coffee in hand, reflecting on life — and my 6-year old inner child, barefoot and wild, brimming with curiosity and imagination. An old soul with a young spirit. 
 I’m a sucker for terrible puns, witty banter and coming up with ridiculous games.
 Your story is worth documenting. I’d be honored to help preserve a part of it.
 Still curious? I’m at least mildly interesting. Learn more of my story here. 
Check out my environmental portraiture, global and fine art photography work .
