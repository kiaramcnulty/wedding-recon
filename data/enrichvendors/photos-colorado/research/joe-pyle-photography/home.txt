Why couples choose us for Estes Park weddings
We live in Estes Park 
We are local Estes Park photographers, not a team driving in from the Front Range on the morning of your wedding. That matters when the weather shifts, parking gets tight, Trail Ridge Road changes, or your timeline needs to adjust quickly.
Two Lead Photographers 
Every wedding includes both Joe and Kari as lead photographers. That means your day is covered from two creative perspectives without relying on associates, assistants, or second shooters you have never met.
RMNP Planning Experience 
Rocky Mountain National Park weddings involve permits, ceremony site rules, visitor traffic, seasonal road access, and strict timing. We help couples plan around the real logistics of the park so the day feels easier and more intentional.
Estes Park Venue Experience 
We regularly photograph weddings at Black Canyon Inn, Della Terra, The Stanley Hotel, Romantic Riversong, Taharaa, YMCA of the Rockies, and RMNP. That local venue familiarity helps with light, portrait locations, backup options, and realistic timelines.
