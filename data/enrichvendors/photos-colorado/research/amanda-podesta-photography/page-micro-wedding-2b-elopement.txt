Micro Wedding + Elopement Planning 
Let&#x27;s Just Elope!
 You want to focus on the two of you. You want to avoid any wedding drama. You want an epic day of exploring. You want something different. A big wedding just isn't who you are. 
An elopement sounds like the right fit for you and your partner. Great! We've made a decision! But now what? Let's plan it out. Leave all the heavy lifting to me. I have captured and handled planning many elopements. Whether you are envisioning the beaches of Mexico, the red sandstone landscapes of Moab, the Rocky Mountains, or pretty much anything in between. It is my passion to know and seek out the best intimate locations for your elopement and portraits. As well as, the very best vendors. 
You want intimate.
 You want the day to be about the two of you. You don't like the idea (or cost) of a big wedding. You cannot stand the thought of not having your nearest and dearest with you on the day of. 
Sounds like a Micro Wedding is going to be your best bet. A major advantage of having a micro wedding is it is so much easier than planning a full-on wedding. Similar to an elopement, pick the destination and day and we will work together to make your intimate soiree dreams a reality. 
My Role.
 Think of me as your guide to your perfect day. My many years of experience working in planning, logistics, and as a photographer allows me to provide my clients with incredible service and attention to detail. 
I have a whole network of vendors all around the United States, and internationally to make your dream elopement or micro wedding a reality.
