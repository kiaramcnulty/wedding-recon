Amanda 
Hi. I'm Amanda. In this life I have learned to savor the fleeting moments. I channel that passion into photography - capturing genuine connections and the raw beauty of life in the places that matter most. As a lifelong mountain dweller and passionate lover of the outdoors, I know that the best moments are shared in natures embrace. I specialize in weddings that capture affinity against breathtaking backdrops. 
Next Steps 
 STEP 1. Reach out! We will work together to connect where we will get to know each other. We will talk about your relationship, what brings you joy and chat about your favorite hobbies. We‘ll go over what your vision and priorities are for your wedding day. 
STEP 2. Following our call or meeting to discuss your priorities, I will develop a tailored proposal that addresses your specific needs. Together, we’ll determine the appropriate level of coverage for your wedding and identify any additional planning logistics or services required to ensure your special day runs smoothly. 
STEP 3. We maintain open communication throughout the entire wedding planning process. Together, we'll create a stunning engagement session, and by the time your wedding day arrives, you’ll feel as if we’re old friends. Which is my ultimate goal.
