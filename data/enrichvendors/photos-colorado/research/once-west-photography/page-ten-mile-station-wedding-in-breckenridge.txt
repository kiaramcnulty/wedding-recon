Ten Mile Station Wedding in Breckenridge: Ali &#038; Trevor
By Michele DeVries 
After meeting Ali & Trevor for their engagement session (that was 9 months prior to their wedding), I was itching for June to roll around. It didn&#8217;t take long, after being around their magnetic personalities, to determine that their wedding was going to be the party of the year. Then I saw their roster of vendors. I couldn&#8217;t help but laugh with excitement. 
June finally came around and this wedding more than met my expectations. When a couple is as wildly in love as Ali & Trevor it creates a kind of electricity that seems to energize every guest and vendor in the room. It&#8217;s a really beautiful thing to witness, and just another of the many, many reasons why I love this job.
Here&#8217;s a peek at Ali & Trevor&#8217;s Breckenridge wedding on Peak 9:
Venue: Ten Mile Station @ Breckenridge Ski Resort
Planner: Distinctive Mountain Events 
Photographer: ONCEWEST Photography
Florist: Lady Bird Poppy 
Stationery: Four 13 Designs 
Hair & Makeup: Alpine Spa & Salon 
Photo Booth: Shutter Bus 
Band: Funkiphino 
Desert: Yours Truly Cupcakes 
★ ★ ★ ★ ★ 
&#8220;Michele and Mark were amazing and so easy to work with. We fell in love with them! Their style was a perfect match for our vision. Their affinity for the outdoors and knowledge of the surrounding areas made it so we didn&#8217;t have to think at all, just show up. They are so good at blending in and going unnoticed that people were concerned they didn&#8217;t get any photos&#8230; Boy, were they wrong! Not only do they get amazing photos, but they capture the moments so well that you feel like you&#8217;re reliving them while looking at the photos. Michele and Mark go above and beyond their job, making you feel like family. TLDR: Amazing work. Unobtrusive. Makes your life and decisions easy.&#8221;
