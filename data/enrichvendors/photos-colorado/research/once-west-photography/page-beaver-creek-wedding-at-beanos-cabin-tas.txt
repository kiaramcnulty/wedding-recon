By Michele DeVries 
When Tasha&#8217;s mom reached out to us last year about having us photograph Tasha & Dustin&#8217;s Beaver Creek wedding this summer, I immediately became invested. In 2017 we photographed Tasha&#8217;s brother&#8217;s wedding and so I already knew what an amazing group of people Tasha&#8217;s family was. The fact that they were planning a wedding at Beano&#8217;s Cabin, one of our all-time favorite Colorado wedding venues, was simply the icing on the cake. 
Fast forward to July of this year and Tasha & Dustin were met with the kind of Colorado summer perfection that every couple dreams of. First they exchanged vows at the beautiful Beaver Creek Chapel. Following the ceremony their guests boarded shuttles and were driven up the mountain to Beano&#8217;s Cabin, a quintessential Colorado restaurant that sits slope-side surrounded by aspens and evergreens.
