The Chalet 
 Our full-service Chalet concierge team is available to assist you with equipment rentals, ski lessons, maps, weather reports, ski condition information and more. 
 Details 
THE VAIL OUTPOST 
 Discover The Vail Outpost, an alpine boutique offering heritage brands and seasonal lifestyle pieces, located in the retail lobby corridor at Four Seasons Resort Vail.
 Details 
Year-Round Pool 
 Tucked within a sunken courtyard, our heated outdoor pool welcomes you for a dip every day of every season. Loungers and two spa tubs invite you to rest and rejuvenate.
 Details 
Everyone Outside 
 Whether you need ski lessons for your children or wish to arrange a memorable sleigh ride for the entire family, our Concierge is here to manage all the details. This allows you to make the most of your time on the mountain.
The Remedy 
 With floor-to-ceiling mountain views, our lively bar is the perfect spot for Sunday football games or après-ski cocktails. Outside, firepits illuminate a year-round patio.
 Details 
Award-Winning Spa 
 With 13 treatment rooms, two couples’ suites and the Relaxation Garden, our Spa is a soothing complement to skiing and hiking adventures on the mountain.
 Details 
Stay Fit 
 For fitness fiends prepping for excursions on Vail Mountain, our gym is open daily with cardio machines, free weights and certified trainers.
 Details 
Winter Activities in Vail 
 Vail in winter is nothing short of magical. Whether you’re carving fresh powder, gliding on a sleigh ride or unwinding at our Spa, we make it easy to embrace the season’s charm. Explore our complete guide to skiing, snowboarding, family fun and off-the-slopes experiences.
 See all winter activities 
Summer Activities in Vail 
 From alpine hikes and mountain biking to whitewater rafting and fly fishing, summer in Vail is alive with possibility. Our curated collection of warm-weather adventures showcases the best of the Colorado Rockies, whether you’re chasing adrenaline or simply soaking up the mountain sunshine
