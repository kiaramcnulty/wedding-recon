Services & Amenities The Chalet
hours Chalet Concierge Highlights More Amenities & Services EVENTs 
The Chalet is a three-storey building in the heart of Vail Village, steps from Gondola One and the Vail Mountain ticketing counter. Offering guests a prime ski-in, ski-out experience, The Chalet provides exclusive access to our Chalet Concierge services. The top floor features a cozy lounge with complimentary refreshments and light breakfast or afternoon bites, while the main floor is home to Venture Sports rental services, offering high-quality ski and snowboard equipment. On the bottom floor, you’ll find personal ski lockers equipped with boot and glove dryers.
Hours
summer 9:00 am – 4:00 pm winter 8:00 am – 4:30 pm Venture Sports Store 8:00 am – 6:00 pm 
Please Note
 The Chalet will be closed from April to early June and Labor Day to November 13.

FAQ
Does Four Seasons Resort Vail offer a ski-in, ski-out experience? Yes, we provide a seamless ski-in, ski-out experience through our dedicated slope-side facility, The Chalet. Instead of carrying equipment through town, guests enjoy a short walk or shuttle ride to the base of Gondola One, where our ski concierge team has your warmed boots and skis ready and waiting right at the edge of the mountain.
Where is The Chalet located in relation to the base of the mountain? The Chalet is ideally situated in the heart of Vail Village at the base of Gondola One, providing immediate access to Vail Mountain's primary lifts. If you or your family are attending ski school or utilizing the terrain near the other base villages at Golden Peak or Lionshead, our ski concierge team can easily arrange transportation, and equipment transfers to accommodate your starting point.
Do I have to walk through Vail Village in my ski boots? No, you can comfortably walk through town in your regular winter footwear. The Chalet features a heated locker room and comfortable lounge seating right at the base of the mountain. Simply arrive in your snow boots, and our ski concierge will have your pre-warmed ski boots ready for you to slip into right before you step onto the snow.
We are travelling with young kids. Will we have to carry their ski equipment to the lifts? No. We know that carrying family ski gear can be the most challenging part of a winter vacation. From the moment you arrive at the Resort, our team will transport your equipment to The Chalet where our dedicated ski valets handle all the heavy lifting. Your family's skis, poles, and snowboards will be staged directly on the snow near Gondola One each morning, so you and your children can simply click in and ski.
Does our equipment stay at the mountain overnight, or do we bring it back to the resort? Your equipment stays right at the mountain. The Chalet provides secure, overnight ski and snowboard storage. At the end of your ski day, simply hand your gear to our valets. We will store your skis, dry your boots on our state-of-the-art boot warmers overnight, and have everything prepared for your next morning on the slopes.
How close is The Chalet to Vail’s ski and snowboard schools? The Chalet is located just a short walk or shuttle ride from Golden Peak which is the primary base area for Vail's children's ski school and beginner terrain. If you are taking a lesson out of Golden Peak or Lionshead, our concierge team can help coordinate the easiest morning routine so your family arrives at drop-off stress-free and ready to learn.
