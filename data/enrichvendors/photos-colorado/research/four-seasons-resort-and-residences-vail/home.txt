Welcome to Vail Village, Where Understated Luxury Meets Mountain Beauty
Steeped in rich history and the ski culture of the American West, Vail, Colorado continues to be one of the most iconic mountain destinations in the United States. After a day of skiing or hiking, retreat into an oasis of relaxation at our Vail Village luxury hotel and resort. Boasting 300 days of sunshine every year, Four Seasons Resort and Residences Vail is widely recognized as the mountain resort of choice for travellers and the crown jewel of Colorado’s Rocky Mountains.
Accommodations
Blanca Two-Bedroom Presidential Suite
Unwind in elevated luxury in this stylish two-bedroom suite, nestled on the top floor of our luxury Resort. Enjoy breathtaking mountain views of Vail, along with the warmth of cozy gas-burning stone fireplaces in the separate living areas and bedrooms. Your stay includes complimentary daily in-room breakfast, laundry service, a fully stocked MyBar and elevated bath amenities – enhancing every moment with comfort and indulgence.

Embrace the extraordinary and discover a curated selection of thoughtful offerings designed to foster connection, adventure and unforgettable memories.

Discover Vail
Rafting and ATV Adventure
 8 hours 
 Jun 1 - Aug 15 
Join us for an epic river trip on the Rancho stretch of the Colorado River. On this trip, you&#x27;ll experience a stunning remote canyon, fun swimming opportunities and a historic homestead walk. Next, ascend to our private 6,000-acre mountain escape, gearing up with goggles, bandanas and helmets before heading to the Four Seasons lookout point. Enjoy a gourmet lunch, lawn games and sweeping views of the New York and Mosquito Ranges. Relax in a private viewing pod before calling your guide for the next adventure. Your afternoon will unfold with a two-hour tour of the pristine, private mountain, where lush meadows, wildflowers, lakes and wildlife create an unforgettable landscape. Wrap up at our check-in cabin before returning to the Resort, where a well-earned cocktail awaits. A private photographer will be on-site to capture both adventures, ensuring a collection of unforgettable moments. Expect 150 to 200 professionally taken and edited photos, delivered via link the next day.
Ziplining Adventure
 4 hours 
 Jun 1 - Sep 30 2026 
Immerse yourself in the pristine beauty of the Colorado mountains with a ziplining adventure at Four Seasons Resort and Residences Vail. Soar over Alkali Canyon while enjoying views of lush forests and rivers. This excursion features light hiking through scenic terrain – with the longest trek lasting approximately five minutes – and offers an optional "Canyon Plunge" for those seeking a thrilling finish to their mountain journey.
Whitewater Rafting
 2 hours 
 May 22 - Aug 1 2026 
Whitewater rafting the Colorado River&#x27;s Shoshone stretch is perfect for visitors to the Vail area. This trip will take you through the gorgeous Glenwood Canyon, where rock walls tower above the river. This trip offers class III rapids and considered a moderate trip for ages 8+. Not a fan of rapids, or does someone in your group not meet the minimum age requirements? These guests can skip the class III rapid but still join the in on the scenic float section of the trip and remain with their group through to the takeout.
