Services & Amenities Sustainability
Our Commitment 
 At Four Seasons Resort and Residences Vail, we are committed to preserving and regenerating the environment and leaving a positive, enduring impact on our local community. Below are some of the sustainability initiatives we have implemented at our property. Click here to learn more about global Four Seasons for Good efforts. 
our initiatives
Environmental programs hide Polestar electric vehicles available for guest use
 Electric vehicle (EV) charging stations available for guest use
 Vegan and vegetarian menu items available
 Linens and towels replaced every third day, unless otherwise requested
Energy, carbon and water reduction hide Energy-saving guest room controls
 Water conservation measures in place
 Water-efficient landscaping
Waste Reduction hide Elimination of single-use plastic water bottles, food and beverage containers, and bathroom amenities
 Recycling program in place
 Soaps and toiletries recycled and donated to those in need through Clean the World
Sustainable Food and Beverage hide Food procurement focuses on attaining local, sustainable products
 Responsibly sourced seafood and cage-free eggs
 Food waste reduction program in place 
Community hide Guests can engage in educational tours at local, natural, cultural, historical or heritage sites to learn more about the local community through our Concierge, including mountain biking in the Gore Range and hiking in the Colorado Rockies
 Donations are made regularly to the Salvation Army, including handmade blankets, which are donated to Shaw Regional Cancer Center and Eagle County Animal Shelter
 Organized canned food drive each Thanksgiving
 "Turkey-to-Go" for first responders on Thanksgiving, including hospital staff, emergency medical technicians and police officers
 Participation in Adopt-A-Family each Christmas
 Organization of the annual highway cleanup on I-70
Purchasing Practices hide Eco-certified cleaning products used
 The Spa features artisanal products from local creators
Four Seasons for Good 
 Four Seasons is committed to building upon our company’s strong history of supporting our communities and the environment. Through Four Seasons for Good , we seek to preserve and regenerate the beautiful places in which we operate, and leave a positive, enduring impact on our communities.
