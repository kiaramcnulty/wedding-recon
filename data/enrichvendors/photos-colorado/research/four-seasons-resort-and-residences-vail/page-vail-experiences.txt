Golf 
 Tee off at some of the region’s top courses in Vail from stunning outdoor courses to advanced golf programs created in partnership with the Steadman Philippon Research Institute. With options to hit the greens at Red Sky Golf Club, a private club boasting an 18-hole Tom Fazio and Greg Norman-designed course with panoramic views of the Rockies, or at the beautiful Beaver Creek, home to a course designed by Robert Trent Jones Jr.
