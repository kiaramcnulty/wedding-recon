Welcome to the EagleVail Pavilion 
Join us at our picturesque venue located in Avon, CO. This property is nestled in the heart of the Vail Valley, between Vail and Beaver Creek, offering stunning backdrops for your special event. For those who want to celebrate in the great outdoors, the venue offers mature pines and landscaping. At EagleVail Pavilion, you will spend your day surrounded by rugged natural beauty, rolling lawns, and waterfront views. Please schedule a tour with us and see for yourself why the EagleVail Pavilion is the local's favorite for a stunning self service venue.
