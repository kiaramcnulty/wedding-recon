Weddings 
The EagleVail Pavilion is where indoor and outdoor spaces come together. With our event lawn and welcoming waterfront deck, our space will 'wow' your guests with authentic Colorado beauty. 
Wedding Week Celebrations 
Ceremonies 
Choose from a multitude of ceremony sites that our space has to offer. A few of our favorites include the event lawn, our deck overlooking the pond, and the pond beach. 
Receptions 
Adorn the Pavilion's main room with elegant linens and china for your guests. With ample seating for 200 guests and room for your dance floor! 
Rehearsals 
Rehearse your ceremony on our event lawn, entertain your guests with lawn games, back the food truck up to the deck, and let the celebration begin!
