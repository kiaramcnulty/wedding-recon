 lived in
 more than a dozen places across the

Here's what studio means today 
Myth - You need to dress formally
Reality - Absolutely not! You feel your best when you’re in your favorites, so
 wear
 ‘em! That can be as formal (or informal!) as you’d like. In fact, we’ve had
 families
 who let their kids come in costume, and it was a ton of fun for everyone.
Myth - You’re a number on a calendar
Reality - Not here you’re not. Every single member of our studio is here to help
 you
 feel welcome, comfortable, and cared for. You are our priority from the moment
 we
 connect.
Myth - Studio lighting is harsh
Reality - We have years of studio experience and technical
 expertise to use lighting to our advantage. Take a look at our galleries to see
 the
 way we work with light to create warm images with depth and emotion.
Myth - Stiff posing and frozen smiles, eek!
Reality - Of course we always make sure to capture at least
 one
 image of everyone smiling at the camera. But we’ve found that your favorites are
 the
 ones where you’re simply interacting with your people—comfortable, natural +
 authentic.
