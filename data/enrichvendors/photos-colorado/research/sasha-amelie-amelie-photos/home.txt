Sasha Amelie Photography | Lifestyle Sessions & Weddings | NYC & Beyond 
 Cinematic. Atmospheric. Timeless. 
 Hey there, I am Sasha, a New York based lifestyle photographer with over 15 years of experience, capturing weddings across the US, as well as proposals, engagement photos, family portraits, intimate moments throughout NYC: from Central Park to the Brooklyn Bridge, from the Upper West Side to DUMBO.
 My approach is simple: real connection, natural light, and a relaxed atmosphere where you can just be yourself. No forced poses, no pressure.
 Whether you’re planning a full wedding celebration, a quiet stroll through Manhattan, an NYC elopement, or a cozy family shoot in the park, I’ll help you create photographs that feel effortless, honest, and deeply personal.
