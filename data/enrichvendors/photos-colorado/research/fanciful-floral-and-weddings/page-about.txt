FINDING MY FANCY 
from perfect petals to perfect weddings 
This is what I love to do. I entered the wedding industry as a floral designer, designing flowers for over 300 ceremonies, working with just as many budgets, families, venues, vendors, timelines, and brides. With each of those weddings, my desire was always to make everything perfect &ndash; down to the last petal. While Fanciful may have gotten it's start from flowers, it has now blossomed into complete wedding planning, offering services in literally every arena you can think of. I have enjoyed partnering with each of my floral and wedding planning clients, and now, I hope to be able to share my love for weddings with you.
