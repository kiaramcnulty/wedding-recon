Experience
After over 500 weddings, nothing phases me, and I&#8217;ll have your back every step of the way.
I started photographing weddings working for other companies&#8211;usually as a third or second photographer rather than the one in charge. I worked over 200 weddings before I even considered branching out on my own. Seeing all those happy people celebrating made me realize just how dang important a wedding day is to people, and I definitely wasn&#8217;t going to risk goofing it up. 
I didn&#8217;t start my own company until I felt like I could walk into any venue, any lighting situation, and deal with any timeline mishaps while still making incredible images for my people. I&#8217;ve got over 500 weddings under my belt these days (maybe more, I stopped counting after year 6 or 7). So, when you work with me, know you&#8217;ve got someone there who knows what&#8217;s up, won&#8217;t get shaken by a little wedding day hiccup, and will take everything in stride to get you the photos you want and deserve!

I want you to experience your wedding day. 
After all those years of weddings, I realized something else big. The photos that meant the most were the moments in between the poses, where people were just being themselves. The people that I really connected with were the ones cherishing every moment of their day, where the camera wasn&#8217;t a thought in their minds, they were just living and experiencing each other, and the day unfolding around them.
So those photos are what drive me now. I live for the little moments that you didn&#8217;t even realize happened, the moments where you lock eyes during your vows and feel all the feelings, and the belly laughs that come from spending time with your favorite people.
