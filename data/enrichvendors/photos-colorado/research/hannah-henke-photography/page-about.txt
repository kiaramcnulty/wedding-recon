I'M HANNAH HENKE
I’m a Vail wedding photographer serving couples throughout Colorado and beyond.
My priority is to document weddings in an honest, story driven way, focused on real moments, real emotion, and the in-between things you didn’t know you’d want to remember.
Most of my work takes place in Vail, Silverthorne, Denver, Boulder, and throughout Colorado’s mountain and foothill regions. I also travel for weddings across the East Coast, the Pacific Northwest, Texas, and destination mountain celebrations throughout the West.
Weddings are full of moments that feel fleeting while they’re happening, but stay with you for a lifetime. I want you to look back at your photos and feel it all again exactly as it was. If this feels right for you, I’d love to hear from you.
