Meet Danielle
Consider a tapestry—woven not with cloth, but with experiences. Composed of stories, spaces, and all the moments in between. Simultaneously sentimental and unexpected, bound together by both the timeless and the beauty of a fleeting instance.
This, I believe, is the texture of your wedding day—a milestone on one of the most compelling journeys you’ll ever take. Photos taken with purpose, I seek out the unscripted, the brief—but extraordinary—moments that define days and last a lifetime.
My work is driven by movement of the heart, deeply rooted in family, and inspired by the land. It’s my connection to stories, my faith in something old and something new, that fuels my creativity and keeps a camera in my hands for more than 15 years. Photographing weddings and portraits in Vail, Aspen , Telluride, Denver and everywhere in between. I cannot imagine a more beautiful way to fill my days.

Background + Approach
Danielle's background includes commercial art school, where she learned the technical skills of professional photography, as well as fine art school, where she honed in on her aesthetic.
She photographed her first wedding in 2001 on a Hasselblad 500cm and hand-developed and printed everything in her home darkroom. Technology is constantly transforming the art of photography, but Danielle will always retain a note of nostalgia. She still shoot film along with digital for each event, for timeless photos that perfectly blend our past with the present.
​Colorado-based since 2005, where she is beyond lucky to be surrounded by never-ending natural beauty. Though the West has captured her heart, Danielle will happily follow the creative call to collaborate with clients all over the US. Danielle's weddings have taken her from coast to coast and everywhere in between, and you can find work published both locally and nationally.
