Meet Danielle
Consider a tapestry—woven not with cloth, but with experiences. Composed of stories, spaces, and all the moments in between. Simultaneously sentimental and unexpected, bound together by both the timeless and the beauty of a fleeting instance.
Photos taken with purpose, I seek out the unscripted, the brief—but extraordinary—moments that define days and last a lifetime.
My work is driven by movement of the heart, deeply rooted in family, and inspired by the land. It’s my connection to stories, my faith in something old and something new, that fuels my creativity and keeps a camera in my hands for more than 16 years. Photographing weddings and portraits in Vail, Aspen, Telluride, Denver and everywhere in between. I cannot imagine a more beautiful way to fill my days.
Telluride Wedding Photography
Since 2008, I’ve worked with families in some of the most stunning locations, including Telluride, Vail, and Aspen, Colorado. Capturing your family’s connection in a place that’s meaningful to you makes for beautiful family keepsakes. Whether it’s Aspen downtown historical charm, Vail's mountain tops, or Telluride's alpen glow, these locations provide the perfect backdrop for authentic family portraits.
I focus on finding a balance of candid and intentional photos, capturing genuine moments and thoughtfully composed shots that highlight your family’s connection and tell your family’s story.
My session always includes time spent with you on the day of the shoot and the professional editing of your photos. Your digital files will be delivered via an online gallery for easy viewing and sharing. While I offer prints, there’s no pressure to purchase—just the option to enjoy your images however you’d like.
Interested in booking a session? Please reach out to me to discuss your upcoming trip and plan your family portraits.
