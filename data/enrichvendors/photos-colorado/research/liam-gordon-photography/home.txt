This is Your Moment.
Your wedding story deserves to be a celebration of who you are and the beautiful individuality of your your love for each other and those close to you.
 My work is for couples who want to look back at their wedding photos and see themselves exactly as they are: stylish, expressive, a little unconventional, and deeply in love.

Hi I’m Liam
Colorado Wedding Photographer.
If you’ve made here, you are probably a little bit like me. You’re not really into the wedding photos that look like everybody else’s. You want beautiful art, but you want it to feel real, vibrant, and a celebration of your relationship and the important people in your life. 
 My approach is to focus on those in-between moments. When tears flow freely, smiles are a bit bigger, and real emotions shine through. This is where the opportunity is to make something magical.

I Will Photograph Your Wedding 
Without Invading It!
I’m not your typical Colorado wedding photographer. Wedding photography is notoriously staged. My approach is much more hands-off. It’s your wedding day and I’m not going to boss anyone around (unless it’s group photo time) or get in the way of you and your guests having the best time. What I am going to do is photograph the heck out of you enjoying every minute of it! 
 Of course, during the portrait session, I will provide all the direction and guidance needed to bring out natural and authentic expressions so you won’t look or feel awkward in your photos.
