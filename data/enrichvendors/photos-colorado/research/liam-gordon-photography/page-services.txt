This is Who I Am
Hey there, I’m Liam… and that’s my wife, Melissa, and our son, Quinn. I’m your photographer, wedding day guide, and occasional tree climber (because it’s worth it for the shot).
 I live and breathe photography and making images for couples who want to be themselves on their wedding day. For those who want the beautiful, tearful, long hugs, and belly laughs photographed more than the food menu and card table.
 If that sounds like you and you can see or feel those emotions in my images, we might be a great fit!
This is the Booking Process
Fill Out The Contact Form
This gives me some basic information about your plans. Don’t worry if you don’t have it all figured out yet, most couples are in the early stages of planning. 
Schedule A Video Call
We’ll get to know one another. You tell me everything you hope your wedding will feel like, and I tell you about my approach and answer your questions. 
Sign The Contract
I will send you a project proposal with the package we discussed, along with a contract that you can sign online and pay the retainer. Then congrats! You’ve got your photographer booked!
