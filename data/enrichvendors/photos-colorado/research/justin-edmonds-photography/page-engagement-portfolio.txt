featured engagement sessions
Engagement photos are your chance to have a warm up prior to the wedding day, have your personality as a couple captured and create memories. You can think of it as an outdoor date with photography. All of the couples that you see here and anywhere else on my website are real people, not models, so you can relax because everyone is capable of having wonderful photos. If you don’t know where to go, I’ve compiled a list of Colorado engagement photo locations for you to look through. Sessions last about 2 hours and you’ll receive 100-150 images. If you’re ready to book your engagement session, contact me to start the process.
You can view some of our favorite engagement galleries below. These are real couples and their real engagement photos. No styled shoots here!

client login
For personal privacy, client galleries are password protected. To login, the password is &#8220;YYYYMMDDlastname,&#8221; where the date is the day of the shoot (wedding day or engagement session) and last name can be either last name. Passwords are case-sensitive, and names are always lowercase. 
For example, the password for a July 3, 2018 wedding between John Smith and Sarah Kline would be either 20180703smith or 20180703kline .
