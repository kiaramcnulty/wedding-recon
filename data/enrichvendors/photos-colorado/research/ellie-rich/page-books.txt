NEW RELEASE 
'Wake of Ruin'
Book 1 in the series 'Empire of Vanishing Light' by Ellie Rich 
This story has lived in my mind and heart for years and now it's finally out in the world...
Writing this book has been an incredible journey! It was shaped by sleepless nights, by pages rewritten and torn apart, by the strange magic that happens when a story refuses to let go.
It's a high fantasy adventure&mdash;full of fierce choices, haunting mysteries, and a heroine who refuses to back down. 
​ 
BUY ON AMAZON 
 Elizabeth has always felt like an outsider in her own world&mdash;never quite fitting in, never truly belonging. Everything changes when an inexplicable force pulls her into an unknown realm, shattering everything she thought she knew.
​ 
In this strange, otherworldly place, magic pulses through the air, and ancient powers intertwine with the land itself. Lost in a world of danger and mystery, Elizabeth must confront forces beyond her understanding. Relying on her will to survive, the pull of a cryptic calling, and the awakening of a burning power within her, she is forced to navigate this new reality.
​ 
As secrets begin to unravel and enemies close in, Elizabeth struggles to find her place in a world torn by everlasting conflict, where nothing is as it seems. To survive, she must rise, adapt, and decide who she truly is&mdash;and what she will become.
​ 
She might lose her name, change her fate, and perhaps even alter the very future of this strange new world. The question remains: will she find the path to salvation, or will she become the force that brings its downfall?
​ 
A gripping tale of survival, transformation, and the relentless pull of destiny. ​ 
This story features moments of fantasy combat, emotional and psychological themes, and light romantic tension.Best suited for mature teens (16+) and adult readers.
