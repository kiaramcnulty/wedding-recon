About Me 
Sylwia D’Amico was born and raised in Poland and moved to the Vail Valley in her early twenties.  She instantly fell in love with the amazing lifestyle and beauty of Colorado. Soon enough Vail became her home, and her favorite playground.
Bio
She began her career in event planning by working at several hotels in the Vail Valley. With 20   years of experience  in hospitality and events, Sylwia D’Amico had planned and managed several weddings and special events for her clients. She has a wealth of experience and  knowledge in hospitality, and had worked for highly prestigious companies such as The Ritz Carlton, Four Seasons, Cascade Resort & Spa,  Arrabelle  & the Lodge at Vail.  After a very successful and rewarding career in Hospitality & Events department, and  with encouragement from previous clients, she decided to follow her dream by starting her own Wedding and Events planning business ~  Glitter & Bliss 
Sylwia D'Amico 
What We Will Do For You
Sylwia D’Amico is a passionate professional with an amazingly positive attitude,  energy, and outstanding work ethic. She is known for always having a big smile no matter what. You can count on her experience that she will always strive to deliver an exceptional wedding day that meets your highest expectations, wows your guests, and creates beautiful and treasured memories that will last a lifetime. She will  invest her time wisely into your wedding preparations, giving each tiny detail the fullest care and attention in order to create your perfect day. You can rest assured that she has incredibly high standards not only for herself but also for all the vendors involved in the wedding process. Her enthusiasm, combined with a renowned ability to offer calming and thoughtful support, will lead you into the planning and allow you to put your trust in everyone involved and stay at ease during your special day.
“I love that planning events allows me to combine both my passions and my strengths. Designing a vision, telling a story and orchestrating a performance all while collaborating with people from all walks of life. I find great satisfaction seeing how happy each client is with the end result of the events I plan. Additionally, and most noteworthy, the relationships that its formed with many of my clients last a lifetime.” 
Sylwia D'Amico
