Moments Matter
 Our Wedding Photography Philosophy
 Some call it photojournalism, candid, or modern wedding photography.
We’re not concerned with fitting into a mold or genre, but instead, our wedding photography style is capturing the
energy, emotion and love your day entails. Years from now, you'll look back on the photographs of moments with
family & loved ones more than the floral arrangements and scenery. It all matters, but moments matter the most.
