EasY Steps 
What now? 
Take your time to explore our Gallery and Stories . What do you feel? If they resonate with you, please schedule a free consultation with us . We reply immediately, or within 24-hours. Also, read our reviews .
Can we meet first? 
We meet all couples to help them with their wedding journey. We can’t tell much about you from texts or emails. Let’s get to know each other proper—a vibe check, some people say. This meeting can be done in person or over Zoom. Let’s schedule here .
Can we book now? 
Yes, of course! You can book us here . Some couples know we’d be perfect for their wedding. These weddings have often given us the best photos. If you’d like to see more talented wedding photographers in Singapore , here’s a useful list.

What to expect before the wedding? 
After the consultation, we will send our pricing guide. Once you’ve confirmed your package, there’ll be a 30% deposit to confirm the booking. We’ll meet to discuss the wedding program 2-3 weeks before the big day. Some couples prefer us to document their pre-wedding portraits. Here are more wedding photography venues to consider !
What happens during the wedding? 
We understand what it means to make timeless photographs. We do our best to capture the feel of the day. We’ll come as early as you allow us: from getting ready, to the ceremony, followed by portraits and the reception after.
How soon can we see our photographs? 
Within 1-2 days, we’ll send you 30-50 highlights. The full set and online slideshow arrives 5-7 working days later. Check our wedding gallery! Also, we’ll deliver a wooden box with a few token prints. Lastly, the fine art photo book within 3-4 weeks after proofing the spreads.
