Bishan-Ang Mo Kio Park
Mad love for nature? The perfect destination for your pre-wedding photo shoot. This scenic park is nestled in the heart of nature, providing a picturesque backdrop that will capture the essence of your love and create timeless memories. Let me paint a vivid picture of what awaits you. Have you considered looking at this list of wedding portrait photography locations for more inspiration? 
Boat Ride: Ubin to Changi
Not your ordinary pre-wedding photo shoot. Welcome aboard the "Love's Journey," a romantic boat ride tailored for your pre-wedding photo shoot. Embark on an unforgettable voyage, where the gentle sway of the boat, the soothing sound of water, and the breathtaking scenery will add an extra layer of magic to your love story.
BOTANIC GARDENS
The ever famous Botanic Gardens! With its iconic gazebos, manicured gardens, and varied botanical landscapes, it’s easy to see why the Gardens are so popular for pre-wedding photography!
BUKIT PASOH
A "Timeless Love Manor," a historical location that promises to make your pre-wedding shoot an unforgettable journey through the past. This stately estate is rich in history, architecture, and charm, providing the perfect backdrop for your special day.
BUKIT TIMAH RAILWAY
All aboard the "Vintage Romance Express" for a charming old-school pre-wedding shoot that captures the essence of timeless love. This vintage railway location is a delightful setting that will transport you and your partner to a bygone era, filled with nostalgia and romantic allure.
CHANGI COSTAL WALK
A coastal paradise that sets the stage for your enchanting pre-wedding shoot. With the sun-kissed beaches, azure waters, and the soothing sound of waves, this coastal location provides a perfect blend of romance and natural beauty.
CHANGI VILLAGE
A hidden gem tucked away in the east side of Singapore, where your pre-wedding shoot will be nothing short of extraordinary. This off-the-beaten-path location promises to surprise and enchant you with its unique beauty and secluded charm. 
EAST COAST
A destination that celebrates your love for the ocean and beach, providing the perfect backdrop for your pre-wedding shoot. This coastal paradise is a celebration of your deep connection to the sea, and it will capture the essence of your love amidst the sun, sand, and waves. Allow the beauty of the ocean and beach to be the canvas for your love story. Let the waves serenade you and the salty breeze embrace you as you create timeless memories in this coastal paradise.
HORT PARK
A hidden gem in Singapore that offers a tranquil and enchanting setting for your pre-wedding shoot. Tucked away from the hustle and bustle of the city, this park is a serene oasis that will provide a unique and intimate backdrop for capturing the essence of your love. Allowing you and your partner to escape the world and focus on each other in a secluded and intimate setting.
CHEK JAWA VISITOR CENTRE, PULAU UBIN
An intimate and secluded island paradise, perfect for your pre-wedding shoot away from the concrete jungle. This small island is a haven of natural beauty, offering a picturesque and idyllic setting for capturing your love amidst the untouched wilderness. Bringing back a little nostalgia.
JEWEL, CHANGI
Jewel the famous enchanting indoor shopping destination that brings the beauty of nature into a modern retail setting. This nature-inspired mall is the perfect location for your pre-wedding shoot, offering a delightful fusion of contemporary elegance and natural charm. Embrace the harmonious blend of nature and modernity. 
WESSEX ESTATE
Built in the 1940s, the Wessex estate provides an idyllic housing option for one-north’s working population. Nestled amid lush greenery off Portsdown Road. An enchanting old estate that provides a timeless and elegant backdrop for your pre-wedding shoot. Stepping into this historic location will transport you and your partner to a bygone era, where the grandeur of the past blends with the charm of the present. 
JOO CHIAT, SHOPHOUSES
A dream location for your pre-wedding shoot, where your love for old architectural buildings and tiles will be beautifully showcased. This heritage estate exudes timeless charm and elegance, pose with poise and grace, and let the vintage aesthetics inspire authentic and heartfelt moments.
LABRADOR PARK
Feeling a little nostalgic? A pre-wedding shoot destination that allows you and your partner to relive your cherished childhood moments together. This whimsical and sentimental location is designed to evoke a sense of nostalgia and playfulness, creating a unique and heartwarming experience for both of you.
ASIAN CIVILISATION MUSEUM
A historical building that offers a clean and sophisticated look for your wedding shoot. Let the historical building's timeless beauty and refined details inspire authentic and intimate shots that capture the significance of your love on this special day.
chijmes
The epitome of rich, elegant, and clean luxury for your wedding photo shoot. This magnificent estate exudes opulence and sophistication, providing a refined and pristine shot that complements the beauty of your special day.
BUKIT TIMAH RAILWAY TRACKS
A pre-wedding shoot location that combines the charm of vintage aesthetics with the romance of a railway track setting. This nostalgic destination will transport you and your partner back in time, capturing the essence of a bygone era and the eternal journey of your love. 
SELETAR AIRPORT
A unique and enchanting location that blends the excitement of travel with the tranquility of lush greenery for your wedding shoot. This airport oasis provides a one-of-a-kind setting where the spirit of adventure meets the beauty of nature. The beauty of nature all around you as you create timeless and enchanting memories in this unique location.
SEMBAWANG BEACH
Another hidden gem nestled in the north-west of Singapore, boasting historical buildings that exude charm and character. This unique location offers a perfect blend of history and nostalgia. Embrace the nostalgia and beauty of this hidden gem as you create lasting memories in a location that resonates with the past.
TIONG BAHRU SIT FLATS
Another one of Singapore’s oldest flats worth capturing memories at. The SIT flats along Tiong Bahru in the estate are a beautiful testament to the Art Deco period, which was fashionable at that time. The combination of a rich heritage as well as charming lifestyle and hospitality options make this neighbourhood a must-see.
PULAU UBIN
A captivating destination that embraces the charm of island life and the tranquility of a quaint village. This picturesque location provides the perfect shot for your pre-wedding shoot, capturing the essence of your love amidst the serene and idyllic setting. Embrace the beauty of nature and the charm of the village setting as you create lasting memories in a location that celebrates the tranquility and romance of island life.
dakota playground
Feeling a little nostalgia? One of the many concrete playgrounds in Singapore is here to help you relive those memories with your partner. Capturing the essence of playfulness and nostalgia.
