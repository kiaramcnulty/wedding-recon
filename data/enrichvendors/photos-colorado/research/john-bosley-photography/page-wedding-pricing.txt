Wedding Pricing
Each year I photograph a limited number of weddings. This allows me to pour all of my energy and creativity into every single wedding I photograph. Wedding photography packages start at $2800. All packages include an engagement session.
 My Style
 I’m not into posing and making you look like someone that you’re not. I think the most unique thing I could ever photograph is your relationship, so that’s what I focus on. I try to get you to laugh, to love, and most importantly, to give me real, authentic smiles. We&#8217;ll definitely get some &#8220;look at the camera and give me a smile&#8221; photos, but believe the best photos happen when you&#8217;re looking at each other, being yourselves.
 I’m a laid back guy and that pretty much sums up my shooting style. Don’t mistake that for lazy. I’m hustling all day to get the shots. I just don’t let things ruffle my feathers on your wedding day. I also approach things with more of an photojournalistic eye. I want to photograph your wedding day as it happens, capturing moments as they unfold. Hopefully you’ll just forget I’m around and enjoy your day!
