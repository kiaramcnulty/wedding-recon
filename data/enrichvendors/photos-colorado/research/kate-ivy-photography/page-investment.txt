The Extras
 Travel
 Travel fees may apply for weddings, elopements, and engagement sessions that are further than an hour and a half from Denver. Inquire here for a custom quote. Check out a recent wedding shot in Valle de Guadalupe, Mexico. 
 Engagement Session $950
 This includes rights to at least 75 high res, edited photos. Gallery to share. Travel fees may apply to locations and hour and a half outside of Denver. Session lasts up to 1.5 hours. Photos delivered within 2 weeks. Ask me about my favorite locations. 
 Extra Hour $800
 An extra hour can be added onto any package. I do my best to not overcharge or oversell you anything you may not need or want. However, the official timeline is not usually confirmed until one month before the wedding or elopement in which case an extra hour may be needed if you choose. 
 Film $600
 For couples who love nostalgia and imperfection. Film captures moments in a softer, dreamier way — subtle grain, rich tones, and that timeless feeling digital just can’t replicate. It’s slower, intentional, and incredibly beautiful. Perfect for those who want their photos to feel like memories, not just images. For a highlight gallery of ~ 40–60 film scans (4-6 rolls) is an additional $600; film is included in the elopement package. Check out some of my film work here. 
 After wedding adventure Session $950
 A sunrise or golden hour session within two months of your wedding, where you get back into your wedding attire and get the epic photos without being on a strict timeline. This includes rights to high-res, edited photos. Gallery to share. Travel fees may apply to locations 45 minutes outside of Denver. Session lasts up to 1.5 hours. 
 “Bridal” Session $800
 A bridal session is a photo shoot with the bride in full wedding attire, hair, and makeup, typically taken before the wedding day. It serves as a stress-free opportunity for the bride to get portraits taken, test her complete look, break in her wedding shoes, and make any necessary adjustments before the big day. A print from this session is often enlarged and displayed at the wedding reception. You will receive around 40 -50 high-res edited photos within two weeks of the session. Session lasts around one hour. 
 Photo Booth $600
 We have our own high end photo booth and will hire an assistant to help your guests get the best photos. Pricing starts at $600 for a minimum of 3 hours. Click here for more info. 
 Video $2400
 I also offer video! Starting at a six hour package for $2400. You receive a wedding film that is 4-6 minutes long. As well as a sneak peek video. Add-on options after that. Inquire further for more details! 
 Associate Photography
 Hopefully, I am available for your day! But if I am not - there is another option! If you love the look and feel of my photos, we can still make that happen. I work with a few other photographers as my associates (we trade off working with each other). Because you won’t be getting me as your head photographer that day this package comes at a discount! The contract and editing will all be through me and my associate will be the one capturing your day. The associates I work with are all at the same (or better) skill set and price level.
