Wedding Photography That Feels Like You * 
Your wedding isn’t a photoshoot — it’s a lived experience filled with emotion, movement, and the people you love most. As a Denver wedding photographer , my approach is documentary, intentional, and deeply human. I focus on real moments as they unfold, not stiff posing or forced perfection.
 I photograph weddings throughout Denver, Colorado, and beyond, working with couples who care more about meaning than performance. As a queer-owned business, inclusivity isn’t an add-on — it’s foundational.
 Wedding Photography Packages
 I offer wedding photography collections designed to support different types of celebrations, without forcing couples into a one-size-fits-all structure.
 Full Weekend Wedding Coverage 
Ideal for couples planning multi-day celebrations. This can include welcome parties, rehearsal dinners, the wedding day, and even day-after sessions. Full weekend coverage allows me to document your story more fully and naturally, without rushing through moments.
 Wedding Day Coverage (Starting at 6 Hours) 
Perfect for couples who want focused coverage of the heart of their wedding day. Six hours is often ideal for ceremonies, portraits, and celebrations, with additional hours available as needed. Check out my Investment page for full pricing and packages.
 All wedding collections include:
 Timeline guidance and planning support
A curated gallery of fully edited, high-resolution images
Online gallery for sharing and downloads
Access to albums and heirloom print products
What It’s Like to Work Together
 I’ll guide you when it’s helpful and step back when moments are unfolding naturally. My goal is for you to feel comfortable, grounded, and fully present — not aware of the camera every second.
 If you’re planning something more intimate, you may also be interested in my elopement photography . For couples who love a nostalgic feel, 35mm film photography is available as an add-on. A modern wedding photo booth can also be added to wedding collections for a playful guest experience.
