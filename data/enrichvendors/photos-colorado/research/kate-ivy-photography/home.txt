Photography Services for the Wildly in Love 
Photo by Kalen Jesse 
Hey Lovers
 Welcome to the love club! Now, tell me your love story (give me all the juicy details). I want to turn your story into a million (or maybe more like 600) photos and moments. I want your grandkids and your grandma to hang these photos up and shed one single tear every time they look at them.
 What Do I Offer? 
 Wedding photography (film and digital). Whether you are eloping or having 200 guest - let’s chat about your vision and if we are a good fit! I also offer couples sessions - engagement or anniversary or if you are just wanting to do a creative lifestyle shoot to capture your love. I’m ya girl!
 Values
 How did I get so lucky? My younger self would die if I told her instead of being a marine biologist you are going to be a wedding photographer lol. On top of all this, I get to shoot most (if not all) weddings with my partner (as in ROMANTIC partner) Taylor - and together we make the dream team. OH and just to make it crystal clear: As wedding photographers in Colorado, we celebrate LGBTQIA and polyamorous unities, people of every size, religion, ethnicity, ability or disability, and those who don’t conform to tradition or expectations. We also have a vendor list of others who share the same values. LET’S GO!!!
 PS. If you are BIPOC and live in Denver I am offering free headshots. And discounts on other sessions!
