What I DEEPLY value 
Authenticity 
 With my background in photojournalism, honest documentation has always been a pillar of my work. A reason I love photography so much is because I get to be a witness to so many life-altering, love-filled moments. The connection you share for your loved ones cannot be manufactured. I’ll be there to document those organic moments of laughter, tears, and overwhelming joy. 
Ease 
 Creating an environment where you feel 100% comfortable and free to be yourself is extremely important to me. Having your photo taken can be intimidating, so I take a laidback, stress-free approach. I want our time together to feel like friends hanging out and having fun. When you’re relaxed, you’re able to let your guard down and invite me into your world — THAT’S when the magic happens. 
Nostalgia 
 For as long as I can remember, I’ve been an incredibly sentimental person. Having photographs of our memories, big or small, helps us feel more connected to our loved ones as well as the world around us. Your children, your grandchildren, your great-grandchildren will pass these down for generations to come. I’m not only taking your photo but creating a family heirloom. 
“ Taking photos can feel uncomfortable and stiff and it felt like we were just hanging out with a friend rather than taking a ton of direction from someone we just hired. Her editing style and style of capturing moments are so unique and raw and make you feel like your family and emotions are really captured in photos. ”
