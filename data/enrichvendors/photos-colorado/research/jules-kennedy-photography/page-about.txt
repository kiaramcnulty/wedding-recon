I believe everyone deserves, heartwarming, soul-reaching, timelessly nostalgic photographs of this messy thing called life.
When I was young, my mom would always have a camera in her hands. I learned quickly just how important photographs are to preserve the memories with the people that mean the most to us. 
 Let's just say I am a feeler, to put it lightly. I feel connected to the people and places around me quite deeply and have such a passion for capturing that connection with my camera—telling stories untold and documenting moments often overlooked. Now of course, I have other great loves that include traveling, my two cats, my cute nieces and the entire month of October, but there's something about the sound of a camera's shutter that sets my soul on fire.

 HI, I’M 
JULES 
WEDDING, FAMILY, & LIFESTYLE PHOTOGRAPHER 
After documenting moments and love stories in Chicago for 7 years, I finally decided to listen to my heart and move out west to the mountains of Colorado. It’s only been a few months, and I already feel more at home than ever before. I may always be a midwest gal deep down, but there’s something so rich and fulfilling about having a mountain range right outside my front door.
Be sure to follow me on Instagram to be up to date on future travels. If I’m in your city, I’d love to connect and document your story.
