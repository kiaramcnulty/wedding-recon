Blue Spruce Wedding Photography: Who Are We? 
The Blue Spruce tree is one of the beautiful evergreen trees native to the rocky mountains of Colorado. A popular ornamental staple due to its natural beauty, it is no surprise that it was hailed as Colorado&#8217;s State Tree. And we believe that it perfectly represents what we do here at Blue Spruce Wedding Photography. 
Just like how the blue spruce tree has its roots deep into the soils of Colorado, Blue Spruce Photography is here to help you dig your own roots by creating memories that your family will cherish for a lifetime. We strive to capture all of the important events, stunning details, and spectrum of emotions before and during your special day. 
The Creative Mind Behind Blue Spruce Wedding Photography 
Blue Spruce is spearheaded by professional photographer Beau Blackburn. His passion for photography developed at an early age and it eventually led him to Ringling College of Art and Design in Sarasota, FL, where he earned a bachelors degree in Photography. After six years of a successful photography career in San Diego, CA, he relocated to Denver to be with the love of his life and pursue the art of wedding photography.
