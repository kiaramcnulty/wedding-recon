Hello, I am Tomi! Photographer, husband, dog owner and traveler. I am also a big nature, sports and Pink Floyd fan.
I am a full time Wedding Photographer based in Broomfield, Colorado, and I often travel to Chicago and Europe for family and weddings &#128248;
I capture authentic, creative and emotionally driven wedding moments. My photography style has a mixture of unique candid photos combined with some traditionally posed shots, all edited with a more warm and true-to-life color editing style.
What sets me apart? My unique way of capturing genuine moments throughout every wedding day. Like a ninja who is always ready to see and capture the best of you! On top of that I have a fun accent that makes everyone ask me where I am from haha, combined with the must fun energy and professionalism. Just say "I do" to my Facetime\Video call, let me get to know your love story and you can be sure I'll become your best friend, your hype man, your adventure buddy and whatever you need me to be + your positive photographer &#128248;
