travel & Taxes
• Travel is charged at $1/mile round trip to your reception location. 
• The city center of Denver is used as the starting address. 
• If you have a second shooter, the travel fee is doubled. 
• For weddings over 200 miles round trip hotel room(s) may be required for each photographer booked. 
• Local taxes are added when required.
