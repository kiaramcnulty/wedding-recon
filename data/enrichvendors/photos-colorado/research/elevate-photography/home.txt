Denver Wedding Photographers You Can Trust
At Elevate Photography, experience matters. Our team has photographed over 1,500 weddings since 2001, giving us the ability to anticipate moments, adapt quickly, and deliver consistently beautiful results. As experienced Denver wedding photographers, we know how to handle changing timelines, unpredictable weather, difficult lighting, and all of the little moments that make a wedding day unique.
We believe great wedding photography is about more than beautiful images. It is about creating an experience where you feel comfortable, present, and able to fully enjoy your wedding day while we document it naturally.
Denver Wedding Venues We’ve Photographed
Denver is one of the best cities in the country for wedding photography, offering everything from elegant historic venues and modern downtown spaces to nearby mountain views. Over the years, we have photographed weddings at many of Denver’s most popular venues, including Magnolia Hotel, The University Club, Denver Botanic Gardens, Seawell Ballroom, History Colorado Center, Grand Hyatt, blanc Denver, The Brown Palace Hotel and Spa, Mile High Station, Artwork Network, Four Seasons Denver, Denver Athletic Club, and Kevin Taylor’s at the Opera House.
You can explore real weddings from many of these venues through the links above, and over time we’ll continue adding dedicated venue guides for couples searching for a photographer with experience at their specific location.
Wedding Photography Across Colorado
While many couples find us while searching for a Denver wedding photographer, we photograph weddings throughout Colorado. Whether you are planning a city celebration, a mountain wedding, or a destination event, our team brings the same consistent experience and storytelling approach.
If you are planning outside Denver, you can also explore our pages for Colorado Wedding Photographer , Boulder Wedding Photographer , Colorado Springs Wedding Photographer , Vail Wedding Photographer , Aspen Wedding Photographer , Breckenridge Wedding Photographer , South Denver Wedding Photographer , and Rocky Mountain National Park Wedding Photographer .
Colorado Mountain Wedding Photography
Colorado is one of the most beautiful places in the world to get married, and many couples choose the mountains for their wedding day or engagement session. From dramatic alpine views to charming mountain towns, we love helping couples create images that feel adventurous, timeless, and uniquely Colorado.
If you are planning a mountain celebration, learn more about working with a Colorado Mountain Wedding Photographer .
Why Couples Choose Elevate Photography
What sets us apart is our team approach. Our photographers share ideas, locations, techniques, and years of experience so every couple benefits from the knowledge of the entire team. That collaboration helps us stay creative while also giving our couples something incredibly valuable: built-in backup and peace of mind.
Most importantly, we focus on real relationships. When you feel comfortable with your photographer, your images feel natural, authentic, and full of genuine connection.
Denver Wedding Photography FAQs
How much does a Denver wedding photographer cost?
Wedding photography pricing in Denver varies depending on coverage, photographers, and services included. You can explore our pricing page to build a package that fits your wedding.
Do you photograph weddings outside Denver?
Yes. While we are based in Denver, we photograph weddings throughout Colorado and beyond.
Do you offer engagement sessions?
Yes. Engagement sessions are a great way to get comfortable in front of the camera while creating beautiful images in Denver, the mountains, or another meaningful location.
Have you photographed at our wedding venue?
There is a good chance we have. We have photographed weddings at venues throughout Denver and Colorado, and we continue expanding our venue-specific resources.
