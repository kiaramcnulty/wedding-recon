Wild Fern is our passion project.
 Established in 2014, the husband-wife duo Kim Klein and Kyle Houghtaling bring a unique combination of Kyle’s love for the rugged Colorado landscape and Kim’s eye for portrait photography to their work.
 Our goal is to keep this business small and close to our hearts. We limit our bookings to 10 couples per year to provide personalized service and create a memorable experience for each client.

FAQ
How long have you been a wedding photographer? 
 Kim started photographing weddings professionally in 2014, working closely with two established wedding photographers all over the state of Colorado. In 2018 she launched her own business, Wild Fern Photo Co. Kim and Kyle met in 2020, and shortly after they started photographing and adventuring together! 
 Can we view complete galleries? 
 Yes! We would love for you to get a feel of what your final gallery might look like. Typically we deliver in the range of 60 images per hour of coverage. Our goal is to make it easy to relive your wedding day in detail for years to come. 
 Please contact us for complete gallery links!
what equipment do you use?
 We shoot Nikon Z-series mirrorless cameras with Nikkor mirrorless lenses. This is Nikon’s latest line of cameras and lenses. They are known for having the widest lens mount of any digital camera, which means they let in more light creating a faster performance. We shoot matching camera bodies with different lenses throughout the wedding day, making a nice variety of images for the final gallery. 
 When will we be able to view the edited photos?
 Our gallery delivery window is just 30 days after your photos were taken. For weddings we deliver quite close to the 30-day mark, but portrait sessions often are returned even sooner than that! We truly mean it when we say we cannot wait for you to see your finished images!
 Does kyle always wear a hat?
 Yes, yes he does. He has two different hat looks available for weddings, we are happy to provide more details if you’re interested ;)
