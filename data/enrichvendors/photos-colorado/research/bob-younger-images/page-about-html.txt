About Bob Younger
It's all about relationships. Bob Younger is not just a photographer. Bob is dedicated to his craft. Bob has travelled the country providing in depth, personalized service to his photography family. With international awards and recognition, Bob is forging a new direction for portraiture, wedding, senior photos and fashion photography. His work has been recognized in La Jolla, CA, St. Louis MO, Chicago IL, and Las Vegas NV. 
"We are constantly working to not only provide the highest quality photography for our clients, but world class customer service. We aren't just a shoot and burn studio. We provide individualized, customized shoots that will not look like everyone else's." 
Your photos will be dramatic, meaningful and lit professionally with artistic intent. Bob also is an instructor, teaching the next generation of photographers how to create incredible imagery. He has provided classes to new and professional photographers. Bob loves to travel for his work, his favorite locations include La Jolla, CA and Chicago, IL.
