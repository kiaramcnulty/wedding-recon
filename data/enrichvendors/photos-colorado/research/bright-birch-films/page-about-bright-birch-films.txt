Curating Authentic Stories
Bright Birch Film Co. is a tiny production team and we put a heavy emphasis on organic maker-style videos and photo.
We prioritize the look and feel of true cinematic productions. The core of everything we do is a human story – we create narrative films that blend music, voice and image to crystallize the big and small moments in life.
Hey, I'm Bryan Way! My film work lead to an interest in wedding videography and elopement photography, and I quickly fell in love with finding ways to make these projects as unique as possible.
My passion became capturing the lives of clients and creating a lasting film that will be cherished. That is why Bright Birch was born; as an outlet for professional films at a personal level.
We create cinematic videos for weddings and adventure elopement photography in the Rocky Mountains. However, we will shoot anywhere in the country/world, and regularly travel across the States to film our clients' special days.
Our focus is collaboration: We want to take your idea and turn it into something tangible, beautiful, and emotional. Nostalgic, atmospheric work is what we specialize in, and we shoot to emulate the look of the medium of film.
