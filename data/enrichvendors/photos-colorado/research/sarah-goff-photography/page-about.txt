ABOUT SARAH GOFF PHOTOGRAPHY
"Sarah set us both right at ease and captured all our love and joy in a way that felt natural and true to us".
For wildly devoted and joyful couples who want a stress-free and guided photo experience.
Hey, I'm Sarah.
I&#8217;m a color-obsessed Colorado native who lives for epic celebrations. I have been photographing weddings since 2015. My own fairytale marriage is the driving force behind my love of celebration. I met my husband, Brian, in 2006 during a summer of working together. In October of 2007, he popped the question at Lake Isabelle and it was an easy answer: YES! I immediately began wedding planning and in May 2009, we had our dream wedding at Tapestry House. We began our marriage journey on the shoulders of our loved ones. We are extremely passionate about our purpose as a couple and spending time together enjoying our favorite hobbies: traveling the world, dipping our feet in mountain streams, chasing our favorite food trucks, sipping new tastes at local breweries, watching Chuck or Psych, and going for sunset walks. 
WE ARE 100% A TEAM IN LIFE
 and 100% DEDICATED TO
MAKING YOUR WEDDING DAY EXTRAORDINARY.
Brian is my biggest supporter and is an integral part of the experience. He is a music teacher by weekday, wedding-dress-fluffer-expert by weekend.
 My experience of personal loss and passion for extraordinary marriage fuel my commitment for wildly devoted and joyful couples who want to share the beauty of the state with the people they love most and want to feel stress-free and incredible in the process.
 I&#8217;m a type-A introvert who craves a good party and consider myself a creative nerd. I have an insatiable need to keep learning and I&#8217;m utterly fascinated by nature, easily spending an entire afternoon just watching the clouds float by. I am an animal person to my core, so yes &#8211; bring your dog!
