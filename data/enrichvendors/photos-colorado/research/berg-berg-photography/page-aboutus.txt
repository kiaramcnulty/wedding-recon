Berg Berg Photography is an adventurous , authentic and inclusive wedding photography business in Golden, Colorado. 
 We’re an ADVENTURE-loving husband/wife team with over 20 years of professional experience capturing artistic photographic memories for couples and families. Having found each other later later in life, we bring a different kind of understanding to the couples we photograph. We know what it feels like when a moment actually means something. 
 AUTHENTIC means your photos will be timeless, you will look like you without the latest filter fad so you won’t look back on your photos and cringe. We are both artists , not just people with fancy cameras. We use an unobtrusive , journalistic style to tell your story, plus we’ll take charge and make sure we get the all-important family photos while everyone is together to celebrate your wedding. 
 We are looking for the elements of your style, emotional moments and details of your wedding or event that make it uniquely yours. We are especially interested in capturing the energy and connection you have and will accomplish that with coaching when you’re unsure what to do or are afraid you may feel awkward. We have photographed in all types of Colorado weather and are prepared for anything . We do not outsource, we edit all of our own images. We carry insurance required for many Colorado venues. Most of our work is near Golden but we will also travel anywhere.
 INCLUSIVE is not just a token buzzword to us. We have had LGBTQ family and chosen family our whole lives. Love is love, we see you and support you. We have photographed Christian, Hindu, Muslim, Jewish, Buddhist and non-religious weddings and have the experience to photograph your important moments with planning, skill and respect. We are there to document your day however you choose to celebrate. Hopefully you will bring your dogs !
Kevin Bergthold
Lead Photographer
Kevin has been an award winning professional photographer since 2004. He is a member of the international Wedding Photojournalist Association currently ranked as one of the Top 10 Wedding Photographers in Colorado and 133rd in the World for 2025 . His work has also been featured in Rock n Roll Bride . 
 Kevin grew up in the thick of skateboarding and punk rock near Sacramento, California where he skateboarded and worked with his brother who started Blockhead Skateboards in their family’s garage. He approaches his work with an alternative eye, not always following the rules learned at Brooks Institute studying for his Bachelor of Arts Degree in Professional Photography . 
 He has photographed 300+ weddings . Living in Colorado since 2006, he’s had the opportunity to work and explore all over the state. Kevin is a Photoshop expert, we don’t outsource anything, every photo is hand edited with his expertise. Latest obsession: Adding 35mm film photography to a shoot.
 Favorite Adventure- Dropping into the 100 mile White Rim Trail in Utah (unsupported) for our first bikepacking trip. It was full commitment and amazing!
Michelle Blomberg
Second Photographer
Michelle has a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Visual Communications and a Master’s of Education degree. She worked in web design and marketing in Ann Arbor and Detroit, Michigan. She is currently faculty at Glendale Community College near Phoenix where they also have a home and works as a second photographer for Berg Berg.
 Michelle has photographed 50+ weddings and is a member of Fearless Photographers , a competitive group of international wedding photographers. Her photographic style is influenced by her background in design and ability to connect with her subjects. Upon meeting Kevin in 2015, they have collaborated professionally and in 2021 rebranded his photography business as Berg Berg to reflect their partnership as photographers.
 Favorite adventure- Camping and mountain biking in Gallup?! Who knew it was full of beautiful red rocks, mountain trails and undiscovered retro charm? 
Why Berg Berg?
 As a husband/wife team, we don’t have the same last name but the names both have a “berg” or “mountain” in Swedish. We enjoy our mountains every chance we get, on and off shoot. We were married in a fever with pretzel rings when the world seemed to be falling apart in March 2020 and haven’t looked back. When we’re not photographing kick ass couples, you will find us with or without our Border Collies, Rocket and Ryder and our 4 teens, riding mountain bikes , snowboarding , skateboarding , climbing , vanlifing , at one of our favorite local breweries or watching Star Trek.
