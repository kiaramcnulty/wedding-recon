Hello! We're Kevin and Michelle, a husband-and-wife team based in Golden, Colorado with over 20 years experience photographing weddings. We met later in life, which means we understand that love doesn't follow a schedule. Our style is photojournalistic and timeless, no filter trends, and no forced poses. Just your day, exactly as it happened.
