Let's get to know each other!
Veronica Stanley
Hi I'm Your Photographer and Videographer,
I am located in the western slopes of Colorado, What started as a simple hobby quickly transformed into something more. I absolutely love capturing those extraordinary moments that hold a special place in your heart and transforming them into everlasting memories. My goal is to assist you in preserving those cherished moments that deserve to be remembered. Through my years of experience and constant learning, I have honed my skills in capturing those candid and genuine moments that often go unnoticed.
Being a photographer means being a witness to love, joy, and happiness. I am your assistant in freezing time, so you can always look back on those moments and feel the same emotions you felt when they were happening. It's the magic of photography - being able to capture the essence of a moment and preserve it forever.
Before any shoot, I take the time to understand your vision and desires. Your story matters, and I believe in creating a collaborative atmosphere where we can bring that story to life through imagery. Photography is not just about capturing pretty pictures; it's about authentically reflecting who you are and the emotions you are experiencing in that moment.
When I am behind the lens, I pay attention to every detail, ensuring that every photograph reflects the emotions and the beauty of the moment. I aim to make you feel comfortable and at ease, so that the true essence of who you are shines through in the photographs.
Melissa Miller
Second Shooter at V's Photography & Media
I'm Melissa, and I'm thrilled to be part of the V's Photography team alongside Veronica. I live in Montrose, Colorado, with my husband and our two adventurous pups. Living here means I'm constantly surrounded by the breathtaking beauty of the Western Slope&mdash;a daily inspiration that fuels my passion for photography.
For me, photography is about more than just capturing images; it's about telling stories. I love connecting with clients, understanding their unique visions, and bringing those ideas to life through my lens. Whether it's a candid laugh, a tender glance, or the majestic backdrop of Colorado's landscapes,
I aim to create photographs that resonate and endure.
Working with Veronica has been an incredible journey. Her dedication to capturing genuine moments and her commitment to making clients feel comfortable and celebrated align perfectly with my own approach. Together, we strive to provide an experience that's not only professional but also personal and heartfelt.
When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring new trails, soaking in the natural wonders of our state, or spending quality time with my family. These experiences enrich my perspective and infuse my work with authenticity and warmth.
I'm excited to meet you and help tell your story through images that you'll cherish for years to come.

Frequently Asked Questions
ARE YOU AVAILABLE OUTSIDE OF MESA COUNTY OR COLORADO? 
Yes Absolutely! We travel all over where we are needed.
THIS IS MY FIRST PHOTOSHOOT AND I'M KIND OF NERVOUS. 
It's completely normal to be nervous or a little anxious a lot of people are camera shy. But I will do my absolute best to make you feel comfortable and by the end of the session you'll leave feeling confident and excited.
CAN YOU RECOMMEND LOCATIONS. 
Yes! if you don't already have a location in mind just let me know and I can recommend a location based off what look your going for.
WHAT KIND OF PHOTOGRAPHY DO YOU DO. 
We do all kinds from Wedding and Events to families and individuals. If you need us we will be there to capture that moment.
HOW MANY PICTURES WILL I RECEIVE? 
The amount of images is depending on the package that you choose to go with.
WHAT SHOULD I WEAR? 
Outfit choices are completely up to you if your session is going to be outdoors with mother nature you might want to wear vibrant colors, and if your going with a busy background such as downtown or in a public area I would suggest a more neutral color. At the end of the day the choice is yours on what you wear and if you need assistance just let me know I'm more then happy to help you pick.
