Saige creates timeless images for couples with a classic style, beautifully capturing each unique love story. Utilizing natural light and stunning backdrops, her photography reflects the extraordinary beauty of each moment. She blends an artistic vision with documentary-style storytelling so you can truly relive the emotions and intricate details of your wedding day. From delicate lace to intimate ceremony moments, each image evokes a deep sense of nostalgia and sentiment.
 Aspen Sage Photo photographs weddings, elopements, and portraits in Aspen, Carbondale, and Glenwood Springs, Colorado, and also serves all of Colorado and surrounding states. Aspen Sage additionally photographs in upstate New York—including, Hudson Valley Cragsmoor and nearby areas—bringing the same approach and care wherever the location.
Wedding photos and portraits are best when they feel natural and genuine—when you’re relaxed, your true emotions and connections show. Keeping things calm and easy lets everyone be present, so images capture honest, heartfelt moments. 
 I’ll be right there with you, helping keep things comfortable and making the experience as fun as possible so your photos tell your real story.
 Getting married or eloping in Aspen, New York, or Puerto Rico brings a unique sense of adventure and fun—each location offers its own backdrop and energy that make the day memorable. Aspen’s alpine peaks and golden aspens lend a dramatic, outdoorsy thrill; New York provides vibrant city energy or quiet, intimate spots in parks and rooftops; Puerto Rico delivers sun-soaked beaches, lush tropical landscapes, and a relaxed island vibe.

Meet your Photographer, Saige. 
Originally from the Lower East Side New York , I’ve called the Roaring Fork Valley home for 15 years while spending my winters in the warmth of family in Bayamón, Puerto Rico. 
 Being from many different cultures has made every wedding feel like home. While I love photographing the big days and special moments. I simply love making clients feel comfortable and getting to witness incredible times in their lives.
 Photographing weddings and elopements in Aspen, Glenwood Springs, and Snowmass Colorado have always been a favorite. Elopements in the Rocky Mountain National Park continue to keep me in awe. Upstate New York Weddings and Elopements have always felt like home. Let’s not forget to mention the dancing and food in all the San Juan, Puerto Rico elopements. Every place has a room in my heart.
 After earning my photography degree from Colorado Mountain College in 2015, I took the reins on the business side of my photography career. With over 14 years of experience in elopement, event, journalism, and fashion photography.
