Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What types of photography services are offered? 
A: Wedding, elopement, and portrait photography services are available to capture a variety of special moments.
 Q: How can a package be customized? 
A: Packages can be tailored to individual needs through a personalized consultation, Depending on hours needed or how many photographers wanted.
 Q: What is the Process to book? 
A: Once availability and budget are locked in (or as close as we can get). You will receive a contract and invoice for deposit. Deposits are usually 30% of the total cost. once everything is finalized you can sit back and relax. 
 Q: How soon should a photographer be booked? 
A: It is recommended to book as early as possible, but last minute is totally ok because life never goes as expected. If there’s no availability I will give some recommendations of other photographers in the area.
 Q: Are engagement or pre-wedding sessions included? 
A: Engagement sessions can often be included or added to packages for an additional fee.
 Q: How long does it take to receive the final photos? 
A: Delivery time varies, but typically final images for a wedding are provided within 4 to 8 weeks after the event.
 Q: Can photos be printed or shared online? 
A: Yes, high-resolution digital files are usually provided for printing and online sharing rights.
