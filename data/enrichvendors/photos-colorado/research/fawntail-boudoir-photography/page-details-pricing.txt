SESSION PRICING 
 A Simple, Transparent Approach
 I don’t believe in high-pressure sales or hidden costs. Everything is explained clearly before you book, and nothing is sprung on you after your session. I don’t charge session or sitting fees, or make you buy photos after the session. Every image I edit for you, I deliver to you . Even if I gift you a few extra, I never up-charge you or pressure you to buy more. Printed items like heirloom albums, wall art, and fine art prints, are available at the highest quality possible, and can be added on at any point.
 My goal is for you to feel relaxed and confident about the experience, the pricing, and your decision to be here.

FAQs
How far in advance should I book my session? 
It’s always advised to contact me as soon as possible. Dates book up several months in advance depending on the month, or I might have a last minute opening next week. The turnaround time is about 3-4 weeks including ship times so plan accordingly for special events. If you’re a planner and you’re really far out, it doesn’t hurt to go ahead and get in touch so we can start planning, or reserve a date if you have one in mind. I have started chatting with future clients as much as 2 years before their sessions! 
I'm a little nervous, but excited, will you guide me? 
It’s totally normal to be nervous! We always start out super easy with a pose that doesn’t require you to do very much so you can get used to me directing and guiding you, and then flow through the rest of your session while we chat. It’s easier than you think, I promise! 
 If this is something you want to do (if you’re reading this, I’m guessing it is!) then please don’t let nerves rob you of this empowering experience, you deserve to do this and you can do it! 
I’ve never done this before, how should I prepare? 
I usually tell clients to think of your session as a special event. You want to feel your best, but you don’t need to go overboard. When we chat I’ll go over the important stuff, and you’ll get access to my full timeline checklist that has everything you’ll need to get ready for your day in front of the camera.
I have a specific vision in mind, can I customize a session? 
I’m always up for a fun, creative challenge! Let me know what you have in mind, and we can probably make it happen!
Do I need to get naked? 
What you show is totally up to you! You can cover up entirely, stick a leg out, or bare it all for a few shots if you want. 
 Plenty of clients do a hybrid with some more clothed, and some nude. You can always see how you feel on the day. 
 Even if you are completely nude, rest assured that my images are always tasteful and classic. 
 You don’t even have to wear lingerie if that’s not your thing. A tank top, slouchy sweater over a bralette, flowy shirt, even leggings, there are so many ways to customize a session to fit your comfort and taste. 
What should I wear? Do I need to bring my own outfits? 
Because I photograph women of all different sizes, I do not keep more than a few universal prop pieces at the studio (I have a few lacy robes, a veil, some fun fabric.)
 We’ll thoroughly go over wardrobe when we chat, but depending on which session you book you’ll want to bring a few outfits. My advice is to start in your closet, you may have a simple black bra & underwear set, or a bodysuit, paired with a sweater, white shirt, jean jacket, leather jacket, tank top, whatever you have and there you go! So many options to choose from already!
 If you do want to do some shopping, I’ll make some suggestions when we chat about what I think might fit your style best, and share my master list of lingerie sites with you.
Are my photos private? 
Your images are completely private to you unless you choose otherwise. I never post your photos anywhere without your permission. After your session you’ll have the opportunity to decide which, if any, photos you are comfortable sharing, and if you don't want to share any, that's totally ok! 
How long will it take to see my photos/get my prints? 
After your session it typically takes 2 weeks for me to edit your session. 
 You’ll get a link to your private gallery and/or flash drive with images on it once they are done being edited.
 If you’d like to add on an album or prints, we’ll talk about it once you have had time to see your photos and I’ll walk you through the design process.
