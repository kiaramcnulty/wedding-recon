Colorado Engagement Photographer
Recently Engaged? CONGRATULATIONS!
I'm based out of the Denver Metro Area, photographing engagements throughout the state of Colorado.
Adventure can mean anything.
 I'm well versed in all of the amazing places that Colorado has to offer - everything from toasting your engagement at 14,000' to those just outside the car door views.
Looking for Wildflowers in early Summer? The San Juan Mountains offer amazing wildflowers with otherworldly views.
The best fall foliage? I've never seen as much Yellow as I have in Telluride or Crested Butte.
More of a Winter couple? Dream Lake is a short hike where you can walk out onto the ice together and watch the sun come up over the horizon.
