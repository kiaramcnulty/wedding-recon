Frequently Asked Questions
01 | Do you travel?
Yes, I'm documenting destination weddings and elopements throughout the year. Although I'm based in the mountain west, I'm experienced in many destinations across the United States and internationally. Inquire about my travel schedule if you'd like to take advantage of local rates. 
02 | What does turnaround look like?
Depending on your session type (portraits or events) the turn-around will vary. I include a 48 hour sneak peek for all weddings and events, and you full gallery or videos in up to ten weeks following that. Portrait sessions have between a two and four week turnaround. Video turnaround is 8-10 weeks. 
03 | How is payment made?
Portrait sessions required a 50% retainer to confirm your session, while weddings and events have a variable retainer based on package. I offer a payment plan option and payment is handled through a secure portal online when you sign your booking contract. 
04 | Why you?
I truly enjoy capturing a little bit of your story and getting to know each client that I work with. Experience and care is at the forefront of my journey with each client and I hope that you feel that when I document these moments for you.
05 | What are the booking steps?
After submitting an inquiry, I like to set up a consult call, or take time to chat via email/text. If you decide to move forward in booking you'll receive a document with a contract and invoice. I want this process to be seamless for you, so please reach out if there's anything that I can do to modify this in a way that works best for you.
