PRICING
Here's a little information on my pricing. I offer 30, 60, 90 and 120 minute sessions, with half and full days available upon request as well.
I suggest choosing the type of shoot based on the time tolerance of your subjects (i.e. toddlers), your budget, number of locations you'd like to visit. Keep in mind we can walk a lot at one location (like Chautauqua for instance), and get a lot of variety!
And best of all, pricing includes beautifully edited, high-resolution, digital files! 
I can't wait to work with you...
