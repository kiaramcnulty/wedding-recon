BOULDER WEDDING PHOTOGRAPHER
My name is Eleanor, and I’m a Boulder, Colorado based wedding photographer. I am a quiet observer who does this job because I adore humans, and the magical moments I see between people, and capturing those connections literally gives me goose bumps every time.
A few words on how I work: I will not drag you away from your guests (if you have guests) for hours upon hours of photography, and I won't pose you in awkward positions, but rather I will gently pose you in a kind and empathetic way. I will prompt you to find even more love in each other's eyes, to see your partner in a way that maybe you hadn't before. Those are the reasons I do this job. Oh, and I also run cool as a cucumber, so getting worked up or stressed out is just not gonna happen on your wedding day. 
I’ll also help you keep your day low-stress by honoring your space, because photography should capture the experience, not dictate it. With experience, I've developed an intuition about when and where things will happen, so I won't miss a beat. 
Speaking of which, I want you to be organized, so if you book me, I'll send you planning worksheets and arrange a walk-through of your venue with you, if that is appropriate. I'll help you and your partner develop a timeline that works for you, and fits your style as a couple. I'll also bring a second photographer if you'd like one, and that's included in your cost. Your images will be delivered within 3-4 weeks, in a beautiful gallery which you can share. You can download images straight to your home computer or mobile device, or I can send you a customized USB drive. 
All wedding bookings include:
 * Vendor recommendations and consultation
 * Venue walkthrough and planning meeting with timeline assistance
 * Wedding planning worksheet and portrait list
 * Second photographer
 * Sneak peek blog post delivered in 2-3 weeks
 * Full gallery delivered in 3-4 weeks
 * High-resolution digital files, beautifully color-corrected and ready for downloading and printing. You can expect around 100-150 images for every hour that I shoot. 
I shoot weddings anywhere in the Boulder area, but some of my most frequented are St. Julien Hotel, Boulderado Hotel, Chautauqua Park, Flagstaff Mountain, Wedgewood Weddings Boulder Creek, Halfway House, Lionsgate, Sunrise Ampitheater, Riverbend, and Rembrandt Yard.
Meet Madison and Annah, my amazing second photographers! I always bring them for my own peace of mind, as well as yours. It makes the day run so much smoother, and you will have more moments to savor.
