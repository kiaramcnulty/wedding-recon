The Landing at Estes Park is home to the most picturesque wedding venue in the Estes Park valley. We offer stunning riverfront ceremony space & a charming reception hall.
 We are located just outside the main gate of Rocky Mountain National Park, and within minutes of the downtown, ample lodging options, and the most popular activities that Estes Park has to offer.
The Landing at Estes Park
 Unique & Memorable 
Event Venue
We host weekend and weekday weddings throughout the year and the property is exclusively yours when we do. This provides you and your favorite people a memorable and intimate experience in the beautiful mountains of Colorado, enjoying a fun and unique setting.
REHEARSAL DINNERS: We offer one of the few venues in the Estes Valley able to host larger welcome/rehearsal dinners! We offer an instantly cozy and inviting location for your guests as they arrive to Estes Park. Catering is accommodating and flexible. We host these dinners each day of the week.

About Our Venue Hall
Best Wedding
The Landing is reminiscent of a quaint, old world hall that expertly combines rustic romance with the same upscale finishes that The Landing is known for. 
 Within this structure we offer 3,000 square feet of incredible gathering space. Our event hall holds up to 100 guests and features an authentic barn wood bar, a catering kitchen, an enormous deck overlooking the river, large wooden beams with custom iron detailing, old world light fixtures, a cozy stone fireplace, lofty sliding glass doors, and panoramic windows that frame stunning views of Longs Peak and the sparkling river below. 
 We hold only one wedding at a time and no unaffiliated guests are onsite during your event.  
If you are seeking a lovely environment and the opportunity to make lasting memories amidst breathtaking scenery, The Landing at Estes Park is your place. Should you wish to learn more, would like to inquire about availability, or to schedule a tour please reach out!
