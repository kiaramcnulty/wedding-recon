I’ve been on both sides of the lens. I’ve been the frazzled mother of 4 under five, trying to find a patient and practiced photographer who could capture the unique essence of our family. Now, I get to be behind the lens, bettering this industry and client experience for you + your family.
Today, I’m mom to the coolest high schoolers + middle-schoolers. While I love celebrating life as it is with them now, I also cherish the memories around my home of our family's entire journey and story because life is changing fast… 
When you’re a parent faced with change + this peculiar, delicious mixture of grief and pride of your kids growing up, having the cherished memories of your life together surrounding you brings such comfort. 
I’m lucky enough to witness the families I photograph change and grow just as I see them with my own. But the magic of photography is that it freezes each special stage and moment.
