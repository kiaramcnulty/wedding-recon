Why Choose Siroky Photography for Your Engagement Session
Customized Experience: We tailor each session to your vision, style, and comfort level.
Local Expertise: We know Colorado Springs inside and out—from hidden trails to iconic landmarks.
Storytelling Approach: Our goal is to capture authentic moments that reflect your relationship.
Select the Perfect Location
Colorado Springs offers a diverse range of backdrops. Consider these top spots:
Location Highlights 
 Garden of the Gods Dramatic red rock formations and wide-open skies. 
 Old Colorado City Historic streets, brick walls, and cozy cafés. 
 Pikes Peak Panoramic mountain vistas above the clouds. 
 Palmer Park Rolling hills, wildflowers, and city skyline views. 
 Helen Hunt Falls Lush forest setting with a cascading waterfall. 
 Monument Valley Park Grassy fields, mature trees, and river views. 
Styling Tips for Your Shoot
Coordinate, Don’t Match
Complementary Colors: Choose outfits in coordinating hues—think soft neutrals or jewel tones against red rock.
Layer Up: Light jackets, scarves, and hats add texture and let you adapt to changing mountain weather.
Timeless Pieces: Avoid overly trendy items; classic styles photograph beautifully and stay relevant.
Footwear & Accessories
Dress for both city streets and rugged trails—stylish boots, simple jewelry, and minimal branding logos.
Time Your Session for the Best Light
Overcast Days: Clouds diffuse light, reducing harsh shadows for even skin tones.
Golden Hour: Schedule 30–60 minutes after sunrise or before sunset.
Blue Hour: The soft twilight period adds a dreamy, pastel-toned backdrop.
Master Natural and Flattering Poses
Forget stiff, forced poses. Aim for candid interactions:
Walking Hand in Hand: Capture movement and genuine smiles.
Forehead Touch: A classic, intimate gesture that centers focus on your connection.
Seated Close-Up: Sit on a rock or bench, facing each other to highlight expressions.
Playful Moments: Laugh together, spin around, or share a gentle dip.
Pro Tip: Bring a small Bluetooth speaker to play songs that make you both smile and relax.
Incorporate Personal Elements
Customize your session with items that reflect your story:
Hobbies & Passions: Hiking gear, a vintage camera, or your pet (where allowed).
Seasonal Props: Blankets and mugs of hot cocoa in winter; picnic baskets in summer.
Meaningful Locations: The coffee shop where you first met or a favorite mountain overlook.
Communication & Comfort Are Key
Pre-Session Consultation: Discuss locations, outfits, and must-have shots.
On-Location Guidance: We’ll coach poses and offer gentle direction to keep you at ease.
Breaks & Flexibility: We build in time for rest, wardrobe changes, and spontaneous ideas.
After Your Session: Choosing & Showcasing Your Photos
Image Selection: We’ll review a proof gallery together to pick your favorites.
Editing Style: Choose from vibrant color, soft matte, or classic black & white.
Sharing & Printing: Receive high-resolution digital files plus options for prints, albums, and wall art.
