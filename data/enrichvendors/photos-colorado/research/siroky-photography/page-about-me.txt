About Siroky Photography, Your Personal Colorado Springs Photographer
Jerry Siroky
Professional Photographer
Jerry Siroky is emerging as the premier photorapher in Colorado Springs.
Philosophy
I am dedicated to capturing every moment of your life’s journey through photography. With niche photography gaining popularity, I want to emphasize that photography is my specialty.
We can begin with our First Year Collection, which includes Maternity, Newborn, and Cake Smash sessions. Through our services, photography, and personal connection, we will build a lifelong relationship. As we continue, we can document Family moments, High School Senior portraits, Boudoir sessions, and your Engagement.
After capturing your Wedding Day, often considered the most unforgettable day of one’s life, and the announcement of your pregnancy, we can start the cycle anew with the birth of a new life.
MY BEGINNING
I studied photography at Adams State College in Alamosa, while also pursuing degrees in English Journalism and Business Advertising. It wasn’t until the last summer of college, while working for local photographer Gary Atencio, that my lifelong passion for photographing people truly began.
After graduating and moving back to Colorado Springs, I immediately opened my own portrait studio, focusing on weddings, families, high school seniors, and children. Following ten years as a portrait photographer and a brief move to New York City, fashion photography became an important part of my work. This fashion influence can now be seen in various aspects of our current portrait work.
