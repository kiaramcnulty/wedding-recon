Meet Emily,
Your Colorado Elopement Expert 
WHATTUP! It’s ya girl, Emily! Here’s some things you should know about me: 
 I’m fiercely passionate about making my couples wedding experience full of emotion, intimate moments, and more than anything, JOY ! 
I am a huge believer in couples choosing a wedding experience that THEY want. People can have a lot of opinions about your wedding or elopement day, and I’m here to encourage couples to not feel bullied into planning a wedding day they won’t enjoy, but rather focusing on a day that TRULY speaks to them. 
I’m here to be your resource on all things wedding and elopement planning. I’ll guide you through the planning process, and will act as an open book to you and your boo with all things planning. 
If you're looking for a bestie in your photographer, I’m your girl ! I’m all about the experience you get along with your beautiful photos, and that includes gaining a pal!

On our wedding day, we made it exactly what we wanted. I could not have possibly imagined a more perfect elopement day, and I’m here to tell you that you deserve this too.
