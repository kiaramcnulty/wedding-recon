Colorado Stop-Motion Films 
Why stop-motion?
Stop-motion is a stylized approach to capturing moments. They’re very reminiscent of the vintage home movies your grandparents took, and the finished product looks like something that could have been caught on Super-8. As someone who practically lives at antique and thrift stores, I get to combine my love of capturing moments in a way that feels nostalgic, while also providing a cool new way to watch your wedding day back.
Stop-motion films are a stylized way of capturing movement through photography! Basically, I take photos super fast, then later stitch them together to bring the movement to life. These are edited in 2:3 vertical without audio, so they’re perfectly sized for social media! 
What is stop-motion?
So you want Stop-Motion magic… 
1. Book me
 Reach out to me to book your date! Once we chat to make sure we’re a good fit, we’ll talk about adding on a stop-motion film to your day. Stop-motion films are included in my highest package for wedding and elopement days, but are available for add on for all other packages.
2. Plan it all out
 During our planning calls leading up to your day, we’ll discuss what you’re hoping to get out of your film. We’ll chat vibes, ideas, mood boards, ALL of it. I’ll come prepared day of with a list of shots I’d like to get to make your film amazing. Most of your film will tell the story of the day, but there’s room for us to play a bit as well, and have some fun!
3. Get your film
 After your day, I’ll hit the editing room and create your stop-motion film. You’ll receive your photos first, but your film will be close behind! Then, you’ll have a gorgeous stop-motion film to share on social media with your family and friends, as well as watch everyday forever!
