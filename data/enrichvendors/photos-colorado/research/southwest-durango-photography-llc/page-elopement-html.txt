Colorado elopement photography is my absolute favorite type of wedding photography. I have been exploring and adventuring in the Southwest for over 20 years and would love to take you to one of my favorite spots, depending on your constraints, whether that is on top of a mountain or directly off of the side of a National Forest road. My Colorado elopement packages start at $2400 for small elopements in my area that require less than 4 hours of photography and less than a 3 hour drive. If you are looking for a Durango elopement photography session I can recommend several amazing spots depending on how far off of the beaten path you would like to adventure (the above photo location is a five minute walk from a two-wheel drive road about 20 minutes from Durango, Colorado). I would be honored to help you plan your ideal elopement! If you would like to discuss your perfect day with me please don't hesitate to reach out. I would love to be your adventure photographer, Colorado elopement photographer, and consultant!
