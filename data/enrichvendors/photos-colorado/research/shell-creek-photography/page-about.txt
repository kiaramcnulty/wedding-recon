Meet Malachi: A Fusion of Nature, Photography, & Adventure
My name is Malachi Lewis, & I&#8217;m an adventurous wedding & elopement photographer traveling across the western U.S. with my husband, Kaleb. I have a profound love for travel, birds, dogs, & anything adventurous that draws me outdoors—especially when I can share these experiences with others!
Having grown up surrounded by nature, I find constant inspiration in the natural world. My photography style is deeply influenced by this connection. I adore the warm, bright light of the sun & the vibrant colors of birds & flowers. This reverence for nature is why my photographic style is natural & colorful, aiming to capture the stunning beauty that surrounds us while also providing you with photos that are full of joy & color!
Nature has always been a pivotal part of my life. Currently, I share my space with my dog, Bunji, a parrot named Petri, & a variety of plants, all of which add to the richness of my everyday inspirations.
FINDING YOUR IDEAL PHOTOGRAPHER 
Looking for a photographer who blends into the background yet captures every moment with clarity & beauty? I might just be the one you&#8217;re searching for! Here, ALL LOVE is celebrated—whatever your sexuality, religion, or beliefs. Come as you are, & let’s create a wedding day that reflects who you truly are!

Having a More Sustainable Wedding
Want another reason to elope or have a smaller wedding? Larger, more traditional weddings can produce a lot of waste. Each wedding usually produces at least 400 pounds of trash! When you elope or have a more intimate wedding day, you don’t spend a bunch of money on things you’ll later throw away, you’ll invest in a truly just for you experience. And if you’re like me, you value experiences over things & want to elope because it just sounds like an amazing start to the new adventure of life that you are starting together.
