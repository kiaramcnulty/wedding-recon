WELCOME, I’M TOM
 My wedding photography approach is about 70% documentary — real, unscripted moments — paired with 20% timeless family and group photos, and a 10% dose of adventurous , Colorado-inspired imagery.
 With over 20 years of experience, I’m comfortable in anything from perfect golden-hour light to fast-changing mountain weather. When conditions shift, I adapt quickly and calmly, so you never have to worry.
 I specialize in using light intentionally — whether that’s natural light or carefully placed off-camera lighting — to create images that feel both natural and cinematic, no matter the environment.
 On your wedding day, I show up with a calm presence, a problem-solving mindset, and genuine curiosity about you and the people around you. That trust allows me to capture moments as they actually happen — not forced, not staged.
 You’ll see in my portfolio a wide range of weddings — large and small, formal and informal, indoors and outdoors — because every celebration deserves to be documented with the same level of care.
 And unlike most photographers, you won’t be waiting weeks to relive it — your fully edited, ready-to-print images are delivered within 72 hours in a private online gallery, along with full personal-use printing rights.
