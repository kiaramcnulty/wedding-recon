FAQ.
How would you describe your photography style? 
My photography style is bold, vibrant, and timeless. It is not overly posed as I am always making sure I capture not only posed photos but also candid moments.
How do you edit the pictures? 
I am meticulous in my work, ensuring that your photos are enhanced to their fullest aesthetic potential. Whether it’s addressing stains, editing out imperfections such as frizzy hair, or eliminating any elements that are not permanent and should not be present in the picture, I am dedicated to delivering a flawless final result.
Do you offer payment plans? 
Absolutely I do! I always offer a flexible payment plan especially for weddings.
When should I book you? 
For timely scheduling, I recommend booking as soon as possible. Specifically, for weddings, I advise securing a date at least 6 months in advance. For elopements and engagement sessions, scheduling can be a bit more flexible, particularly if weekdays are convenient for you. It’s important to note that dates are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis, so prompt booking is advisable to secure your preferred slot.
Where do you travel? 
All over Western Colorado for engagements & couples sessions, and all over the state + Moab for weddings & elopements!
