Western Colorado Weddings
From intimate elopements at Blue Lakes to grand celebrations beneath Colorado’s towering mountains, I capture the authentic moments that make your wedding day uniquely yours. The dramatic landscapes of our region become part of your celebration’s story, whether you’re exchanging vows in a historic ghost town, dancing under mountain stars, or celebrating in one of our charming local venues.
As a photographer who calls these mountains home, I know the hidden spots, the perfect timing for alpenglow portraits, and how to work with our region’s dynamic weather. From Montrose’s vibrant venues to Ouray’s epic canyon walls , I blend artistic vision with local expertise to create wedding photos that honor both the magnificent mountain scenery and the intimate moments of your day. Your wedding photographs should be as extraordinary as the location you’ve chosen to celebrate your love.

FAQ.
When Should We Book Our Wedding Photographer? 
For Colorado weddings, I recommend booking 12-18 months in advance, especially for peak summer and fall dates. Winter weddings typically book 8-12 months ahead.
Do You Travel Between Telluride, Montrose, and Other Mountain Venues? 
Absolutely! I regularly photograph weddings throughout Western Colorado’s mountain venues. Travel fees are included for locations within the San Juan Mountain region.
Do You Know the Best Photo Locations? 
After years of photographing in these mountains, I have an extensive list of stunning locations – from iconic spots to hidden alpine meadows. I’ll help you choose locations that match your vision and accessibility needs.
What's Your Photography Style? 
I blend artistic candid moments with naturally posed portraits, always working to incorporate the vibrant magnificent mountain scenery.
Do You Work With Other Vendors? 
I have strong relationships with the area’s top wedding planners, venues, and vendors. This collaborative approach helps ensure smooth timeline coordination and the best possible photo opportunities.
How Many Images Will We Receive? 
Full-day weddings typically receive 600-800 carefully curated, fully edited images. You’ll get a sneak peek within 24 hours and your complete gallery within 6-8 weeks.
What About Winter Weddings? 
I love winter weddings! I’m experienced with snow photography and know how to work with winter light and conditions. We’ll plan timing around the shorter daylight hours to capture both ceremony and portraits beautifully.
Do You Hold Our Date With A Retainer? 
Yes, a 50% retainer and signed contract secures your date, with the balance due 30 days before the wedding. All packages include pre-wedding consultation and timeline planning.
