Hi, I&#8217;m Julien.
a Colorado Photgrapher
When I first moved from France to the United States, I thought I knew exactly what my American Dream would look like. Armed with my French college degree (which I soon learned wasn&#8217;t recognized here) and determination, I dove into corporate America as a Business Analyst. On paper, everything looked perfect—but something felt missing.
Then I got my first camera and that simple purchase changed everything.
What started as a way to capture the stunning Colorado landscapes around me quickly became my passion, then my purpose. I discovered that my American Dream wasn&#8217;t about climbing the corporate ladder—it was about capturing the real, authentic moments that make life beautiful.
Today, I&#8217;ve transformed that passion into an art form. After years of dedicated practice, countless workshops, and endless hours perfecting my craft, I&#8217;m now not only living my dream as a photographer but also teaching others how to capture their own vision through photography. It&#8217;s funny how life leads you exactly where you&#8217;re meant to be, even when you start on a completely different path.
