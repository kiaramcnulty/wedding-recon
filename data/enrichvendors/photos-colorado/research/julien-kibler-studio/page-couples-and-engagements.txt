FAQ.
How long will our engagement session last? 
Each session is a relaxed 1-hour experience, giving us plenty of time to capture both posed and candid moments at your chosen location.
What's the best time of day for photos? 
I typically schedule sessions during “golden hour” – the hour before sunset – when the San Juans are bathed in that magical mountain glow. However, sunrise sessions can be equally stunning and often offer more privacy at popular locations.
How do we choose a location? 
Unless you have a specific place in mind, after booking, I’ll share my premium location guide featuring hidden gems across Telluride and Western Colorado. We’ll choose a spot that matches your vision – whether that’s epic mountain views, intimate aspen groves, or historic town settings.
When will we receive our photos? 
You’ll have access to your full online gallery within 2-3 weeks of your session. Your gallery includes unlimited downloads of all your best edited images.
What should we wear? 
I provide all my couples with a detailed style guide to help coordinate outfits that complement both each other and our mountain settings. The key is choosing comfortable pieces that make you feel confident!
What if the weather doesn't cooperate? 
Mountain weather can be unpredictable, but the unpredictability can make for some of the best photos! Don’t worry though, I keep a close eye on forecasts and if conditions simply aren’t safe or ideal, we can easily reschedule your session.
Can we bring our dog? 
Absolutely! Your furry family members are always welcome. I recommend bringing them for part of the session, with a friend nearby who can take them when we move to different locations or focus on couple-only shots.
Do you help with poses and direction? 
Yes! I guide you through natural poses and prompts that bring out genuine interactions and laughter. Even couples who say they’re camera-shy end up having fun and feeling comfortable.
