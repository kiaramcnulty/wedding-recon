Our Services 
Wedding Concierge and Planning Service
 What does 35 years experience as wedding planners do for your day? From start to finish, you will work directly with not only the owners but your day-of service team to create the perfect package, find the perfect location, and create the details of your wedding that will create a lifetime of memories for you and your guests. 
Officiating
 Every ceremony is custom written for each couple. Between completing our questionnaire and telephone or in-person consultations, we will create a ceremony that is perfectly suited to the two of you and your lives together. 
More About Officiating Services 
Bridal Flowers
 As 3rd generation LOCAL bridal designers with over 30 years experience, we can create any style from sleek and traditional to a mountain wildflower masterpiece, a theme arrangement for your unique style or a glamorous look for a formal wedding. 
More About Bridal Flowers 
Ceremony and Reception Decor
From simple aspen arches to elaborate arches and full ceremony aisle and altar sets to full reception decor that will dazzle your guests. 
More About Decor 
Photography
 A photo is worth 1000 words, and your wedding photos are worth a lifetime of memories. Our services are based on hourly rates so you can find the perfect fit for your budget. Copyright free photos allow you unlimited options for printing your own images, or use our professional lab for canvases and albums. 
More About Photography Services 
Ceremony Music
 Whether it is just one song to come down the aisle, or a romantic first dance celebrated in nature, our portable sound systems ensure that every detail is available to you. 
Wedding Cakes
 Our local bakery is the perfect ending to a sweet day with a custom made cake for 2 or for 200!
