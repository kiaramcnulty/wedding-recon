Eloping or Intimate Destination Weddings in estes park 
 Colorado Rocky Mountain National Park Estes Park Elopements and MicroWedding
 Why Estes Park for your elopement? We are ranked #1 in Colorado, #7 in the United States, and one of the top 20 locations world wide for elopements and destination weddings. Well known for our multitude of "Fourteeners" - mountain peaks topping 14,000 feet above sea level, we boast not only endless sites of majestic mountain backdrops but sparkling lakes, rivers and streams and lush meadows with wild flowers that provide you with an romantic experience and picture perfect backgrounds. Our elopement and intimate wedding packages take care of the stress of long distance planning by providing you with all of the essentials - from ceremony officiating to photography , flowers , cakes and champagne, and special amenities such as unique unity and blended family ceremonies, flower petal strewn aisle, decorated arch, and even fur trimmed capes for those winter weddings. Top off your package with a customized photo keepsake certificate overlaid with your own wedding vows to hang on your wall afterwards. Started in 2005, we are the Original Estes Park's finest Elopement and Micro-Wedding Company in Colorado! 
Treat your guests to an unforgettable wedding experience topped off with hiking, biking, nature tours, rafting, fly or lake fishing, boating, picnicking, camping or just relaxing in a stream side hot tub or poolside watching grazing elk, deer, big horn sheep and a multitude of other wild life. 
With over 35 years in the wedding business and and being native to Estes Park, we offer assistance finding the perfect location that fits your personality, style and budget whether a lodge, upscale resort, a historical hotel, private mountain cabin or a secluded spot in one of the most beautiful spots on earth!
