Hi, I'm Sean! 
I’m Sean, a photographer based in Colorado specializing in wedding photography, engagement photography, senior portraits and family portraits. My primary service areas for wedding photos are in Fort Collins, Estes Park and Denver but I frequently travel all over the state and throughout the country.
 With a passion for capturing love stories, I’ve been a full time wedding photographer since 2010, and was recently voted by a panel of judges with the Wedding Photojournalist Association as one of the top 5 wedding photographers in the world.
 I take an editorial, photojournalist approach focusing on capturing real moments and emotions while putting an artistic edge on your images.
 As your photographer for any Colorado wedding or other event, I pride myself with providing you the quality service you deserve in a fun and professional manner.
