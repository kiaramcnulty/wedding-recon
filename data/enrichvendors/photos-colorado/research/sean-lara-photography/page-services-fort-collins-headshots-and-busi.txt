Business Headshots & Portraits
I offer business portraits and headshots in Fort Collins and all of Northern Colorado for multiple types of professionals.
 Whether its personal branding photography, business headshots, lifestyle portraits and or professional portraits, we got you covered. I’m an “on-location” photographer, meaning I have a mobile studio and I come to you.
 For most businesses and employees, our two most highly requested services are  Traditional Headshots  and  Branding Sessions,  but I can certainly customize a package if you need something more specific.

Pricing & Options for Business Headshots
Business Portrait Session & Headshots
To keep things streamlined and intentional, headshots are offered at a flat investment of $850 . This session is designed for business owners, creatives, and professionals who want more than a quick headshot &#8211; portraits that feel polished, confident, and aligned with your brand.
Your session includes: 
Up to 45 minutes of photography 
Intentional, guided portraiture with posing and expression coaching
A professional lighting setup tailored to your space
Retouched, high-resolution images ready for web, social, and print use
Usage rights for personal branding and business marketing
Travel included throughout Northern Colorado
This service is ideal for individual professionals or small teams looking for elevated, consistent imagery without the need for a full studio environment.
If you’re working with a larger team , need multiple looks or setups , or require something more custom, please get in touch for a tailored quote.
