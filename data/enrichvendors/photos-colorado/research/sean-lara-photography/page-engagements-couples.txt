Engagement Sessions
Engagement sessions are often your first family photo experience together. They’re an intentional opportunity for us to connect, helping you feel relaxed and confident in front of the camera while creating imagery in extraordinary locations that are often unavailable on the wedding day itself. I photograph engagement sessions throughout Colorado and regularly travel to destinations around the world.
