Partial Wedding Planning
 WITH WEEKEND COORDINATION
Package Inclusions
 This package is designed for the couple who would like to take on some of the wedding planning themselves, with the option to have Veils of Vail book and arrange the remaining vendors needed for their wedding weekend. Our partial planning services are perfect for the destination couple that need a little professional guidance throughout the wedding planning process to ensure everything is on track, and our services in finalizing all of the important details and logistics. We will make sure that everything is in place over the months and weeks leading up to your wedding weekend. Below is a list of the inclusions Veils of Vail provides with our partial planning package, this package can be customized based on your wedding planning needs and budget. 
 Veils of Vail’s Partial Planning Package ensures that our couples can confidently plan parts of their wedding on their own with ease, and have our professional expertise during the entire wedding planning process, in addition to our onsite coordination. We will perfectly direct and execute the timeline of events during your wedding weekend!
