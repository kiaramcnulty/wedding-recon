Package Inclusions
 This package is designed for the couple who plan on taking on the wedding planning themselves, booking their creative partners and vendors, and arranging all of the details for their wedding weekend all while having Veils of Vail’s professional guidance and assistance keeping everything right on track throughout the planning process. 
 This package is more than just “Month of”, below is a list of all the inclusions Veils of Vail provides so that you and your fiance have all the tools to confidently plan your wedding with ease, and our professional expertise of onsite coordination on your rehearsal and wedding day directing and overseeing your vendor team, set up, and executing the timeline and flow of events. Veils of Vail wants you to be able to enjoy every moment of your weekend you’ve worked so hard to plan.
