I currently live in Durango, Colorado but I was born and raised in México. I started my career as a wedding photographer 15 years ago in Puerto Vallarta, Jalisco. I fell in love with photography because of the way it makes me feel. I still remember my grandfather showing me pictures of his wedding, and how some of the photos felt like they had life inside of them. It gave me purpose. I aim to capture emotions before everything else. I want you to go back and look at your wedding photos and experience reliving those same feelings, and a photograph has the beautiful power to take you back.
