BEHIND THE CAMERA WITH
ABIE LIVESAY
For almost 20 years, Abie Livesay has been quietly and artfully documenting weddings with a keen eye for the real, unspoken moments—the fleeting glances, deep belly laughs, and quiet hand-squeezes that say more than words ever could. With a documentary-style approach, she captures weddings as they truly unfold, without staging or spectacle, creating images that feel intimate, effortless, and deeply personal.
Known for a calm presence and a quick wit, Abie brings a playful energy that puts people at ease, allowing connections to unfold naturally. Whether it’s an emotional exchange between generations or the unfiltered joy of a packed dance floor, the goal is always the same: to tell a story that feels honest, unforced, and timeless.
For Abie, wedding photography isn’t about trends or theatrics—it’s about legacy. It’s about preserving the relationships, the connection, and the quiet magic that make a wedding day unforgettable. Because in the end, the best photographs aren’t just seen; they’re felt. They only become more valuable with time.
