Meet Your Photographer
Hi, I’m Bonnie! 
Raised in Boulder, CO, I spent most of my childhood climbing trees, crushing on Aragorn, pretending to be an X-Wing pilot (X-Wing Fighter on Windows 98!!!), and being outdoors with my Girl Scout troop. If you’ve ever felt like the “weird ones,” the introverts, the rule-breakers, good news, you’ve found your photographer. I have deep empathy for folks doing things their own way, because I’ve always done the same. 
From our first meeting with Bonnie, to our engagement session, through wedding planning, and finally our big day, she was absolutely amazing to work with. 
-Lucy D 
I’m here for you. For your day. Your joy.
You want someone who’s been around the block and still gets excited about every couple’s day like it’s their first. That’s me. I officially launched Bonnie Photo in 2017, got married myself in 2019 and never looked back. My own wedding only confirmed what I already knew: these photos matter . They’re the memories you’ll show your kids and friends. The snapshots of people you love who may not always be around. They’re legacy stuff.
So, whether your venue has a secret bookshelf door in a cabin, a haunted theater in the mountains, or your grandma’s backyard, if you&#8217;re looking for a photographer who’ll get pumped about your nerdy, weird, beautiful love story? Let’s do this.
Photography: my passion, my art
Why photography?  
I’ve always had a passion for visual imagery. From painting classes as a kid, to studying Soviet Cinema for my Master’s Degree from the University of East Anglia in England, it all led to the epiphany of taking photos with a fancy point and shoot camera while traveling in Iceland. Exploring, getting all those settings exactly right. I knew I wanted more. 
Cool, but why wedding photography ? 
Because when I photographed my first couple, Abi and Lizzy, just goofing around RiNo, being cute AF and in love, I realized this is it. This is the good stuff. Capturing your love, your weirdness, your epicness? Freaking addictive. I absolutely thrive on getting to meet all kinds of people from all different backgrounds and making lasting connections. 
My true passion?
Weddings with meteorologists, D&D nerds, Mario Kart experts, Hobbit want-to-bes&#8230; where everyone’s being their true selves and the love is palpable. Small and intimate or huge dance parties, when the people around you are celebrating the hell out of your relationship and, I get to bottle it all up in photos? 🔥🔥🔥
