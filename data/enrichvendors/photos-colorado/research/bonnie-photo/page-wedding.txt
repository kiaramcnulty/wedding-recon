A Wedding Photographer You Can Have Fun With!
Hi! I&#8217;m Bonnie, an LGBTQ+, neurodiverse, and diversity-affirming professional photographer. As a wedding photographer, I love to work with couples of all different backgrounds and beliefs. I vibe best with couples who&#8217;d rather skip the awkward poses and staged Pinterest trends, and instead focus on enjoying their day exactly how they want. About Bonnie
Meet Your Photographer
Hey, I’m Bonnie. I’ve been photographing weddings since 2017, and I still can&#8217;t help but smile seeing people beam at each other over cupcakes or sharing jokes during vows. I don’t think your wedding should feel like a performance: it should feel like you .
I’m a proud Colorado born-and-raised nerd (ask me about my Lord of the Rings-themed wedding), human and dog mom, and all-around snack enthusiast. I’ve photographed everything from backyard cookouts to massive, diverse dance parties, and I approach each one with the same calm, non-judgmental energy.
My “why”? Because I believe your wedding photos should help you remember how dang fun, weird, sweet, or hilarious your day really was, without forcing you into someone else’s idea of what a wedding “should” be.
