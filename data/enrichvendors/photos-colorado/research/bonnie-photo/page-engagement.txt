All The Deets
How to Book Engagement Photography with Bonnie
Let&#8217;s Chat
Once you’re in, we’ll hop on a quick call to plan the perfect location for your session.
Booking
Choose your package, sign the contract, and handle payment: all through a secure online portal. Easy peasy!
Style Guide
I’ll send over my exclusive style guide to help you nail your look for the session!
Your Session
I’ll send you the meetup details and be there early to set up. During your session, I’ll guide you through natural poses, making it fun, easy, and totally you !
Proofing
About a week after your session, you’ll get a proofing gallery to pick your favorites. I’ll then hand-edit each one to perfection over the next two weeks!
Photo Delivery
Your final, hand-edited photos will be delivered in a beautiful online gallery: easy to access, share, and order prints from. I’ll also send your yearbook photo where it needs to go!
