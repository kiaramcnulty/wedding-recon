For couples who care more about the moment than the pose
Hi, I’m Abbi, a wedding and elopement photographer based in Durango, Colorado.
 I’m here for the real stuff; the moments you actually feel when you’re fully in it together. Not overly posed versions of your day, not anything forced or scripted. Just honest, connection as it naturally unfolds.
 You don’t need to know how to pose or worry about doing things “right.” I’ll guide you when it helps, but mostly I’m stepping back and letting your day breathe, so you can actually experience it while I document it.
 My approach is simple: keep things natural, keep things easy, and create space for real moments to happen. 
 I care a lot about honest connection, good energy, and creating an experience that feels easy from start to finish. Whether you’re out in the mountains or somewhere that simply feels like home, I’m there to document it in a way that’s true to you.
