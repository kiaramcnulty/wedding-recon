Candid storytelling for couples who
 feel most at home in nature
I’m here for the real stuff; the in-between, the unplanned, the moments that feel like you. There’s no pressure to perform, no need to be anything other than what you already are. We’ll keep it easy, maybe a little wild, and let the magic happen naturally.
About Me
You’re probably not looking for someone to overly pose you or turn your wedding into a photoshoot. You want to actually be in it, laughing, moving, taking it all in, and trust that it’s being captured as it unfolds.
 That’s where I come in.
 I’m the kind of photographer who gives you space to be yourselves, while knowing exactly when to step in and guide things when it matters. Nothing forced, nothing overdone, just a natural, easy experience that lets your day feel like your day.
 I’m a free-spirited, adventure-seeking gal who will never turn down a good time. After moving to Colorado in 2012, I quickly fell in love with this part of the country. It’s a big part of why I connect so deeply with couples who feel most at home outside.
 I’m based in Durango, where you’ll usually find me mountain biking, hiking, exploring new places, checking out breweries, or planning my next trip. That love for adventure naturally finds its way into how I approach weddings. 
 I fell in love with photography shooting film in college, and it never really let go. What started as curiosity turned into something much bigger, getting to document real connection, real emotion, and all the in-between moments that make your story yours.
 If you’re looking for someone who will meet you where you’re at, whether that’s a laid-back day in the mountains or something a little more wild, I’m all in. I’ll be there to support you, guide you when needed, and capture it all in a way that actually feels like you.

Q & A 
What does it actually feel like to be photographed by you?
It’s easy, relaxed, and doesn’t feel like a photoshoot.
 You don’t need to know what to do or worry about being “good” in front of the camera. I’ll guide you when it’s helpful, but for the most part, I’m giving you space to actually be in it. Laughing, moving, taking it all in while I document it as it naturally unfolds.
Do you pose us at all, or is everything candid?
Think of it more like gentle guidance rather than posing. I’ll set you up in a way that feels natural, then let the real moments take over.
We’re awkward in front of the camera, will you help us?
Absolutely and you’re not alone in that.
 Most couples feel this way going in. You don’t need to perform or have anything figured out. I’ll give you just enough direction to get you out of your head, and then let things happen naturally from there.
