Why have a wedding? And why hire a film photographer to document it? 
Because family means everything to you. Because you’ve gathered friends from all circles of your life. Because you want to be surrounded by a sea of everyone you love. Because every person on that guest list played a part in your journey to each other.

 whatever that Looks like, 
 whatever that Feels like.
No matter which traditions you're sticking with or which trends you're dodging, I'm here to support you with seasoned experience, calm presence, and an artist’s way of seeing things.
