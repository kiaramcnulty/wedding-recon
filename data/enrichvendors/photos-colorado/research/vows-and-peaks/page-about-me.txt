About Sean Oblizalo
This is my office, seriously.
Some people hike for fun. I do it for work.
I carry my camera to places most people need a rescue for. I started my career as a landscape photographer, working with large adventure brands to bring visuals of epic mountain scenes to their advertising. I fell in love with landscapes first, and then I fell in love with helping couples plan their perfect day. 
I’m with you on this wild adventure from narrowing down locations, guiding your day with a customized timeline (while always following LNT Ethics), and tackling logistics. I won’t just be an adventure buddy, but a full-blown ninja for everything you need!
